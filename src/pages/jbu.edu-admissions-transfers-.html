Schedule my visit Admissions requirements for transferring If you've earned more than 24 college credits, you need a 2.5 college GPA to be accepted. If you've earned less than 24 credits, you'll be admitted based on our typical requirements of a 2.5 high school GPA with a 20 ACT, 1020 SAT or 66 CLT score. Either way, follow the transfer admissions checklist or get started on your application.
