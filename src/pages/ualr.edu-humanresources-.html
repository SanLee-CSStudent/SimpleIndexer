Services and resources: Benefits Training and Development Employment Guidance Tuition Discounts Employee Relations Performance Review Management Development, Implementation, and Interpretation of Policy Affirmative Action Statement The University of Arkansas at Little Rock is an equal opportunity, affirmative action employer and actively seeks the candidacy of minorities, women, veterans, and persons with disabilities. The university\u2019s Affirmative Action Plan sets forth specific result-oriented procedures to which the university commits itself in order to overcome such institutional barriers to equal employment opportunity. It also establishes an organizational structure for developing future goals and procedures and for assuring that all efforts are made to meet these goals on an ongoing basis. Under Arkansas law, all applications are subject to disclosure. Persons hired must have proof of legal authority to work in the United States.
