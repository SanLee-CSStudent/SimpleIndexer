Alumni RAWC Memberships The UAFS Alumni Office and Recreation and Wellness Center (RAWC) would like to extend membership opportunities to Alumni of the institution to utilize our Recreation and Wellness Center. Memberships are limited to the first 100 alumni to sign up, and access will be given from January 10- July 3rd 2022. Yearly membership (Jan 10-July 3rd and July 5th to Dec 22nd) is available at a discounted rate of $250. Registration will end on February 1st, 2022.
