About The Program The Office Management Technical Certificate prepares students for careers in clerical, general secretarial and administrative assistant careers. Students will learn to operate computers and other office machines, become proficient with common business software and develop good work and organizational habits. This program emphasizes the development of personal attitudes and traits that enable students to work harmoniously with employers and co-workers.
