Field of Interest The computer science industry is a fast-paced, constantly evolving environment where creative problem-solving and attention to detail are as important as the computer training you receive \u2014 but to get your foot in the door of this exciting career, you'll need the essential computer training first. Start today and join the Computer Science program! This program will teach you how to write in a variety of programming languages and computing environments. You will also learn mathematical foundations, algorithmic principles, and computer science theory \u2014 necessary skills for building complex software systems. We'll teach you how to analyze a problem and use critical thinking to identify the computing requirements needed to solve the issue. This is more than a computer course \u2014 this valuable computer training program is your key to an exciting career. The knowledge and skills you take away from the Computer Science program will lay a solid foundation down for a fruitful career. What are you waiting for? Make it happen today!
