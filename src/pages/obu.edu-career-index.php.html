Career Fair Ouachita's Career Fair will be Tuesday, March 8 from 1-4 p.m. The fair is a great event to share internships and employment opportunities for students following graduation. Along with tangible job opportunities, we also want to give recruiters and students the chance to network and create connections that lead to conversations about current and future career and internship opportunities. We are always excited to bring a diverse number of employers to campus so that we can connect our students with outstanding opportunities. REGISTER Graduate & Professional School Fair Thank you to all who attended Ouachita's 2021 Graduate & Professional School Fair. The Graduate School Fair is used to introduce students to numerous academic and professional programs. Recruiters are available to talk with students about acceptance criteria, tuition, program specifics as well as what their alumni have gone on to accomplish in their careers.
