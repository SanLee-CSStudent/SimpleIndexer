The Blazer Express Blue Route and Green Route both stop across University Boulevard from the Campus Recreation Center. The Green Route also has a stop by the 9th Avenue Parking Deck, next to the facility. There are also bike racks located on the northeast and northwest corners of the Campus Recreation Center, along with bike lanes on 10th Avenue South and 16th Street South, leading to the facility. All UAB University Recreation Members and Guests have the option to park in the Public Parking Lot 5A located across University Boulevard from the UAB Campus Recreation Center. Unfortunately, all costs associated with parking at the Public Parking Lot 5A will be the responsibility of the member or the guest to pay when exiting the parking lot. All employees who park in UAB facilities at the established full-time parking lot, may be granted NIGHTS and WEEKEND parking privileges to the 9th Avenue parking deck on their UAB One Card by the UAB Parking and Transportation department. Campus Recreation Center Location UAB Campus Recreation is located on University Boulevard just steps from most major UAB buildings; making it easy for you to workout every day. It is on the corner of University Boulevard and 15th Street South.
