Leadership Opportunities Events Student Discounts Life at UA Little Rock The University of Arkansas at Little Rock is a metropolitan research university in the heart of Arkansas. We are proud to offer a dynamic learning environment where students can immerse themselves in the college experience and take advantage of opportunities both inside the classroom and out. The Trojan community is a diverse group with nearly 10,000 students from all over the world and from all walks of life. The Student Experience Center is focused on creating a vibrant and exciting campus that will enhance your experience outside of the classroom. You can enjoy a variety of programs, services, and events, including: Greek Life \u2013 fraternities and sororities Special events and cultural observances Student Government Association Registered student organizations The Forum Student Newspaper Students can also take part in campus recreation, Little Rock Trojan athletic events, the campus garden, the Military Student Success Center, or explore resources like tutoring, health services, and on-campus jobs.
