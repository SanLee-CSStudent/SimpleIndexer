Learn Why You Belong at UM Undergraduate Freshman Requirements The University of Montevallo has waived the ACT\/SAT test score requirement for admission and scholarships for students through fall 2024. In order to be admitted to the University of Montevallo for any term, incoming freshmen must meet: 3.0 cumulative GPA requirement Students with less than a 3.0 GPA will be evaluated on a case by case basis Incoming Freshman Admission Requirements Transfer Student Requirements A cumulative GPA of 2.0 or better based on a 4.0 scale. A transfer applicant must be a student in good standing. A maximum of 64 semester hours (or 96 quarter hours) may be transferred for credit from either a community or junior college.
