NPC's mission and vision indicates the focus of creating a better future for our students, community, stakeholders, and employees. Adult Education offers free services to residents in the area such as Adult Basic Education, General Adult Education, English as a Second Language, the W.A.G.E. program, and GED Testing. Career Services assists students and alumni in their search for career and education opportunities. Child Care Aware is a free child care resource and referral program that services thirteen counties in Arkansas. Services include but not limited to parental support, locating child care, child care provider training. Community & Corporate Training works with business and industry to provide customized workforce training and affordable professional development for local employers. Continuing Education specializes in non-credit courses which help develop the community in personal enrichment, specialized technical areas, hobby instruction, and training programs. Innovative Technologies Center generates opportunities for local businesses, schools and entrepreneurs to explore and create within the rapidly changing world of technology. NPC Foundation helps support students and campus improvements through scholarships and donations from the Hot Springs community. Alumni & Friends operates to keep alumni, faculty, staff, and friends of the college knowledgeable of events, activities, and news of the college.
