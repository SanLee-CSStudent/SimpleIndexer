How do I check in? \u2013 Call our Admissions Office at 870-235-4040 upon arrival to Overstreet Hall to check in for your tour. Where will check in be? \u2013 Check in will be outside of Overstreet Hall. Do I have to wear a mask? \u2013 Yes. If you do not have a mask we will be able to provide one. Will I be able to meet with other departments and professors? \u2013 Yes, we can arrange a meeting as part of your virtual or in-person tour. Capacity is limited. Virtual tours are still available.
