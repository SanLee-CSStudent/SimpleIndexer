The Board of Trustees (\u201CBoard\u201D) is the governing body of the University of Montevallo (\u201CUniversity\u201D) and establishes Board Policy, secures the financial resources to support adequately the University\u2019s programs, selects the President of the University, and approves the University budget. The Board authorizes the University President to develop and implement \u201Cpolicies and procedures required to administer effectively the operations of the University\u201D (Board Policy 01:001 ¡×9). Board Policies Policies adopted by the Board of Trustees. In general, Board Policies are statements usually dealing with broad university-wide issues that guide Board and Administration action. University Policies and Procedures are university-wide in scope, but are aimed at carrying out the President\u2019s charge to ensure the effective administration of day-to-day operations at the University.
