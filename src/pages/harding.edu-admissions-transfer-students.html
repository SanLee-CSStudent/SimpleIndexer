Transfer to Searcy Bison Ready is a program for transfer students who are admitted to Harding and will be enrolling for a future semester. Whether you are transferring from another college or university or upon completion of two-year program prerequisites elsewhere, you can be Bison Ready. No matter when you\u2019re officially joining us, we want to welcome you to the herd and help you get involved. Learn about Bison Ready Transfer to NWA Harding Northwest Arkansas, located in Rogers, works closely with Northwest Arkansas Community College (NWACC). The two-year degree completion program offers students the opportunity to earn a bachelor\u2019s degree in education after completing an associate\u2019s degree or the required prerequisite coursework at NWACC. Designed with the nontraditional student in mind, HUNWA offers flexible\/evening scheduling, online classes and eight week courses. Learn about HUNWA 2+2 Partner Institutions These partnerships between Harding University and 2-year colleges allow a seamless transfer experience to a bachelor's program at Harding for students who have earned an associate degree in a qualifying program from a partnering institution.
