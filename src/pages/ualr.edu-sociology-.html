In the study of our behavior as social beings, sociology offers a broad range of interests, from analyzing short contacts between anonymous individuals on the street to examining global social processes. Our Program Provides students with methodological and analytical skills that will allow them to conduct research and analyze data. Helps students to communicate effectively through writing and presentations. Encourages critical thinking in an environment where students learn to solve problems and become aware of how individuals, groups, and societies interact. Offers a valuable foundation for direct entry into meaningful careers or graduate study in a wide range of fields including law, education, counseling, social work, public health, and public administration.
