Download Red Mountain Campus Map (PDF) Federal Work Study Job Openings Tour Guides - The Recruitment Department Duties Include: The position will be the first point of contact for prospective students and their parents. Tour guides will deliver in-person and virtual campus tours associating buildings with programs of study, exploring points of interest, locations of services, etc. These tours could be in small, medium, or large groups. Tour guides may also be required to perform other job-related duties as assigned. The ideal candidate would have: Exceptional customer service skills Effective oral communication skills Experience with social media Preferably bilingual in Spanish Responding to visitor inquiries and serving as a consistent point of contact throughout the pre-visit process Maintaining records and contact lists for prospective students and their families The position may require work during evenings and weekend To Apply: If you are interested, fill out the application using the QR code, click the flyer above or email your resume to patricia.avila.torres@mesacc.edu. You can call (480)461-6392 for more information. Note you will also need to apply through the district HR system(job ID# 310454) for any Federal Work Study (FWS) Position. More Information For more information on upcoming virtual or in-person campus tours for the Red Mountain or Southern & Dobson campus, contact the Office of Outreach and Recruitment at 480-461-6392 or via email at recruiter@mesacc.edu.
