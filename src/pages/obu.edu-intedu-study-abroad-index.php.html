Check out what we offer, and start planning now! 10 REASONS TO CONSIDER STUDYING ABROAD There is a mountain of research that affirms the profound impact that an international experience has on the development of students. As a liberal arts university, we provide an education that addresses the whole person and seeks to produce students who will become comfortable as citizens of the world. Students gain the opportunity to learn and engage as members of the global community. Cross-cultural experiences help strengthen self-confidence and stimulate a sense of curiosity. International study opportunities provide a powerful way to grow personally and culturally. Globally competent graduates help foster national stability, security and economic vitality. Many schools, corporations and federal agencies seek employees with language skills and cross-cultural competence. Study abroad experiences provide a strong foundation for future educational and career choices. Studying abroad offers a great opportunity to cultivate new friends from around the world. International travel builds lifelong memories and vital coping skills. Studying abroad enhances the value of your degree and expands potential employment opportunities. Studies show 1.5% of university students in the U.S. actually study abroad. Don't miss your opportunity.
