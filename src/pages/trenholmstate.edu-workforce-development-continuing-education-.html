Education Continuing Education Purpose To extend the resources of Trenholm State Community College and provide programs and services with an emphasis on work skills upgrade, work-related training, or personal development in response to business, industry, and community needs. Non-Credit Continuing Education courses are: Offered during the day, evening, and weekends Offered in many different time spans, from a few hours to six to eight weeks Open to all age groups To participate in Continuing Education, you do not have to apply for admission and wait to be accepted. After completing the registration process, you are immediately accepted. Continuing Education Catalog 2022 Course Schedule has been published in an electronic format. The documents can be viewed and printed using Adobe Reader, which can be downloaded from the Adobe Reader Download Page.
