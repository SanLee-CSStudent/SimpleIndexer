Eligibility Requirements: You must have custody of a child under the age of 21 who lives with you full time and at least one of the following: Current or Former recipient of TEA Currently receiving SNAP, Medicaid, or ARKids Household income is less than 250% of the Federal Poverty Level. Program Benefits: Gas Vouchers Childcare Assistance Tuition and fees if you are not able to receive a Pell Grant or other scholarship Books Testing fees for Board Exams Want to apply for Career Pathways? First, submit your Intake Form online or bring in this Intake Form (PDF) to Student Service building, Room 157. When we get ready to have orientations for the next semester, we will email you our Orientation PowerPoint. Once you have familiarized yourself with our program you can call us to set up an appointment time because we meet with all applicants one-on-one to look at how we can help you. You\u2019ll be asked to bring in this documentation (PDF), including this questionnaire (PDF), to complete your file. Then, before the next semester begins, you will receive an award\/denial letter from our office letting you know what assistance we can help you with. Once you are in the CPI program you will need to submit tracking sheets (DOCX) and work verification (PDF) each month to continue to get assistance. You can find our Career Pathway Student Handbook here (PDF). Feel free to call or email us if you ever have any questions.
