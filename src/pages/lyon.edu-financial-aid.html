Pledge Forms Helpful Links We can't wait to meet you Schedule a visit Financial Aid for Lyon College The Office of Student Financial Aid offers a variety of financial aid resources, including Lyon College's Scholarship Program, federal and state grants, loans, and work-study programs. Over 99 percent of last year's incoming freshmen received scholarships and other forms of financial aid. Students must apply and be admitted to Lyon to receive a financial aid award. Applying for financial aid by mid-February ensures maximum eligibility for the aid available. Apply for Financial Aid Contact the financial aid office at (870) 307-7250 for more. Financial Aid Checklist FAFSA Complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) online at fafsa.gov using your FSA ID for electronic signatures (available at fsaid.ed.gov). Lyon College FAFSA code: 001088 Are you an Arkansas resident? Complete the Arkansas Department of Higher Education \"YOUniversal\" Financial Aid System application. College Tuition and Fees Lyon College focuses on creating an exceptional educational and cultural student experience at an affordable cost. Lyon is among the nation\u2019s elite for best bargain among private, selective liberal arts colleges.
