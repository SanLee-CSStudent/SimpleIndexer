The Class of 2022, Evening Bachelor\u2019s Degree Program The following list, presented in alphabetical order, contains each graduate\u2019s major, hometown, and Latin honors, where applicable. Latin honors are awarded according to the following criteria: Cum Laude: GPA of at least 3.5 with at least 45 graded hours at Huntingdon; Magna Cum Laude: GPA of at least 3.75 with at least 45 graded hours at Huntingdon; and Summa Cum Laude: GPA of at least 3.9 and a minimum of 60 graded hours at Huntingdon.
