Engineering Bachelor of Science in Construction Management Bachelor of Professional Studies Associate Degree Minor Welcome to Construction Management and Civil and Construction Engineering Students in our department participate in activities and classes to prepare for employment in construction. Leaders in the industry are created through management, construction and technology courses. We offer a minor, an associate degree and three bachelor degree options. Our bachelor of science in civil and construction engineering holds ABET accreditation and is a unique combination of two practices of engineering. The bachelor of science in construction management is ABET and ACCE accredited. Many scholarship opportunities exist for our students. There are several other resources available to help throughout their collegiate career as well. The faculty in the department hold many professional licenses and years of experience in the field. The Industry Advisory Board ensures that the department is staying up to date with industry standards. We build tomorrow\u2019s construction leaders in the Department of Construction Management and Civil and Construction Engineering. Join us in building tomorrow.
