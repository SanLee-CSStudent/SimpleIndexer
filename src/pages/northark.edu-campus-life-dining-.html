The Grill offers everyday items as well as specials, and it isn't your typical college food. The Northark Grill, located on the South Campus, is open for breakfast and lunch. The Grill has hearty and light breakfast items, soups, sandwiches, and healthy, low-carb options. You don't have to be a student to get this delicious food. The Northark Grill is open to the public. Northark Grill cards can be bought in increments of $25 each, and financial aid can be used for the cards.
