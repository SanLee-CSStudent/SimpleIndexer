Salon and Spa Management Programs of Study A- A+ Health Science Division\/Nursing Mathematics Division Business and Computer Science Division English and Fine Arts Division Natural Sciences Division Skills Training Social Sciences, Speech and Foreign Languages Division Tech Programs Industrial Electronics Salon and Spa Management Industrial Systems Technology Machine Tool Technology Medical Assistant Engineering Technician Emergency Medical Services Drafting and Design Technology Certified Production Technician Building Construction Athens State University at NACC Academic Maps Class Schedule Catalog SALON & SPA MANAGEMENT The Salon & Spa Management program at NACC provides opportunities for licensure and entrepreneurial training in cosmetology, esthetics and therapeutic massage. We prepare our students for careers in the salon and spa industry as licensed cosmetologist, estheticians and massage therapist. Instructors are licensed, degreed, and enthusiastic. Students receive practical, hands-on training and apply those skills by practicing in a real-life salon and spa environment. The Salon Institute is located in Scottsboro and houses classrooms, offices, and labs for the program, as well as a fully-functioning salon and spa. A required internship in the salon and spa gives students a competitive advantage by assuring that they have experience in all aspects of salon and spa management. Entrepreneurship and salon\/spa management business classes provide students with the knowledge, skills, and abilities they need to open their own successful business. Day classes are available at The Salon Institute in Scottsboro. Take advantage of the growth and stability expected in this exciting field by applying for admission today. Programs Cosmetology\/Esthetics\/Nails Cosmetology, Esthetics and Nail Technology prepares students with the skills, tools, and knowledge needed to pass the Alabama State Board of Cosmetology Licensure Examination. Esthetics Students receive knowledge and hands-on training with state-of-the-art equipment while practicing techniques in real-life salon situations in facials, dermabrasion, hydrofacial and cosmetic applications. Students are prepared to pass the Alabama State Board of Esthetics Licensure Examination. Therapeutic Massage More than just a relaxation massage, therapeutic massage involves manipulation of the muscles and tissues to alleviate pain, stress, and discomfort. In fact, physicians and chiropractors sometimes prescribe massages for their patients with certain injuries. The massage therapy program is part of the Salon and Spa Management program at NACC, is housed in The Salon Institute, located in Scottsboro. This state-of-the-art facility provides classroom and lab facilities for training students, as well as, private client therapeutic rooms. Students learn anatomy, physiology, kinesiology, and are afforded the opportunity to apply their skills in a real-life setting. Students who successfully complete the program are prepared to pass the Alabama Board of Massage Therapy Licensure Examination.
