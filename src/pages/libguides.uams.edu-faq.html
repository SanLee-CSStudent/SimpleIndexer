Let us know what you find useful or needs improvement: Feedback Form How do I print PowerPoint slides 3 or 6 to a page? If the slideshow is in Blackboard, save it to the computer or a USB flash\/thumb drive first. Open the slideshow then click on the File Tab on the top left corner of the screen and scroll down to \u201CPrint.\u201D In the center section click on the box that says \u201CFull Page Slides Print 1 slide per page\u201D and from the handout section of the drop down menu choose the number of slides per page you wish to print. For best results click on the \u201CColor\u201D box and select \u201Cpure black and white.\u201D How do I print a document in the library? PRINTING AND PHOTOCOPYING: The Library has 4 printer \/ copiers: 1st floor behind the staircase (color and black and white) 1st floor Silent Room (black and white) 2nd floor Northwest corner near the Title IX Coordinator's office (black and white) 3rd floor Northwest corner near the Writing Center office (black and white) Copies and prints are $0.10 per page for black and white and $0.20 for color. Pay for prints by adding funds to your UAMS ID badge at the Circulation Desk. When you log into a Library computer, you will receive an account balance notification on the screen. The Library accepts cash, checks, credit\/debit cards, and inter-departmental transfers. SCANNING: The copier\/printers also scan documents and email them to your UAMS email address. There is no charge for this service. For any other questions or help with printer cards, copiers or printers, please see the staff at the Circulation Desk during Library hours. How do reset my domain password? If your password has NOT expired you can change your password at any time by going to http:\/\/webmail.uams.edu\/ and clicking on the link titled \u201CPassword Reset.\u201D If your password has expired or you have forgotten your password you will need to call the IT Technical Support Help Desk at 686-8555. How do I access my USB flash\/thumb drive? Plug-in your USB thumb\/flash drive into one of the USB ports on the front of the computer and the computer will automatically recognize it. Go to \u201CMy Computer\u201D and Click on \u201CRemovable Drive (E)\u201D to access your files. If your thumb\/flash drive is not listed under \"My Computer\" contact a library staff member for assistance. How do I access a webpage that is not working in Internet Explorer? Shortcuts for these browsers can be found on the desktop of each public computer.
