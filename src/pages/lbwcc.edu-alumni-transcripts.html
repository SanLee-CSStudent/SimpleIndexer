Wallace Community College has partnered with Credentials Solutions to accept online transcript orders. Students and alumni are able to order their official transcripts 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Transcripts can be sent electronically or mailed in hard copy, depending on the receiving institution or destination. More information can be found on the transcript ordering page using the buttons below.* Current Students Your student email address (User ID) and Password Required. Current students (active students) Log Into your myLBWCC(OneACCS) Account (with student email address and password) Select \u201CStudent\u201D Tab Select \u201CStudent Records\u201D Select \u201COrder Official Transcript\u201D Select \u201CBegin Order\u201D and follow instructions on screen Former Students No student email address (User ID) and password required. Former students who have not been enrolled 12 or more months. The cost for an official transcript is $5 which may be paid by Visa, MasterCard, Discover and American Express. Check Status Use the above Link to check the status of your transcript request. Contact Credentials Solutions with any questions regarding transcript ordering problems. *Please note that all financial obligations to the college must be satisfied prior to a transcript being released. ? 2022 Lurleen B.
