The History & Evloution of Human Sciences. Distinguished Lecture Series speaker June Henton. The College of Human Sciences will present its annual IQLA Distinguished Lecture Series, featuring June Henton, on Tuesday, March 22, at 2 p.m. at The Hotel at Auburn University and Dixon Conference Center. Henton, the 2021 recipient of an International Quality of Life Award, served as dean of the College of Human Sciences for 34 years, before retiring in 2019. She will speak on the history and evolution of human sciences, having walked through many changes and advances over the past three decades. In-person seating in the hotel auditorium will be available on a first-come, first-served basis. To learn more about the history of the International Quality of Life Awards, go to iqla.auburn.edu.
