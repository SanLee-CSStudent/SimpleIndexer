The division also offers the following certificates in Computer Information Systems: Google IT Apple Swift Computer Graphics and Web Design Office Technology Support Cybersecurity Technician Security+ Add-on We offer classes in Programming, Microcomputer Applications, Computer Maintenance, Web Design, Cybersecurity, and Networking. We are also proud to be a Cisco Networking Academy. The Computer Information Systems Department also encourages participation in SkillsUSA. SkillsUSA is an organization for students who are enrolled in training programs in trade, technical and skilled service occupations.
