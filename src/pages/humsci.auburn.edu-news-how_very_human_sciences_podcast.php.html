Welcome to the new College of Human Sciences podcast \u201CHow Very Human \u2013 Sciences.\u201D It will feature alumni and industry leaders sharing their stories and insight for the students of today. In the inaugural episode, College of Human Sciences Director of Communications and External Relations Kim Hendrix sits down with Hospitality Management alumnus Catherine Wayman, '07 to talk about her career with C. Wayman Floral, Events and Production and the exciting and unexpected course it's taking now. Catherine Wayman is creative director for C. Wayman Floral, Events and Production in Atlanta. She graduated from Auburn with a degree in Hotel and Restaurant Management in the College of Human Sciences in 2007. Catherine was recently recognized as the 2022 Outstanding Alumna in the Hospitality Management Program.
