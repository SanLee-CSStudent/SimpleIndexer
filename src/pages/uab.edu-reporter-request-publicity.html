What channels could be used to publicize my submission? University Relations maintains a network of campus communication channels, including: Internal communications resources, such as: UAB Reporter website, which houses campus updates, policy and human resources announcements and feature stories on Blazer accomplishments. MyUAB portal GreenMail, UAB's student newsletter Publishes each Wednesday during the academic year except for holiday periods, and every other Wednesday during the summer. Subscribe to GreenMail. the eReporter, UAB's employee newsletter Publishes Tuesday and Friday of each week. The deadline for submission is 10 a.m. Monday and Thursday for the following day's edition. Publishing exceptions can include Tuesdays following UAB closures for federal\/state holidays and during academic winter break. Subscribe to the eReporter. The UAB Campus Calendar, the central hub for all university events Please note that Campus Calendar submissions may take up to 48 hours for approval depending on the volume of active submissions. UAB Magazine Digital signage in campus buildings Banner slider on UAB Home Banner sliders on websites such as UAB Faculty and UAB Students External communications resources, such as: UAB News, the central repository for UAB announcements of potential interest to news media Social media channels such as Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn and Twitter University Relations also works closely with UAB distributed communicators to determine whether in-unit communication channels (such as department, division or school news websites, newsletters, social media channels or digital signage) are the best fit for the submission. Did you know you can manage your UAB e-newsletter preferences? Visit our Subscription Center to select the specific emails you want from the UAB units you're interested in. You can also access the Subscription Center by selecting the link at the bottom of the UAB emails you currently receive. How are requests reviewed? Submissions are reviewed by University Relations' content strategy team and are assessed based on factors such as audience, institutional priorities, timeliness and relevant dates or deadlines. As noted above, University Relations is working to prioritize sharing stories, events and announcements that exemplify UAB\u2019s strategic plan and shared values; consider those as a framework when sending submissions and pitches to University Relations. Any submission can be revised or edited based on University Relations' style guidelines. How will University Relations communicate with me about my submission? Once a request is submitted, a ticket will be opened with University Relations for processing and an email will be sent to the submitter. Updates will be provided via the ticketing system during the processing, and submitters can communicate with University Relations by replying in the the email thread. How can I submit a clinical trial? To submit a clinical trial, use the clinical trials form. Submit a Publicity Request Note: A BlazerID and password are required to use the request form.
