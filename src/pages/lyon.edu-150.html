Campus Map News Commencement 2022 Explore Lyon We can't wait to meet you Schedule a visit Come celebrate Lyon College's Sesquicentennial! We have planned a week of 150th Anniversary Celebrations to honor Lyon College's perseverance and legacy. The events will kick off with ScotsFest (Homecoming & Annual Arkansas Scottish Festival) on Oct. 14-16, with daily activities throughout the following week. The celebrations will culminate in Founders' Weekend and a gala on Oct. 22. You only get to celebrate your 150th anniversary once! Be sure to register for the events you want to attend! Share your Arkansas College and Lyon College stories: The College is compiling memories from alumni and friends. You can share a video, audio file, or written message with us here.
