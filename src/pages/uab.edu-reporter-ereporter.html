FAQs + - I can\u2019t unsubscribe from my UAB email address Click to collapse Because the university is committed to the principle of prompt and open communication with its employees, the UAB eReporter is distributed to all UAB faculty and staff with a BlazerID, without exception. We understand that you may choose to delete the messages you receive from this account; however, we would encourage you read it regularly so you will not miss important matters of policy and events affecting the entire campus that will be communicated through the eReporter. + - I don\u2019t work at UAB anymore but I still receive The eReporter Click to collapse There are only two reasons that would happen: either you have subscribed using a non-uab.edu address or you are still active in the UAB HR database. To unsubscribe from your personal Internet service use this form. If you still are identified as an employee in the Campus Directory, contact the department in which you were employed and ask that your status be changed to reflect your departure. The mailing list is auto-generated from the employee database, which our staff cannot alter.
