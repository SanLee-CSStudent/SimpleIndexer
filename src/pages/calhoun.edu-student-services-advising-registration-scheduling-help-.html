Select OneACCS Self Service Select Student Select Student Landing Page Select Registration Select Prepare for Registration Select Term and Continue How do I register for classes? Click OneACCS Banner Self Service Log in with your calhoun.edu email address and MyCalhoun portal password Click Student Click Student Landing Page Click Registration Click Register for Classes Select Term Search for a Subject You can enter multiple subjects, or just one Enter Course Number if you know it Click Search Click the course title for more information Click Add Button to the right Click Search Again to add more classes Click Submit Button in the bottom right corner to officially register for those classes How do I view\/print my class schedule? Click OneACCS Banner Self Service Log in with your calhoun.edu email address and MyCalhoun portal password Click Student Click Student Landing Page Click Registration Click Register for Classes Select Term Click Schedule and Options Click Printer Icon to print a copy of your schedule How do I drop a class? Click OneACCS Banner Self Service Log in with your calhoun.edu email address and MyCalhoun portal password Click Student Click Student Landing Page Click Registration Click Register for Classes Select Term In the summary box, Select Action (drop) next to the class you want to drop How do I view my transcript? Select OneACCS Self Service Select Student Select Student Landing Page Select Transcript Type: Unofficial Transcript Select Submit How do I view course descriptions?
