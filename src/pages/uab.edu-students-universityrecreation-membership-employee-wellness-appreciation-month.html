UAB Employee Wellness Appreciation month (EWAM) is a FREE, \u202Fmonth-long\u202Fprogram centered\u202Faround\u202Ffitness, wellness, and nutrition that focus to improve the healthy lifestyles of the UAB employees. Each Monday in June, participants will receive an email related to that specific weekly theme, where they will have an opportunity to learn something new, or to be challenged in a fun, engaging way, and ultimately, to become a better version of themselves! You will also have a chance to participate in our in-person tabling sessions or workshops each Thursday to further improve their well being. Weekly Themes: Week 1 - Fitness Center Orientations Week 2 - Hydration Challenge Week 3 - Free Mini Fitness Assessments Week 4 - Working Life Recipes Week 5 - Living Active ¡Á Treadmill or Deadlifts (It's lot more!) All participants will be eligible to earn a FREE month of UAB University Recreation Membership + earn points in their My Health Rewards account. Register below for UAB EWAM and come to the UAB Campus Recreation Center at least 2 out of the 5 Thursdays in June to receive a voucher for a FREE MONTH OF MEMBERSHIP! Note: Only ACTVE UAB employees are eligible to participate in the UAB Employee Wellness Appreciation Month.
