We invite prospective international students to consider us for further education. At JSU, International students are students who are not Permanent Residents, U.S. Citizens or Undocumented\/DACA students. You are an international student if you need a student visa to come to the United States or if you hold any other visa status that makes you eligible to attend universities in the United States.
