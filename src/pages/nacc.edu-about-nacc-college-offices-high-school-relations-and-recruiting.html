The Office of High School Relations and Recruiting at Northeast Alabama Community College works to: Interpret programs and courses and other printed materials offered by the college to prospective students and high school counselors. Visit high schools to assist students in preparing to become a NACC student Coordinate campus visits Counsel with prospective students (first-time, transfer, and returning) and parents, serving as an on-campus liaison between the prospective student and the college until an advisor is assigned. Represent the college at high school career day\/night programs and other functions Plan special events on campus for high school students, teachers, counselors, and prospective students Schedule a campus tour to check out our beautiful and student-friendly campus!
