Vision Statement The University of Central Arkansas Campus of the Future will be a public destination that integrates a 21st century learning environment with the urban vitality of Conway and the greater region. UCA students, faculty, staff, alumni, visitors, neighbors, and potential development partners will experience a vibrant, twenty-four\/seven campus. The UCA Campus of the Future will feature exemplary live\/learn\/work\/play opportunities and environments that engage the learner, satisfy the curious, inspire the creative and attract the larger Conway community.
