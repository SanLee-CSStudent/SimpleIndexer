NPC's Corporate Training program is designed to help Arizona employers develop and train outstanding employees so you'll stay competitive in your market. Our training isn't just generic; we focus on specific best business practices that are tailored to meet or exceed your company's specific standards and expectations. We can facilitate meetings, planning sessions and focus groups to help you identify your biggest training needs and the best strategies for achieving your goals. NPC business training offers: Quality business training to meet your specific requirements and standards. Our services are guaranteed. Not satisfied with our training? Make your recommended changes and NPC will re-offer the training at no extra charge. Save money when you train locally. Customized in-service employee training, tailored to your needs. Short-term training sessions; we work on your time frame, not ours. Reasonable cost, expenses are kept to a minimum. We can even broker shared-cost workshops with other employers who have similar needs. Your business, large or small, will benefit from NPC's decade-long experience in training employees in northeast Arizona. Call today. A representative will be happy to visit your workplace to discuss our training options and needs! For more information contact Community & Corporate Learning at (928) 536-6232 | email.
