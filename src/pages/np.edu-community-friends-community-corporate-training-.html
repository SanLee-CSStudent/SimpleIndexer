Our Mission Community and Corporate Training offers a wide range of services to help business and community members achieve personal and professional goals. Continuing Education We offer a variety of short, non-credit classes to meet your professional and personal goals. Classes are offered on campus as well as online. Continuing Education Innovative Technologies Center The New Innovative Technologies Center at National Park College offers a unique technology oriented educational experience to the Local Industry, K12 Educators, and the Community. Innovative Technologies Center Workforce Development Our staff will consult with your company to identify workplace skills needs and deliver customized training designed to meet your strategic goals. Workforce Development Child Care Aware Child Care Aware of West Central Arkansas at National Park College is a community child care resource and referral program providing services in thirteen counties. Child Care Aware Apprenticeships Apprenticeship is an industry-driven, high-quality career pathway where employers can develop and prepare their future workforce and individuals can obtain paid work experience, classroom instruction and a portable nationally-recognized credential.
