See skills employers want.> What is Prior Learning Assessment (PLA)? With PLA, you have three potential ways to help you save time and money on your online business degree: Have any relevant training, industry certifications, or work experiences evaluated for potential credit towards your degree. Choose from a wide range of journal topics such as public speaking, volunteering or more, and write a journal entry about your past experience to see if you can earn credits. If you have extensive knowledge in a specific subject area, you could take a test to earn potential credit toward your degree. How does an online business degree prepare me for, or enhance, my business career? Coursework in our business degrees is relevant and taught by faculty with experience in their fields, helping you gain in-demand skills in a growing field. What type of course requirements are needed to complete a business degree? By following a \u201CLearn, Practice, Apply\u201D curriculum model, you\u2019ll learn the key elements of business and how to apply that knowledge and skillset to real-world scenarios. These practical activities will give you the valuable feedback you need to better review and prepare for assignments throughout your courses.
