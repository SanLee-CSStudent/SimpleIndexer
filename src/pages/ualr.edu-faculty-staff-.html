Awards Helpful Links Human Resources Notaries on Campus Events Calendar Accessibility Organizational Chart Faculty and Staff at UA Little Rock The University of Arkansas at Little Rock is a metropolitan research university that provides an accessible, quality education through flexible learning and unparalleled internship opportunities. We prepare our more than 9,000 students to be innovators and responsible leaders in their fields. Committed to its metropolitan research university mission, UA Little Rock is a driving force in Little Rock\u2019s thriving cultural community and a major component of the city and state\u2019s growing profile as a regional leader in research, technology transfer, economic development, and job creation. UA Little Rock is known for its community service, teaching excellence, and dynamic research. This concentration to service, teaching, and research attracts world-class faculty. UA Little Rock faculty regularly receive national and international recognition for their pioneering research or published works. No matter your role, UA Little Rock offers opportunities to learn and grow. We are a community that is warm and inclusive, diverse, professional, and committed to your success.
