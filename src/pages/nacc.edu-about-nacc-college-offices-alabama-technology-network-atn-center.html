ATN's mission is to provide the most innovative technical assistance and training to continually improve Alabama businesses and industries. Since its opening, the ATN staff has worked with many area businesses in an effort to improve industry productivity and quality. Among the businesses of the DeKalb and Jackson County area, the center has completed projects with Gold Kist, Inspec Tech, ICB Electronics, and the Gas House. In addition to local projects, the center has served many other businesses throughout the state. The center is located in the Workforce Development Building in room 124.
