Accreditation at UA Little Rock Comprehensive View Frequently Asked Questions Program Review Non-Accredited Programs Accredited Programs UA Little Rock Rock was reaffirmed for accreditation in 2020 on the Open Pathway by the Higher Learning Commission (HLC). HLC is a institutional accrediting organization recognized by the U.S. Department of Education. During the 2019-20 academic year, UA Little Rock completed the Open Pathway that included a Quality Initiative Project, compiling information and feedback from every sector of the university community, a Comprehensive Evaluation and Site Visit. The process resulted in a reaffirmation on a 10-year cycle. UA Little Rock will undergo a midpoint assurance review at the Open Pathway in 2023-2024. Announcements Reaffirmation of Accreditation findings released OCTOBER 23, 2020 UA Little Rock announced the successful reaffirmation of accreditation through 2029-2030 academic year on July 2020.
