Institutional Research About Institutional Research The purpose of the Office of Institutional Research at Southern Arkansas University is to create decision support that helps SAU fulfill its mission to educate students for productive and fulfilling lives, as well as provide accurate statistical information for dissemination to the public while maintaining the highest professional standards of accuracy, objectivity, and timeliness. For more information, please review our Student Outcome Data and the College of Education\u2019s CAEP Accredited Programs and Education Student Outcomes.
