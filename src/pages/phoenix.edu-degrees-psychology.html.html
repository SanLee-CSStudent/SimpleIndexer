Our online psychology bachelor\u2019s degree programs can prepare you for careers in communications, human resources, marketing, and media while our online psychology master\u2019s degrees equip you with the skills to work in human resources, talent management and organizational development. You\u2019ll also be able to apply psychological principles to address personal, social and organizational problems. Can I specialize or customize my online psychology degree? Yes, our psychology bachelor\u2019s degree programs allow for the opportunity to specialize your onliine degree by choosing electives in a combination of Business, Economics, Human Resources, Marketing, Diversity, Culture, Communication, Global Perspectives, Journalism, Media, and Information Systems.
