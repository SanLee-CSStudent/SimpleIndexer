Welcome to the Montevallo family! We are excited to meet you at Orientation! All new UM students must attend an Orientation session in order to register for their classes. We have three goals for your Orientation day: To answer all of your questions To assist you as you select and register for your classes, and; For you to leave excited to return for your first semester at UM! 2022 Freshman Orientation For freshman students beginning school in August 2022 We have three orientation dates still available for new freshmen beginning school in August 2022. The 2022 Transfer Orientation options are June 9th and July 21st. Registration for these dates is now open. Transfer Orientation Information 2022 Summer School Orientation For all new students beginning school in June or July 2022 We have one orientation date available for new students beginning school in the summer term.
