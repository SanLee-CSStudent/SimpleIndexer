If you need a course to meet a state or educational requirement, or expand your area of practice, you can complete all our counseling courses online and in 6-8 weeks each. In order to practice as a Counselor in any state, you must be licensed as a Licensed Professional Counselor (LPC), Marriage and Family Therapist (MFT), or other comparable state license designation. License requirements may vary. It is your responsibility to ascertain and meet licensure requirements in any state in which you desire to practice. These courses do not lead to professional certification or licensure as a mental health provider. Learn from qualified faculty From family therapists and mental health counselors to clinical directors and psychologists, our faculty are professionals who bring a diverse range of experience to the classroom. Meet your goals on your timeline These courses are only 6-8 weeks long. You can enroll online, learn on your schedule, and fit them into your life. Frequently Asked Questions What are counseling courses like at University of Phoenix? As a student, you can choose courses that will help you learn: Primary theoretical models of personality theory and counseling practice, including psychodynamic, affective, cognitive behavioral, humanistic, interpersonal, multicultural, and systems theory Counseling and communication skills such as, attending behaviors, eliciting, structuring, and summarizing. How to develop competencies necessary to provide career and vocational counseling to clients Crisis theory, methods of crisis response, psychological effects associated with trauma, assessment strategies, and intervention models Models of supervision and counselor development, supervision and management processes, assessment and evaluation issues, and ethical and legal aspects of supervision The many facets of human sexuality and the treatment of sexual dysfunctions in a safe and respectful environment Why do I need to meet prerequisites? Prerequisites are gathered for individual courses to make sure every student enrolled in the course has the foundational knowledge to be successful. To show you meet the required prerequisite for some courses, you simply have to upload a digital copy of your transcript (official or unofficial) during the checkout process.
