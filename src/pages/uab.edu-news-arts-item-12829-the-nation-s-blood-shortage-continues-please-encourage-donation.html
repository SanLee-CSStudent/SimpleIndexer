Please encourage donation. by Bob Shepard May 04, 2022 Print Email Blood supplies continue to be low nationwide. Blood supplies remain dangerously low across the United States. UAB Medicine and the American Red Cross will host a blood drive at UAB Hospital North Pavilion, Tuesday, May 10th through Friday, May 13th. The drive will operate from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m. each day. Appointments are encouraged and are available at www.redcrossblood.org, sponsor code \u201CUABNP\u201D, or call 1-800-RED CROSS (1-800-733-2767). UAB\u2019s North Pavilion is at 615 18th Street, Birmingham. All donors will receive a t-shirt. Donors of all blood types are needed. A blood donor card or driver\u2019s license or two other forms of identification are required at check-in. Individuals who are at least 17 years of age, weigh at least 110 pounds and are in generally good health may be eligible to donate blood. Donors will receive a recognition item and a cholesterol screening. UAB is one of the largest users nationally of blood supplied by blood centers. Donors can give blood up to six times a year, every eight weeks.
