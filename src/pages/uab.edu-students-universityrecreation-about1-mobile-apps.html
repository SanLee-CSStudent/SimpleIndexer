UAB Campus Recreation CSI App UAB Campus Recreation CSI App This application allows you to manage your UAB University Recreation account directly from your mobile device. You are able to: Get your access code, in case you forget your ID View basic contact information for UAB University Rec Edit your contact information Submit comments, questions, and feedback to our staff View fitness class schedules and set reminders for classes Register for programs and services Check your reservations How to access the App Download the app from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store by searching \u201CUAB Campus Recreation\u201D (Download the white icon app). Open the Campus Recreation App and you will be prompted in with login information. In order to log in, you will need to use your OLS user name and password *The OLS user name IS NOT your Blazer ID. B Well UAB app Mental Health Tools at Your Fingertips Juggling school work, jobs, family and a social life, life can be stressful causing you to forget about certain things. Some of those things being good sleep habits, nutritional meals, movement, and resiliency. B Well UAB app has been created to help you create a personalized self care plan! Mental health and wellness goals are NOW in your reach. Download the app today and see it can improve your wellbeing for yourself! With the B Well App, you are able to create a personalized self-care plan that builds good habits in sleep, movement, nutrition routine and resiliency. The habit checklist and wellness journal \u2014 visible only to you \u2014 help you meet your goals and track your journey to better self-care and mental health. In addition, self-help tools in the app put mental health services, mindfulness resources, and campus mental health and recreation events at your fingertips. Crisis help is also available. Build your plan today to help reach your mental health and wellness goals.
