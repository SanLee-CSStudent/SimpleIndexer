The Arizona-Sonora Desert Museum If you\u2019ve never seen a prickly javelina or an oh-so-cute prairie dog up close and personal, this world-renowned Southwest destination gives you the chance to do so. Bike Paths Whether you\u2019re riding your cruiser to brunch or cycling 111 miles for the Tour de Tucson bike race, Tucson is one of the best cities in the nation to go by two wheels. CHILL TOP PLACES TO KICK BACK AND UNWIND Coffee or tea? Whether it\u2019s the Scented Leaf Tea House, Caf? Luce, or the always-popular Cartel Coffee Lab, there\u2019s no shortage of perfect study spots (or caffeine). World-famous resorts Rub elbows with the rich and famous right here in Tucson. Resorts like Miraval and the Ritz-Carlton at Dove Mountain are many celebrities\u2019 top getaways. La Encantada Peruse high-end retail stories at this outdoor shopping center. CULTURE ENJOY AUTHENTIC TUCSON Dia de los Muertos You can\u2019t miss the annual All Souls Parade in downtown Tucson. Practice your sugar skull face painting skills ahead of time. Tucson Meet Yourself Food trucks, music, artists. This annual festival is a great way to introduce yourself to our hometown. Fourth Avenue Street Fair Shop \u2018til you drop at this biannual fair featuring local artisans. FOOD A FOODIE DESTINATION UNESCO City of Gastronomy Tucson has unique eats around every corner. Some of Wildcats\u2019 favorite spots are the Hub, Caf? Poca Cosa and Culinary Dropout. Foodies everywhere, unite. The Best 23 Miles of Mexican Food Tucson boldly claims having the best Mexican food in the country. There\u2019s only one way to find out. Sonoran Hot Dogs A true local delicacy, Sonoran Dogs are one-part hot dog, one-part bacon, and many parts tasty toppings. Sounds weird? Just trust us on this one.
