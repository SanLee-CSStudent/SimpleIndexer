To report a crime, contact Chief Sam Martin at (870) 715-7553 or Officer Eddie Ragland at (870) 302-5453. The North Arkansas College Police Department is here to serve and protect. The North Arkansas College Police Department consists of Chief Sam Martin, Police Officer Eddie Ragland, and a Reserve Force of additional part time Officers. Campus Police officers are certified by the state of Arkansas. The North Arkansas College Police Department continues a working relationship with the Boone County Sheriff\u2019s Department and Harrison Police Department for additional resources as needed. Arrests and fines are processed through the Boone County District or Circuit Courts or North Arkansas College. Parking violations and warning tickets are processed through North Arkansas College. Employees, students, guests, and properties of North Arkansas College are under the jurisdiction of the North Arkansas College Police Department concerning police matters. Arkansas Law and US Federal Laws are enforced by North Arkansas College Police Department. For any additional information or questions you might have, please feel free to contact North Arkansas College Police Department.
