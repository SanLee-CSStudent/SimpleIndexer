Arkansas Tech - Norman Hall Gallery Facebook Gallery Hours: Closed During Winter Break 2022 Schedule Spring 2022________________________________________________________ Jan 10 \u2013 Feb 25 New Year, New Prints, works by members of the Arkansas Society of Printmakers Senior Class Exhibitions: Mar 7 - 18 Graphic Design Senior Show Mar 30 - Apr 11 Fine Art Senior Show Apr 14 - Apr 27 Game and Interactive Media Design Senior Show Norman Hall Gallery Mission Statement Our primary objective is to present a diverse collection of exhibitions every year that not only entertain but educate our community and student populations. We embrace our responsibility of igniting the spark of creativity and challenging thoughts in all of the visual media. Each exhibiting artist is solely responsible for their own creative content within our venue. Interaction between the artist and the public is strongly encouraged through gallery openings, gallery talks and classroom critiques held by the visiting artists. Our mission is to display, discuss and develop future artists and art educators through example. Attention: Norman Hall Art Gallery would like to invite any artist interested in exhibiting here at Arkansas Tech University to apply through mail or in person. There is no deadline, this is a continuous open call for submissions.
