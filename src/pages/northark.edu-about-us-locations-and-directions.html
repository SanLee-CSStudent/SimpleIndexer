Tournament John Paul Hammerschmidt Lecture Series Reports and Policies Vendor Information Locations & Directions North Arkansas College has two campuses and the Carroll County Center in Berryville to meet the needs of our students and community. The South and North campuses are located in Harrison, and the Carroll County Center is located in Berryville to help serve students in that area. South Campus 1515 Pioneer Drive Harrison, AR 72601 870-743-3000 The South Campus is home to most general education and allied health courses as well as the offices of admissions, financial aid, registrar, safety, student support and veterans\u2019 affairs. The advising center, bookstore, south library and president\u2019s office also are located on the South Campus. The college\u2019s gymnasium, Pioneer Pavilion, baseball and softball fields and a disc golf course are at the South Campus. Learn more about the South Campus North Campus 1320 North Spring Rd. Harrison, AR 72601 870-743-3000 Most of the courses for technical and skill trades programs are located at the North Campus. The Northark Technical Center for high school students also is housed on the North Campus. Learn more about the North Campus Carroll County Center 804 West Freeman Berryville, AR 72616 870-423-4455 The Carroll County Center is in Berryville and offers traditional classes and electives as well as noncredit community education classes. The center also houses an adult education program and English as a second language classes. Learn more about the Carroll County Center Main Street 823 N. Main Street Harrison, AR 72601 The Main Street location houses the College's business offices as well as Educational Talent Search (ETS) offices.
