Gadsden State offers a variety of club and organization opportunities for students across all five campuses. Contact the Director of Student Life for more information. Clubs & Organizations Gadsden State students have the opportunity to participate in more than 40 clubs and organizations. Athletics As a member of the National Junior College Athletic Association, Gadsden State sponsors intercollegiate teams in tennis, men's basketball, women's basketball and women's volleyball. Fine Arts The Gadsden State Music Department offers classes for the musical novice as well as performance classes for the seasoned performer. Ambassadors The Student Ambassador program at Gadsden State strives to inspire students through leadership development.
