Veteran Student Checklist Enrollment Certification Request Book an Appointment Our Mission Meet Our Team New Students Continuing Students Transfer Students Our Mission The mission of Veteran Services is to successfully transition and integrate student veterans into higher education. Our goal is to provide student veterans with the superior services and support they deserve, while bringing campus-wide awareness of military and veteran culture. Veteran Services collaborates with community veteran organizations to form innovative partnerships to enhance educational and career opportunities for our student veterans. Continuing Students Enroll in classes. Your classes must follow the Degree Plan you have specified with the VA. Note: To change your program of study, you must make an appointment with the Academic Career Advisor for Veteran Services. Request Veterans Benefits for the semester. Every semester you need to complete a Enrollment Certification Request Online. This informs the VA of your upcoming schedule. This form MUST be completed every semester or when any CHANGES are made to your schedule, in order to receive benefits. Request a Facts Tuition Deferment if needed. Do this through Self-Service Throughout the semester, if tutoring becomes necessary, this can be funded through the VA. Ask our office for more information. There is also free tutoring available at our Student Success Center. Students receiving benefits under Chapters 30 and 1606 are required to verify their enrollment every month. This can be done by calling 1-877-823-2378 or going to the WAVE system. Transfer Students Students who are transferring from another college or university need to fill out a Request for Change of Program or Place of Training, or VA Form 22-1995 if using VA Educational benefits Dependents are required to fill out a different form, the Request for Change of Program or Place of Training for Survivors' and Dependents Education Assistance, or VA Form 22-5495. These forms need to be completed and submitted to the veteran services office. They can also be completed on VETS.GOV Apply to Arizona Western College Follow the New Veteran Student Checklist.
