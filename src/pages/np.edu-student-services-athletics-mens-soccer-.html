Training Continuing Education Innovative Technologies Center Parents & Visitors Parents & Visitors Resources Campus Map FERPA Search National Park College: Search Men's Soccer Home > Student Services > Athletics > Men's Soccer Nighthawks Soccer competes as a Division I program within the National Junior College Athletic Association (NJCAA) Region 2. NPC plays its games at Hot Springs Baptist Church in Hot Springs. The Nighthawks Soccer program is led by Head Coach Kevin Tello. Coach Tello comes to NPC from the University of Arkansas Soccer program where he served as Director of Soccer of Operations. Coach Tello has also served on the soccer coaching staff of Hendrix College and the Little Rock Rangers, as well as the Head Coach at Vilonia High School. Coach Tello played collegiate soccer at Ouachita Baptist University. For more information regarding NPC Soccer please contact Coach Kevin Tello at Kevin.Tello@np.edu.
