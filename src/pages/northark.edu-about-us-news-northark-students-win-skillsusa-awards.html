Hammerschmidt Lecture Series Reports and Policies Vendor Information Northark students win SkillsUSA awards by Northark Office of Public Relations Thu Apr 28, 2022 at 10:33 AM North Arkansas College students recently competed in a SkillsUSA competition and won nine awards. North Arkansas College celebrates the recent success of students at the SkillsUSA competition in Hot Springs, Ark., on April 12. Nineteen students from North Arkansas College Technical Programs competed against other schools from the state in their perspective programs. Five students won first place, three students won second place, and one student won third place. SkillsUSA is a national program that promotes student leadership, development, and success for college and high school students within their skilled and technical trades.
