The Arizona Board of Regents (ABOR) is the governing body for Arizona's three public universities. The board consists of 12 members, including two student Regents, the Governor of Arizona and the Superintendent of Public Instruction. Image President Robert C. SPBAC FACULTY SENATE As the legislative body that represents the general faculty, the Faculty Senate creates policies for academic personnel, instruction and curriculum, research, student affairs, ethics and more. Faculty Senate UNDERGRADUATE COUNCIL The Undergraduate Council reviews all undergraduate curricular action items forwarded from the Academic Programs Subcommittee and the Curriculum & Policies Subcommittee, and also from other areas of the university. Undergraduate Council Staff Council Representing all staff employees, Classified and University Staff who contribute greatly to the success of our community, the UArizona Staff Council is active in the university\u2019s shared governance and advises the university administration. Staff Council ASUA Associated Students of the University of Arizona \u2013 the University's Student Government \u2013 represents the student body in order to serve, engage, and empower all students through advocacy and a large variety of programs and resources. ASUA GPSC The Graduate & Professional Student Council promotes the academic, economic and social aims of graduate and professional students at the University.
