Explore Engineering The School of Engineering offers a wide range of educational opportunities for undergraduates seeking bachelor's degrees. The School of Engineering offers Bachelors of Science degrees in five different areas. Biomedical Engineering (3.2 GPA required for admission) Tissue Engineering; Implants\/Devices; Cardiac Electrophysiology; Biomedical Imaging Civil Engineering (2.2 GPA required for admission) Structural Engineering; Construction Management; Environmental Engineering; Transportation Electrical Engineering (2.2 GPA required for admission) Software Engineering; Controls; Telecommunications Engineering Design (2.2 GPA required for admission) Materials Science and Engineering (2.2 GPA required for admission) Composite and Plastic Materials; Casting and Solidification; Metals Processing Mechanical Engineering (2.2 GPA required for admission) High Performance Computing, Modeling and Simulation; Energy and Combustion Systems; Carbon Capture and Sequestration What can you do with these degrees? Does helping people drive you? What you study at UAB, in the Pre-Nursing and Nursing curriculum, will prepare you to serve others in a fulfilling and successful career. Take time to research potential career options within the fields of healthcare and nursing. Majors from the School of Health Professions include: Biomedical Sciences (2.75 GPA required for admission) Career paths include: Physician assistant studies, medicine, physical therapy, biotechnology, clinical laboratory science, genetic counseling, and beyond. Healthcare Management (2.5 GPA required for admission) Career paths include: The management of hospitals, clinics, medical supply firms, and other health care organizations.
