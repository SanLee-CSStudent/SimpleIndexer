Information Clinton School Information The Higher Education Opportunity Act requires institutions participating in federally-funded financial aid programs make information about the institution available to the public, current and prospective students, student loan borrowers, and current employees. The links on the University of Arkansas at Little Rock Consumer Information site provide readily accessible consumer information to help students, parents, employees, and the university community make informed decisions about higher education. After exhausting the department or informal complaint process, students may file a formal complaint with the university at ualr.edu\/deanofstudents\/student-complaints The Student Complaint Form addressed above does not cover the following issues. Please use the links provided to submit a complaint on the following: Title IX Grade Appeals Parking Violations ADA-related Discrimination Other issues where a separate policy and procedure for resolution are already approved For assistance or more information on the student complaints processes, contact the Office of the Dean of Students at deanofstudents@ualr.edu. To submit a complaint to the Arkansas Department of Higher Education, go to ADHE Complaints. To submit a complaint to the Higher Learning Commission, go to HLC Complaints.
