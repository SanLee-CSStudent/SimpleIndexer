The pros vs. the, well, pros. We can think of all sorts of reasons for you to join us at UAB. We offer an exceptional education. We\u2019ve got lots of fun activities. We\u2019re adjacent to a business district loaded with career opportunities. We\u2019re diverse. We\u2019re affordable. So is there a reason not to attend UAB? Well, we guess if you have an aversion to the color green or something, maybe you should pick a different school. Then again, nah. Who doesn\u2019t love green? Academics Not to brag, but we\u2019re good at this. Really good. In fact, we keep getting ranked on list after list for our academics. That\u2019s nice and everything, but our real prize comes from seeing our graduates go on to do amazing things using the very skills they learned here. We\u2019re proud of them. So can you blame us if we brag from time to time? View Academics Birmingham Our city is amazing. In fact, we call it the Magic City. We have renowned restaurants and entertainment. World-class museums and theatres. Murals all over town. More parks than you can imagine. And a 56-foot tall statue of the Roman god of fire standing guard over it all. Like we said, amazing. Get to Know Us Research We\u2019ve put a high priority on investigating and discovering. We rake in hundreds of millions of dollars in research funding every year from all sorts of donors. And if you\u2019re a freshman, we\u2019ll be eager to get you into the game right off the bat. Because, like we said, it\u2019s a priority here. Learn How Innovation + Entrepreneurship Business is a big deal for us. In particular, entrepreneurship. In fact, through our Bill L. Harbert Institute for Innovation and Entrepreneurship and our UAB Blazer Innovation Challenge, we\u2019ve helped launch many student-led start-ups. Maybe yours will be next. Get Started Diversity If we haven\u2019t mentioned our multiple Diversity Championship awards from INSIGHT into Diversity magazine in the last 15 minutes, well, then we\u2019re due to bring it up. We\u2019re extremely proud to be one of the most diverse universities in America. And we\u2019re always eager to talk about it. See for Yourself Career Outcomes + Internships One of our greatest advantages here at UAB is our proximity to so many businesses. Big corporations. Small operations. Innovative start-ups. We\u2019ve got a little bit of everything here in Birmingham. And that means lots of opportunity for you. Plan Ahead Affordability At UAB, we strive to offer a great education for an affordable price. We have many ways to help you pay for school. From grants to scholarships to financial aid and more, we can help you find ways to pay for your education.
