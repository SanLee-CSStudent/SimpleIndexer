Through a unique partnership between UAB and the Alabama Department of Rehabilitation Services (ADRS), the AWARE Program assists current employees and their departments, job seekers going through the application process, and employees returning from long-term disability or medical leave. In addition, the AWARE Program coordinates requests for reasonable accommodations under the Americans with Disabilities Act. Current AWARE services include: Disability assistance for employees and their departments Vocational evaluations and rehabilitation counseling Advice on ADA reasonable accommodations On-site workplace assessments by trained rehabilitation engineers Coordination of medical information and return-to-work documentation Recruitment of qualified applicants with disabilities Disability awareness and sensitivity training Transfer assistance when appropriate UAB is an Affirmative Action\/Equal Opportunity Employer and participates in E-Verify. ?
