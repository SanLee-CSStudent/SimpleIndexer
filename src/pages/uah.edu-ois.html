Through its three support entities \u2013 the English as a Second Language Program (ESL), the Office of Study Abroad, and the Office of International Student & Scholar Services \u2013 the OIS promotes campus and community internationalization; administers the university\u2019s study-abroad and international exchange programs; oversees the recruitment, admission, and retention of international students, scholars, and faculty; provides intensive English language instruction; and facilitates the process of hiring international scholars and employees.
