The Employee of the Month\/ Year Program was recognized as the highest honor reserved for staff at UAB. The program was paused for revitalization in 2019 and is being rebranded and relaunched in January 2021 as the UAB Values in Action Program (UAB VIP Award). Nominations The UAB VIP Award will be presented quarterly to three employees whose work has exemplified and embodied one or more of the values that propel our institution. All nominations must highlight at least one of the UAB Shared Values and explain how the nominee has demonstrated that value. The nomination must clearly demonstrate that the nominee meets the selection criteria and eligibility requirements. A selection committee comprised of individuals from across the campus or hospital shall review the nominations and will select three honorees for the UAB VIP Award. President\u2019s Award for Excellence in Shared Values One of the 12 UAB VIP Award honorees, who has consistently demonstrated UAB Shared Values in an outstanding manner, with a positive impact to the UAB Community, will be selected by the UAB President for the annual President\u2019s Award for Excellence in Shared Values. Criteria for Selection Diversity, Equity and Inclusion Recognition of Award Recipients Criteria for Selection The award criteria include the following: Non-faculty employees employed with UAB. Must be in good standing with the university. Consistently demonstrates a strong commitment to one or more of UAB\u2019s Shared Values in the course of their work. Diversity, Equity and Inclusion The selection committee for the UAB VIP Award program is committed to Diversity, Equity and Inclusion in the review and selection of recipients for this award. We strive to make sure everybody counts, every day, and that we actively seek varied perspectives in our decision-making, including when recognizing our staff.
