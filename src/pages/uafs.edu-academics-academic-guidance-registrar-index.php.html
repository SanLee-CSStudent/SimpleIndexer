Box 3649 Fort Smith, AR 72913-3649 Welcome to the Office of the Registrar Our office is your source for all things related to your academic record. Whether you are requesting an official transcript, getting your Lions ID, or preparing to walk the stage at commencement - the registrar's team is here for you. Our office suite is located on the second floor of the Smith-Pendergraft Campus Center. While most of our services are available online, our front desk is open during the posted business hours. Detail of Office Responsibilities The Registrar's Office is responsible for enrollment verification, maintaining grades and academic records, transfer credit articulation, degree audits, degree awarding, FERPA compliance, and transcript services. These responsibilities, assignments, and services are completed within institutional policies and standards as well as related state and federal laws and regulations. Contact Information For questions about registration, transcripts, and enrollment verification, contact Registrar@uafs.edu. For questions about graduations and diplomas, contact Graduation@uafs.edu. For questions about transfer credit, contact Transfer@uafs.edu.
