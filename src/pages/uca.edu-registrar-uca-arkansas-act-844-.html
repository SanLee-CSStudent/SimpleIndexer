The student\u2019s exemption shall not have expired or shall have been renewed. AND satisfies the following requirements: Resided in Arkansas for at least three (3) years at the time of application to UCA; and Either: (i) Graduated from a public or private Arkansas high school; or (ii) Received a high school equivalency diploma in Arkansas. If you are a current UCA student who meets these guidelines, please submit the following items to the Office of the Registrar, Harrin Hall 224: Copy of one of the following: a) Federal Form I-766 issued to the student or the parent of the student; b) unexpired passport from the Republic of Marshall Islands; or c) unexpired Federal Form I-766 with the annotated category \u201CC33\u201D Copy of your UCA ID card If you are a transfer student who meets these guideline, please submit the following items to the Office of the Registrar, Harrin Hall 224. Copy of one of the following: a) Federal Form I-766 issued to the student or the parent of the student; b) unexpired passport from the Republic of Marshall Islands; or c) unexpired Federal Form I-766 with the annotated category \u201CC33\u201D Copy of your UCA ID card Copy of your Arkansas high school transcript or Arkansas high school equivalency diploma.
