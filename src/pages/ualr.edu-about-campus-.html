Little Rock University of Arkansas at Little Rock Search: About Us Our Campus \u2013 Maps and Parking Welcome to the University of Arkansas at Little Rock. Our campus is where students gather to learn, study, play, and interact. Stretching over 250 tree-covered acres in Little Rock\u2019s midtown area, the main campus is home to more than 50 buildings, including many with LEED designations for their energy efficiency.
