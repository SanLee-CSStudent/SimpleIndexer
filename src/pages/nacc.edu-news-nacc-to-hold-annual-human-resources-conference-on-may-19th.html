News News A- A+ NACC to Hold Annual Human Resources Conference on May 19th May 03, 2022 Northeast Alabama Community College (NACC) will hold its 16th annual Human Resources Conference on Thursday, May 19th, 2022. The conference will be held in the college\u2019s Health Education Building. Attend this conference for a chance to meet and network with other local professionals. Conference attendees will also enjoy a day filled with informative presentations from local industry and community leaders. \"NACC has been limited to the events we held on campus over the last couple years due to COVID-19. However, we are excited to be able to provide the 16th Annual NACC Human Resources Conference to our business and industry leaders this year,\u201D said NACC\u2019s Work Experience Coordinator Melissa Ledbetter. \u201CObtaining professional development hours while networking with colleagues is one of the best ways to learn of additional resources in our community. What better way than to do all of this than spend a day on our beautiful campus at NACC!\" NACC\u2019s Human Resources Conference is made possible in partnership with Alabama Career Center Systems, DeKalb and Jackson Counties Economic Development Authorities, Fort Payne Chamber of Commerce, and the Mountain Lakes Chamber of Commerce. Conference registration is open now, and attendees are encouraged to confirm a spot by May 9th. Registration is available online at www.nacc.edu\/programs-of-study\/skills-training\/non-credit-workshops. The cost to register for the conference is $125. A continental breakfast and lunch will be provided. Northeast Alabama Community College 138 AL Hwy 35, Rainsville, Alabama 35986 | P.O.
