The college offers both technical certification and associate\u2019s degrees and collaborates with other colleges and universities to offer bachelor\u2019s and master\u2019s degrees. Accredited by the Higher Learning Commission of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools, CCCUA is the only community college in the state supported by sales taxes in three separate counties \u2013 Sevier, Howard and Little River. Points of Pride CCCUA offers four associate\u2019s degrees completely online and has a roster of more than 60 online courses. The college won the \u201CExcellence in Distance Education Programming Award\u201D made by the Arkansas Distance Learning Association (ARDLA) in 2001 and 2002. This year\u2019s graduates from the CCCUA Registered Nurses program had a 100 percent pass rate on the National Council Licensure Examination-Registered Nurse (NCLEX RN) test. CCCUA just opened a community health center, one of the first of its kind in the state, with several community partnerships.
