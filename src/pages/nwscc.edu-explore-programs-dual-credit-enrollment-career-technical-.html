More Info Explore Programs All Programs Adult Education Career Technical Programs Dual Enrollment for Dual Credit Academic Dual Enrollment Career Technical Health Studies Special Programs University Transfer Programs Virtual Learning Workforce Training Career Technical Career Technical Dual Enrollment If you are a high school student in a partnering school system, have a minimum of a 2.0 grade point average and are interested in one of the areas listed below, then career-technical dual enrollment may be for you. Technical programs are covered by a scholarship fund and are tuition-free for students. Books and\/or lab fees may be required and are the responsibility of the student. Programs offered include: \u2022 Automotive Technology \u2022 Emergency Medical Technician \u2022 Electrical \u2013 Child Development \u2022 Computer Information \u2022 Design Engineering Technology (Drafting) \u2022 Machine Tool \u2022 Welding Technology To Apply Complete the online application for admission and review the student I.D. policy requirement. Submit your I.D. and\/or transcript, including ACT, SAT, or ACUPLACER scores using the Admissions Documentation Upload. Submit the online Dual Enrollment Registration Form. ***Programs vary by campus and may have additional student eligibility requirements. For More Information, please contact: Casey J.
