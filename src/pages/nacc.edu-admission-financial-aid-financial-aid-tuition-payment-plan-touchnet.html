Tuition Payment Plan (Touchnet) Admission & Financial Aid A- A+ Financial Aid Financial Aid Forms Refund Policy Scholarships Tuition Payment Plan (Touchnet) Veterans Benefits Financial Aid FAQ Consumer Information Financial Aid Policies Direct Student Loans Admissions Class Schedule Catalog Tuition Payment Plan (Touchnet) NACC is proud to offer a tuition payment plan that allows you to pay your tuition and fees over time, making college more affordable! Below you will find information regarding our payment plan. Please contact Mrs. Barbara Cook in Financial Aid with any questions regarding signup\/payment (ext. 2344). STUDENTS CAN ENROLL IN THE SUMMER PAYMENT PLAN BEGINNING April 4, 2022. Payment Methods: Automatic Bank Payments (ACH) Credit card\/Debit card Monthly installments are automatically drafted\/charged using your payment of choice. ***It is the student\u2019s responsibility to update any payment methods which become no longer valid. Payment Plan details: $40 enrollment fee $25 late payment fee $30 returned payment fee ***Our newly improved student portal pulls all allowable charges when setting up your payment plan. Please note, if there are any changes made to your tuition\/fee amount, your plan will be updated to reflect the changes. This means your monthly installment amounts could increase\/decrease depending on the changes made (including Financial Aid added to your account). It is the student\u2019s responsibility to remain current on their monthly installments. Simple Steps to Enroll: Log onto your myNACC portal (link at top of NACC website) Click Banner SSB on \"My Apps\" dashboard Log in again Click Student Click Student Landing Page Click \"Make Payment, Deposit, & Payment Plan\" Once you are redirected to the Touchnet portal, click on \u201CPayment Plans\u201D on the top header You should then see the \u201CEnroll Now\u201D button Depending on the date you enroll, determines which Payment Plan Tier you are eligible for. Please see the schedule listed at the top to determine which tier you fall into. ***The system will determine the payment plan you qualify for automatically based on the date.
