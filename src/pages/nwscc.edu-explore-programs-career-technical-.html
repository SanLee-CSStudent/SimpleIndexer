These courses are tailored to employment needs of area businesses and industries. Career Technical programs strive to provide meaningful educational opportunities appropriate to the needs of students in relation to their futures in the workforce and to develop and cultivate individual talents. NW-SCC offers students the ability to earn an Associate in Occupational Technology degree, career certificate and short term certificate.
