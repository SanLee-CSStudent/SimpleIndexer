All daily lockers need to empty at the end of each day. All personal locks left on the daily use lockers overnight will be cut and the content will be stored for one week in Lost & Found. UAB University Recreation also offers half and full lockers that can be rented on a 3 month and 12 month term. Depending on what plan you choose, your locker will expire on the last day of that month. For example, if you purchase a 3 month locker on August 10, your locker will expire on November 30. Lockers are non-refundable, and non-transferable.
