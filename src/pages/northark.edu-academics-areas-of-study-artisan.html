About The Program A new topic, Professional Seminar, will be offered using Northark\u2019s existing one-credit Special Topics course (SPTC 2791). This topic will provide an overview of entrepreneurship. Presenters who are successful business owners or leaders in their fields will be invited to speak to the class and interact with students. Mentorship will be encouraged. Students will develop portfolios of their work and will design marketing strategies. Paying for College Northark is affordable!
