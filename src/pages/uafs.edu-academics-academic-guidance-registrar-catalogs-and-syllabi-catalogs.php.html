Registrar's Office Registrar@uafs.edu 479-788-7230 Smith-Pendergraft Campus Center Academic Catalogs This catalog will help you unlock the door to your future and prepare you for success both in your career and life. The University of Arkansas - Fort Smith is a place of ideas with a focus on movement. We've designed our courses not only to provide you with the knowledge you'll need to accomplish your goals, but also to challenge you to think critically and creatively. The programs outlined within this catalog reflect the very best of the university, from our dedication to 21st-century scholarship, to combining learning in the classroom with real-world experience, to academic excellence. UAFS students are expected to carefully study the catalog to become familiar with all policies, procedures, and regulations. Knowledge of the information contained within the catalog is the responsibility of each student. The provisions of the catalog are subject to change and should be considered to be for informational purposes rather than as an irrevocable contract between the university and the student. If you are searching for an archived catalog not listed below please contact our office.
