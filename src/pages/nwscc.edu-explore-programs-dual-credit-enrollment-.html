More Info Explore Programs All Programs Adult Education Career Technical Programs Dual Enrollment for Dual Credit Academic Dual Enrollment Career Technical Health Studies Special Programs University Transfer Programs Virtual Learning Workforce Training Dual Enrollment for Dual Credit The Dual Enrollment for Dual Credit Program (DE\/DC) program at Northwest-Shoals Community College allows eligible students to earn both high school and college credit. College courses count toward high school graduation credits, and the courses remain part of a student\u2019s regular college transcript. Are you eligible? Eligible high school students must meet the following criteria: Meet the entrance requirements of postsecondary education. Be in grade 10, 11, or 12. Have a minimum cumulative (unweighted) 2.5 on a 4.0 scale for initial admission in completed high school courses. Obtain written approval from the appropriate principal or counselor at the high school. Continuous Eligibility To maintain continuous eligibility, student must earn a \u201CC\u201D or better in all attempted college courses. Students who fail to meet this minimum GPA or withdraw from a course will be suspended from the program for a minimum one term. The one-term suspension may not be served during the summer. What Courses Can I Take? NW-SCC offers Academic or Career Technical Courses in a Traditional or Distance Education format. Meet with your high school counselor to select the best option for you. Students are required to meet the appropriate prerequisites prior to enrollment in specific courses. Prerequisites include but are not limited to ACT or SAT score minimums, placement testing guidelines, and\/or sequential course requirements. Academic Dual Enrollment Career Technical Dual Enrollment Benefits COST SAVINGS \u2013 Tuition and fees at NW-SCC are significantly less than those of four year colleges. Students taking an on-campus class may apply for a $100 scholarship per semester. SIMULTANEOUS CREDIT \u2013 Student can earn academic\/technical credit for both high school graduation and a college degree.. EDUCATIONAL ADVANTAGE \u2013 Get a jump start on your college career while easing the transition from high school to college. To Apply Complete the online application for admission and review the student I.D. policyrequirement.
