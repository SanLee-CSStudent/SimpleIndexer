Rock University of Arkansas at Little Rock Search: Student Records Transcript Request Current and Former Students (Net Id Required) No Net ID or Unknown ID (Self Register) Requests for official academic transcripts should be submitted online through Parchment Credential Services. Online requests are made behind a secure log in, using your NetID. An $8 fee, plus any relevant shipping or special handling fees, per transcript is required at the time of the request. Incomplete requests or requests without payment cannot be processed. Any obligations to the university must be met prior to your transcript request submission. Transcript requests are not refundable, and cannot be cancelled. You have 17 days to download your transcript document before it expires. You can download the document up to 3 times. If you do not download your transcript document within 17 days, you will need to place a new order for your transcript and pay another charge to receive it. Current and former William H. Bowen School of Law students must make transcript requests through the Law School. The UA Little Rock Office of Records and Registration will not print any coursework from the William H. Bowen School of Law. Official Online Requests Current and Former Students (Net Id Required) No Net ID or Unknown ID (Self Register) Students that do not remember their BOSS login information can click on the following link for Parchment \u2013 Parchment Credential Services \u2013 Self-Register, create an account with Parchment and order a transcript through this service. This service will also instruct the student to pay for the transcript to complete the process. Current and previous students who have access to BOSS may request their official transcripts online by going through myUALR or BOSS directly. This will require your Net ID and Password and your User ID number and PIN, respectively. Online transcript requests are fulfilled through our partner vendor, Parchment Credential Services. Special Requests\/Attachments The Hold for Current Semester Grades option on the transcript request allows a student submit a transcript request early, but not have the transcript mailed or processed until the end of the current semester or part of term when final grades are posted. The Hold for Degree Notation option on the transcript request allows a student to submit a transcript request early, but not have the transcript mailed or processed until the awarding of your degree(s). Please note. The Hold for Current Semester Grades option should not be used after the date grades are posted for the semester. This may result in your transcript being held until the end of the subsequent semester. This function is only applicable for currently enrolled students. High School Concurrent students will not be able to use this option. Attachments Transcript requests that require a form to be included with the transcript for application services such as LSAC, AMCAS, PharmCAS, NursingCAS or other professional organizations should be submitted at the time of the request. Attachments not received at the time of request may result in an additional transcript fee. Unofficial Transcripts Unofficial transcripts can be viewed online via your BOSS Account.
