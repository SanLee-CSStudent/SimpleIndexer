Math Bootcamps Join a 1-week FREE Math Boot Camp this summer. \u2022 Be better prepared for college-level math \u2022 Attend online synchronous live sessions from anywhere \u2022 Learn about online technology used at CAC \u2022 Repeat the camp as many times as you would like \u2022 Make new friends and meet CAC Math Instructors \u2022 Attend STEM and non-STEM breakout sessions \u2022 Pass your math class at a higher rate than students that do not attend a math boot camp Offered: Monday 9- 3 pm; Tuesday -Thursday 9 am \u2013 1 pm 6\/7\/21 \u2013 6\/10\/21 6\/14\/21-6\/17\/21 6\/21\/21-6\/24\/21 Monday-Thursday 9 am \u2013 3pm 7\/26\/21-7\/29\/21 8\/9\/21-8\/12\/21 To register for the course Click Here Writing Academies CAC English faculty invite you to join the 2-week Writing Academy. Enroll in the Writing Academy if: \u2022 you want to brush up on your writing skills \u2022 you want to practice writing at the college level \u2022 you want to save time and money by taking this course instead of ENG 090 or ENG 100 in the fall \u2022 you are taking ENG 101 this summer and need a little extra support \u2022 you are a high school student \u2022 you are a current college student Offered: Online 6\/1\/20 \u2013 6\/15\/20 Course Number and Section: ENGBC001, 20SU2951 Questions: Contact Mark Silvia at mark.silvia@centralaz.edu. If you need assistance in course selection, contact an online advisor. To register for the course, access the fillable form and email it to admissions@centralaz.edu.
