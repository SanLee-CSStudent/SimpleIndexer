Apprentices enrolled in a registered apprenticeship, participate in a proven model of job preparation that combines paid on-the-job learning with related instruction to progressively increase workers\u2019 skill levels and wages. Calhoun Community College serves as both a registered apprenticeship sponsor and related technical instruction (RTI) provider and works closely with the Alabama Office of Apprenticeship to assist students and employers through the registered apprenticeship process. Five components of all Registered Apprenticeships: Business-driven On the job learning (OJL) Related technical instruction (RTI) Rewards for skills gains Nationally recognized credential Benefits for Employers: Fill company\u2019s skills gap Increase employee retention Reduce recruiting costs Diversify your workforce Take advantage of potential tax incentives Message us to learn more about registering your program Benefits for Students: Career progression Paid work experience in your field of study Earn a college degree alongside industry credentials Graduate with no college debt Learn from an industry mentor Learn more about becoming an apprentice 1-800-626-3628 Toll-Free in Alabama Mailing Address P.O.
