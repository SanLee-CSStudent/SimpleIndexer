Requirements & Deadlines Requirements for Completing a Graduate Degree Deadlines for Application for Degree or Graduate-Level Certificate Application for Graduate Degree\/Certificate \u202F(available within BlazerNET) Regarding Your Faculty Chair (Doctoral Candidates) Each doctoral candidate should ask his or her dissertation committee chair or a committee member to participate in the hooding ceremony. If no one from your committee is available to serve in this capacity, the person whom you ask to assist in the hooding must be a faculty member in your program. You will need to identify the faculty member who will hood you to confirm his\/her availability. Faculty should inform the Graduate School of their plans to attend and to provide the names of any doctoral candidates they intend to hood. Receiving Your Degree Upon completion of your degree requirements and the absence of financial holds, diplomas are mailed approximately within 5 weeks after the graduation date to the address listed on your Application for Degree form. After degree requirements are completed, degrees are posted to student academic transcripts within two weeks. Change of Address To change your diploma mailing address, you must notify the UAB Graduate School either by completing the\u202FGraduation Change of Address form. If the Graduate School does not receive notification of your change of address per this form, you will be charged a duplicate diploma fee.
