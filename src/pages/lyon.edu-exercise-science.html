Study Exercise Science at Lyon College! The program offers two tracks: one track for students interested in entering the health profession as physical therapists or occupational therapists and a second track for students interested in fitness and wellness as a career. The courses cover human anatomy, exercise physiology, kinesiology, and the application of those sciences to help people improve their health. Lyon offers both a major and a minor in exercise science. % of Lyon graduates are employed or in grad school within 6 months of graduation :1 student-to-teacher ratio Meet the Faculty Details Courses Careers Admissions Internships Scholarships Why Exercise Science at Lyon? The Exercise Science Program offers an academic major with two tracks, each with a challenging curriculum. The Health and Wellness Track leads to a Bachelor of Arts degree, while the Health Professions track leads to the Bachelor of Science degree. The Exercise Science curriculum promotes critical thinking, encourages the development of leadership skills, and prepares students for careers in and continued study of the application of the principles of human movement, exercise, and healthy living. For more information on the required courses for an Exercise Science major or minor, view our courses page. Exercise Science Classes: BIO 260 Human Anatomy and Physiology I BIO 262 Human Anatomy and Physiology II EXS 201 Health & Wellness EXS 205 Nutrition Concepts for Exercise Science EXS 207 First Aid and Care of Athletic Injuries EXS 302 Kinesiology EXS 309 Motor Learning EXS 325 Fitness Assessment and Exercise Prescription EXS 375 Exercise Physiology EXS 382 Special Topic EXS 450 Senior Seminar in Exercise Science EXS 480 Senior Capstone Careers for Exercise Science Majors Students who major in exercise science can enter healthcare professions, such as physical and occupational therapists or kinesiologists. They can also pursue careers in fitness and wellness as personal trainers, sports nutritionists, athletic directors, and more. Available careers include: Physician Assistant (PA) Physical Therapist (PT) Occupational Therapist (OT) Chiropractor Clinical Exercise Physiologist Cardiac Rehab Pulmonary Rehab Mid-Career Median U.S. Salaries for Exercise Science Major Physical Therapist- $70,000 Physician Assistant- $101,000 Strength and Conditioning Coach- $38,000 Wellness Coordinator- $44,000 Exercise Physiologist- $43,000 For more information on admissions to Lyon College, visit our Admissions page.
