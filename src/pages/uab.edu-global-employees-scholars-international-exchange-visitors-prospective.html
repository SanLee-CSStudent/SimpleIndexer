Looking to come to UAB? The J-1 visa\/immigration status is appropriate for a specifically-defined research or training opportunity sponsored by a specific UAB department at a specific site for a specific period of time and does not allow employment, research, or training beyond that defined in your offer\/invitation letter from UAB. Please note that the Department of State describes the minimum qualifications for a Research Scholar as follows: \"Minimum qualifications for this category are a bachelor\u2019s degree with appropriate experience in the field of which research is to be conducted\" [9 FAM 402.5-6(E)(8)]. The same is expected for Short-Term Scholars and Professors. A master\u2019s or terminal degree in your field is strongly preferred at UAB.
