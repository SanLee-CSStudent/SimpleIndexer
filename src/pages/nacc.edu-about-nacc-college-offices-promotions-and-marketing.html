Northeast provides information on its many educational offerings and services through print, broadcast, and electronic media. NEWS RELEASES: Employees with college news that should be sent to the appropriate media outlets should contact the Director of Promotions and Marketing, Mrs. Meg Nevels Nippers, by email at nippersm@nacc.edu. Her telephone extension is 2210. Her office is located in Room 120 in the Charles M. Pendley Administration Building. COLLEGE BRANDING: The NACC Branding Portal lists the official specifications for printed materials that represent the college. The portal provides the college colors, fonts, typography, logos, other graphic elements, and guidelines for implementation: Employees should consult the portal to ensure consistency and high quality in college printed materials. Questions about branding should be addressed to the Director of Promotions and Marketing. RESPONSIBILITIES: The Director of the Office of Promotions and Marketing\u2019s responsibilities include but are not limited to the following: Media Relations Press release writing and distribution Social Media management Ad design Billboard design and locations Design and publication of the NACC Catalog\/Student Handbook and class schedule Television, cable, radio, and digital commercials General photography NACC commencement program design and publication Mrs. Nippers is a member of the Alabama Community College System Public Relations Association (ACCSPRA), Public Relations Society of America (PRSA), and the National Council for Marketing and Public Relations (NCMPR).
