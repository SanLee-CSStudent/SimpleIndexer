The Backpack is a free email newsletter sponsored by the Office of the Dean of Students and Parent and Family Services. The main goal of this newsletter is to help you support your student during their time on campus. The Backpack is published bi-weekly to keep families up to date with the university and what to be aware of for their student in the first few years.
