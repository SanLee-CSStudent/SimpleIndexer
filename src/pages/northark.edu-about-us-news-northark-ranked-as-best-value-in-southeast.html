Hammerschmidt Lecture Series Reports and Policies Vendor Information Northark ranked as Best Value in Southeast by Northark Office of Public Relations Wed May 5, 2021 at 09:12 AM North Arkansas College (Northark) Transportation & Materials Moving program was named as the \u201CBest Value in the Southeast Region\u201D by Trade College Search in the 2021 Best Value Trade Colleges Rankings. Four other Northark programs topped the list as the \u201CBest Value\u201D in Arkansas: 1st Best Value Clinical\/Medical Laboratory Science Associates Degree Schools in Arkansas 1st Best Value Precision Production Schools in Arkansas 1st in Best Value Transportation & Materials Moving Undergraduate Certificate Schools in Arkansas 1st in Best Value Precision Production Basic Certificate Schools in Arkansas \u201CNorthark has put an emphasis on increasing the number of skilled workers in response to employer needs,\u201D commented Randy Esters, president. \u201CThe programs that are at the top show that we are maintaining academic rigor in hands-on training to give the student an excellent return on their investment of time and tuition. Northark has listened and followed the advice of our community partners and that benefits us all.\u201D The rankings are designed to assist students in sorting through the best options for them. Rankings are developed with a high emphasis on the school\u2019s quality combined with affordability playing a significant factor. Northark offers 37 \u201CCareer in a Year\u201D career programs that can be completed in under 12 months. These are designed to get people trained and into the workforce quickly and without spending a lot of money. Northark is a recognized leader with a focused mission to offer a high-quality and affordable education to students. The College has not raised tuition or fees in 2021 and is waiving online fees for 2021 and 2022 academic years.
