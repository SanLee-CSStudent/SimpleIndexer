A. in Cultural Heritage Studies UAB's Cultural Heritage Studies program\u2014the only professional degree of its kind in the state of Alabama\u2014provides students with an understanding of the relationship between culture, knowledge, and power. The program prepares students to understand, interpret, and promote a broad range of cultural activities, with an emphasis on art and architecture. Students train in heritage interpretation and outreach, promoting community engagement in the process. They develop skills and values pertinent to public history, digital history, museum studies, cultural resource management, and heritage tourism. Details Graduates of the program will obtain a broad foundation of knowledge applicable to careers and graduate study in historic preservation and museum studies; arts management and administration; cultural and public advocacy; art or cultural policy; law and transnational justice; international affairs, diplomacy, government, and foreign service; peace and justice studies; and other fields. You can be part of the important and increasingly prominent discourse on cultural heritage.
