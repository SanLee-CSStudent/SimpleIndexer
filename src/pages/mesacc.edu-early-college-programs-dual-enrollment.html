Dual Enrollment Registration Guide (PDF) **If you are a returning Dual Enrollment student and have completed the Student Admission Application, please go directly to Step 3 to begin the registration process.** Step 1 \u2013 Get admitted to MCC by completing the Student Admissions Application You will be required to submit ID Authentication in this step before you proceed with registration. Upon completion of Step 1, you should have an MCC ID number, an MEID, and password. Return to this website to complete the registration steps once you have your MEID, Student ID number and password created. If you are a returning dual enrollment student, you will need to have your MEID and password in order to proceed to Step 2 and Step 3 below. Step 2 \u2013 Determine prerequisites and\/or testing requirements by viewing the Dual Enrollment High School Partners and Classes Click on your high school and then select \"see available classes.\" Identify the course in which you plan to enroll and ensure you meet the admissions requirements listed on the course list. If you do not meet the course prerequisite, you may need to complete an Ed Ready Placement Exam online. If a placement test is required, test scores will be uploaded 24-48 hours after completion of your test. Once scores are uploaded you may continue with the enrollment process. If you attend a Mesa Public School high school, MCC advisors are available to answer questions. All other students may chat with an Early College representative. Step 3 \u2013 Submit online registration request for your dual enrollment course For assistance, refer to the video guide below: Returning Dual Enrollment Student Online Registration Form (returning students are defined as those who have previously enrolled in a MCC dual enrollment course) First-Time Dual Enrollment Student Online Registration Form (new dual enrollment and\/or new to MCC dual enrollment students) Have all test scores, ID authentication documents, and a parent\/guardian with you when you complete the registration request. It may take staff 5-7 business days to process the registration request. Once the form has been processed, communication will be sent to your Student MCC email address. Step 4 \u2013 Pay tuition and fees Once you have received a confirmation of your enrollment (sent to your student email address 7-10 business days from submission), you will need to make your tuition payment. Failure to make tuition payment in full may result in your being withdrawn from dual enrollment. Pay online at my.maricopa.edu, over the phone (480-461-7600), and\/or student tuition payment plan.
