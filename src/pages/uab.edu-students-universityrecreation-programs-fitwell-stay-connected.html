Discord Interested in joining the greater URec community online? Join our community discord! Here you can chat with others in the UAB fitness community, share photos and successes, ask questions you may have about our programs and get updates on them as well. This is your one stop shop to get all the goings on at URec. Discord is still a work in progress and other areas may be added soon, but for now the fitness community channel is up and running. So join in! When you join the server please be sure to read the rules and accept it to gain membership role, then select fitness in your role selection to gain access to those channels!
