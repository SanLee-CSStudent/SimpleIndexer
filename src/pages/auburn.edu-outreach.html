The AuburnServes network encourages students to take an active role in fulfilling the University mission by volunteering and getting involved with local organizations. Key programs include: Community Partner Training and the Virtual Volunteer Fair. The Center for Educational Outreach and Engagement (CEOE) establishes and participates in K-12 programs with underrepresented students, schools and communities. Key programs include: CultureBump¢â Training and GEARUP Alabama. The Encyclopedia of Alabama (EOA) is a free, online reference resource on Alabama's history, culture, geography, and natural environment. EOA is an outreach initiative of AU, working in collaboration with a number of state agencies, educational institutions and historical\/humanities organizations across Alabama. The Office of Faculty Engagement provides campus-wide support, advocacy, and leadership in the promotion of outreach and community engagement at AU. Key programs include: Award for Excellence in Faculty Outreach, the Faculty Outreach and Engagement Council and the 2021 Faculty Engagement Survey. The Government and Economic Development Institute (GEDI) promotes effective government policy and management, civic engagement, and economic prosperity. Key programs include: EDAA Leadership Institute and the Alabama Local Government Training Institute (ALGTI). The Office of Professional and Continuing Education (OPCE) makes the educational resources of AU available for non-credit education programs and conferences designed to promote lifelong learning. Key programs include: Auburn Youth Programs and the EagleCast Webinar Series. The Office of Public Service (OPS) promotes faculty, student and staff engagement through the development of community and institutional partnerships. Key programs include: the AuburnServes Network, Campus Kitchens Project, and Webinar Training Courses. The Osher Lifelong Learning Institute (OLLI) offers member-centered courses, hands-on learning experiences, and social interaction for people over 50. Experienced volunteer instructors lead affordably priced courses in a variety of subjects and settings. Join OLLI today! Outreach Global is creating opportunities for global engagement and contribution towards community development across the globe! Key programs include: the Community Festival Day, the Ghana Healthcare Program and the Ghana Service Learning Program. Outreach Information Technology and CEU Office provides support for all departments within University Outreach. Key functions include: Help Tickets, Access Request Forms, and CEU Certification, Reporting, & Non-Credit Transcripts.
