All of our rooms are double occupancy (two students per room), however, on occasion and as needed, we reserve the right to triple rooms. Regarding the single occupancy, please call us at 520-494-5470. Each room is furnished with a desk, chair, closet space, dresser, and bed (with an extra-long twin mattress) for each student. One MicroFridge unit is available in each student room. **No outside Refrigerators and microwaves are permitted Room and Meal Prices Room price is $1500 per student, per semester. Meal Plan price is $2450 per semester. Single Room price is $2850 per semester. Room Deposit Room Deposit Update Central Arizona College does not require a room deposit. Community Living All students may choose the type of community they would like to live in from our various options. Please indicate the housing option you are requesting to be assigned for the fall semester. Final community and room assignments are made by the Director of Residence Life. Quad: Each quad houses up to eight residents and consists of four rooms, two bathrooms, and one community room. Each student room is furnished with two beds, two dressers, and two desks. One MicroFridge unit is available in each student room. Two double occupancy rooms share one bathroom and all residents will have access to the quad community room. The final room assignment is determined by the Director of Residence Life. Early requests are encouraged as space is limited. Suite: The room is accessible from the outside and conjoined with another room through a Jack and Jill bathroom. Each room houses two students. Each student room is furnished with two beds, two dressers, and two desks. One MicroFridge unit is available in each student room. The final room assignment is determined by the Director of Residence Life. Early requests are encouraged as space is limited. Tower: A traditional three-story co-ed residence hall with rooms accessible through the inside hallway. The rooms are conjoined by a Jack and Jill bathroom. The hallways are co-ed with rooms that share a bathroom being single gender. Each student room is furnished with two beds, two dressers, and two desks. One MicroFridge unit is available in each student room. The final room assignment is determined by the Director of Residence Life. Early requests are encouraged as space is limited. Students may be assigned a roommate or search our resident directory and make their own roommate match! Browsing the directory and creating a roommate group is all done as part of our online application system in eRezLife! Meal Plan All residents are required to have a meal plan. The meal plans are currently priced at $2450 per semester. Students will be provided a $2450.00 balance to be used in the Dining Hall. All funds are non-transferable, non-refundable, have no cash value, and can only be used at The Peak dining hall and The Point cafe. All funds expire at the end of each semester.
