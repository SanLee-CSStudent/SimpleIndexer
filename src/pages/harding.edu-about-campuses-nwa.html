Our Northwest Arkansas location \u2014 an extension of Harding University \u2014 is located off Interstate 49 in Rogers, Arkansas, within the Ozark mountains. This area includes four of the state\u2019s largest cities and is home to three Fortune 500 companies. Harding University is committed to the development of Christian scholarship and ethics and the promotion of citizenship with a global perspective. Our programs will equip you with the knowledge and skills you need to enter and succeed in your career, integrating faith-filled learning and living along the way. College of Education Programs Part of the Cannon-Clary College of Education, we offer specialized programs in degree completion (Bachelor of Arts in education) plus master\u2019s, specialist and doctoral degrees. Degree Completion in Education: Complete a two-year program and earn a bachelor\u2019s degree to teach Birth to Kindergarten\/Special Education Integrated, K-6 Elementary Education, or 4-8 Middle Level Education after completing an associate\u2019s degree and\/or the required prerequisite coursework. Online classes, reduced tuition and financial aid are available. Graduate Studies in Education: Become a teacher and further your professional development and career opportunities through graduate programs in education. Depending on the program, courses are designed to coordinate the scheduling needs of professionals with online, on-ground or hybrid options. Mental Health & Wellness: Earn an advanced degree with professors who are invested in developing you as a caring and competent counselor. We offer master's and educational specialist degrees, school counseling and licensed professional counseling degrees as well as a certificate and additional licensure opportunities. Prepare to navigate today's challenges and provide hope. For more information contact us at hunwa@harding.edu or 479-268-5812 (Fax: 479-268-5818). You can also fill out the Request Virtual Info Session form. College of Nursing Programs Accelerated Bachelor of Science in Nursing (ABSN): Part of Harding\u2019s Carr College of Nursing, home of the No. 1 nursing program in Arkansas, our ABSN track blends online accelerated coursework and hands-on learning to put you on the path to success as a confident nurse. Harding ABSN students participate in nursing skills and simulation labs multiple days each week at our site in Rogers, Arkansas. For more information contact us at absn@harding.edu.
