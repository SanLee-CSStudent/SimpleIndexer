Student Financial Assistance Assistance Available for Federal, State, Local, and Institutional Programs: Federal Student Aid: NWTI participates in the Federal Pell Grant Program. Students apply for federal financial aid by completing the FAFSA (Free Application for Federal Student Aid) and being eligible for the Federal Pell Grant. Students must make Satisfactory Academic Progress (SAP) each year to continue to receive federal financial aid. State Aid: Students may be eligible for the Arkansas Future Grant from the Arkansas Department of Higher Education to attend NWTI. Please see visit scholarships.adhe.edu to apply and for more information. Institutional Aid: Information on scholarships available to NWTI students can be found here. Student Outcomes Retention Rate: 84% of full time students at Northwest Technical Institute who began their studies in fall 2019 returned in fall 2020 to continue their studies. Graduation Rate: 77% of full time students at Northwest Technical Institute who graduated within 150% of the required time for the 2019\/2020 academic year. Job Placement and Job Placement Rates for Graduates: 82% of students at Northwest Technical Institute who either graduated or did not complete the diploma\/certificate requirements for the 2019\/2020 academic year found jobs within the occupation in which the student was enrolled in. 81% of students at Northwest Technical Institute who graduated in the 2019\/2020 academic year found jobs within their graduating occupation.
