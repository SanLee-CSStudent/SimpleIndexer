If I don\u2019t graduate during the time period I\u2019m initially sent the email for Merit Fellow, do I still need to register or will I have the opportunity to register when my own graduation comes around? No. Recruitment for May\/August graduates is always in July\/August and recruitment for December graduates is always in December\/January, that way you can enter the program within a year\u2019s time to complete the requirements. As long as you still meet the qualifications of credit hours, having a 3.0 or higher, and graduating during the time frame of when your class is recruited, you will receive another invitation to register with your graduating class. 2. How much does it cost to become a Merit Fellow? Participation is completely FREE \u2013 you do not have to pay for the graduation cord. 3. How will I be notified of upcoming events and programs? You will receive weekly emailed newsletters about upcoming events. You can also find and register for all of our upcoming events on our Handshake page. 4. Where can I make a resume review appointment? You can schedule an appointment with one of our staff members by visiting Handshake and selecting on Career Center, then Appointments on your Handshake account. Or by calling 501-450-3134. ** You must send an updated copy of your resume back to the staff member that reviews it in order for your resume review to be considered complete** 5. The program deadline will vary depending on your graduation date: May & August 2022: April 22nd December 2022: November 18th 7. How can I check to see how many activities I have completed? After you attend one of our interest meetings, you will receive a follow up email to your cub account and it will contain your Merit Fellow ID number as well as all of the information that was covered in the interest meetings. 9.
