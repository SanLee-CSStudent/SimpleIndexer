The main UAS campus is located in Auke Bay, 12 miles from downtown Juneau. Housing, campus services, the library, and most classrooms are located in Auke Bay. What's Happening on the Juneau Campus About Juneau Juneau is the state capital and the largest city in southeastern Alaska. The city offers a flourishing downtown business and arts scene, an extensive library system, and public transportation. As Alaska\u2019s capital, Juneau sits at the epicenter of Alaska\u2019s governmental and economic affairs. Visit Choose Juneau and Travel Juneau to learn more.
