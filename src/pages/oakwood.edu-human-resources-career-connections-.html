Oakwood University Career Connections Center (CCC) creates a comprehensive, holistic, integrated pathway leading students to successful entry into graduate school and careers that will enable them to serve God and humanity. The goal is to ensure that students who complete matriculation at Oakwood will be able to enter into graduate or professional school, can be employed as an entrepreneur or as an employee. Or can be profitably engaged in community service in their field within 12 months of graduation.
