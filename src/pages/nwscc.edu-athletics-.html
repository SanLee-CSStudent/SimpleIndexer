More Info Athletics Athletics are back at Northwest-Shoals Community College (NW-SCC)! The College announced in fall of 2021 the reinstatement of athletic programs at the College. Beginning in fall of 2022, NW-SCC will field men\u2019s baseball and women\u2019s softball teams. Athletic competition is an important part of the overall education of students, and NW-SCC is committed to provide that opportunity to our community and area high school student athletes. The exhibition season is scheduled to begin in September 2022 with conference play beginning in March 2023. NW-SCC is currently a member of the Alabama Community College Conference (ACCC) and will compete against various opponents within the ACCC, National Junior College Athletic Association (NJCAA) and the National Collegiate Athletic Association (NCAA). Message from the Patriots' Athletic Director \"This upcoming year promises to be an exciting time for Northwest-Shoals Community College's Patriots Athletics program. I am excited to work with our administration, coaches, community and student-athletes as we return sports to the College. We will strive for championships and making a positive difference in our local community.
