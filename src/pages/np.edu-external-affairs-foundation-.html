The Foundation was established in 1980 to support National Park College in achieving its mission of student success. We\u2019re proud to partner with alumni, local leaders, businesses and organizations, and those who genuinely love the college. Our goal has always been, and always will be, to support our students. We invite you to join us as we make a difference through financial aid packages, academic programming, campus upgrades and growth. Mission Statement National Park College Foundation, Inc. strives to inspire philanthropy as it partners with donors to grow and manage assets that support student success. Vision Statement The National Park College Foundation values excellence in student success as well as access to lifelong learning that will provide resources to enhance the College's ability to sustain its position as a community innovator and leader. Areas of Greatest Need NPC Impact Fund: The cornerstone of NPC, providing unrestricted funding to make the largest impact while fulfilling our mission of student success. NPC General Scholarships: Providing financial assistance to NPC students with less restrictions, offering flexibility and support to those who may not qualify otherwise. NPC Nighthawk Athletics: Supporting Men's and Women's Basketball, Softball, Baseball, Cross Country and Soccer. Visit our Athletics section for more information. Capital Improvement Projects: Brick and Mortar, Technology upgrades, campus improvements. Have you considered National Park College Foundation in your estate plans? To learn more more, contact us. Policy regarding scholarship gifts: Gifts made by an individual to a student for financial assistance in which the recipient has already been identified by the donor will be applied directly to the student's account in the NPC Business Office. The Donor Bill of Rights was created by the Association of Fundraising Professionals (AFP), the Association for Healthcare Philanthropy (AHP), the Council for Advancement and Support of Education (CASE), and the Giving Institute: Leading Consultants to Non-Profits. It has been endorsed by numerous organizations. International Statement of Ethical Principals in Fundraising sets out the values, beliefs, and principles that govern professional fundraisers across the world. It sets out what unites us in the way that we fundraise, and joins us together as a global fundraising community dedicated to achieving fundraising excellence for our causes, donors, and supporters.
