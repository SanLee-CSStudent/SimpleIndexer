High School Art Competition BSC Arts Alliance Department of Art and Art History Arts Calendar The Durbin Gallery Located within the Kennedy Art Center\/Azar Studios complex, the Durbin Gallery provides BSC students, faculty and the broader community with an outstanding resource of visiting, faculty, and student exhibitions. The various traveling exhibitions highlight the works of regionally and nationally recognized artists working in a wide variety of media. All shows are free and open to the public. Regular exhibitions include the work of the art department studio faculty, the Biennial Southeastern High School Art Competition, the BFA senior exhibition. and a competitive Juried Student Exhibition open to all students enrolled in art courses during the course of each school year.
