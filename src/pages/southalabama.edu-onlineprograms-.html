Spotlight: Educator Excellence Scholarship The Educator Excellence Scholarship is for graduate students majoring in a teacher education program, who have previously earned an Alabama Professional Educator certificate. This scholarship is specifically designed for those seeking a Class A or AA certification.* This scholarship equates to 20% of tuition cost each semester for 6 consecutive semesters! Students granted admission to an eligible program automatically qualify and are awarded the scholarship.
