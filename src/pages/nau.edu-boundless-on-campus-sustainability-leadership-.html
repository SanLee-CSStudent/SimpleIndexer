Northern Arizona University\u2019s Printing Services was selected to receive the 2021 AASCU Excellence and Innovation Award for Sustainability and Sustainable Development (innovative project). The Innovative Project Award honors projects that \u201Cdevelop new sustainable products, strategies, and services.\u201D Printing Services initiated their sustainability program in 2019. They successfully designed and implemented numerous projects to encourage sustainability across the campus, including repurposing old banners, using an electric bicycle for deliveries, implementing \u201CGo Green\u201D products, and prioritizing sustainability in print technology.
