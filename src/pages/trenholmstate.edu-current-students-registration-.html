OFFICIAL college transcripts should be sent to our mailing address directly from the prior institution(s). OFFICIAL documents may also be sent electronically if the prior institution(s) provide this type of transcript delivery. We cannot accept faxed or unofficial college transcripts. Students who have achieved a minimum of a Baccalaureate degree are only required to submit a transcript from the granting institution. Electronic transcripts must be emailed through a secure network (such as eScript\/Credentials, Parchment or The National Student Clearinghouse) to admissions@trenholmstate.edu Mailing Address: Office of Admissions P.O. Admissions Webpage Know your Admissions Status! You will be Conditionally or Unconditionally admitted to the College, if otherwise eligible, once this Residency Form and Valid Id are on file. Admission Status Types: There are two types of admission status: Conditional Status: Students who have not submitted all required transcripts and\/or documents may be admitted as conditional status. Conditional students will also be emailed access with instructions to their student email and MyTrenholm student portal. Unconditional Status: Students who have submitted all required documentation may be admitted as unconditional status. Conditional Status Please Note: Conditionally admitted students are allowed to register for classes. However, financial aid cannot be processed until student's admissions status is Unconditional. Failure to provide documentation by the end of the first semester, as determined by local institutional calendars, will prevent a student from future registration and official transcript release. Your admissions status will be emailed to you weekly, along with other important enrollment information. Continue to check your email frequently. Admission inquiries can be directed to admissions@trenholmstate.edu or 334-420-4310. Step 2 Access Student Email and \u201CMyTrenholm\u201D Trenholm uses a single-sign in platform. Therefore, your email login and password need to be accessed BEFORE you can access your \"MyTrenholm Portal or CANVAS. Step 3 Sign Up for Advising VIRTUAL AND ON-CAMPUS OPTIONS All NEW students are ENCOURAGED to meet with their program advisor BEFORE registering for classes for the first time. Program Advisors will assist new students with course selections and introduce them to their degree plan. There are several options and opportunities for Program Advising and Class Registration. Virtual, On-Campus, and By Appointment options are available. BY APPOINTMENT Look up the contact information for your PROGRAM ADVISOR using the FACULTY ADVISOR DIRECTORY. Use the contact information from the directory to call or email your program advisor. Please use your student email account when contacting your advisor. Provide a good phone number and the best time to contact you. Also, check your student email daily for communications from your advisor. Your advisor will make contact by phone or email to advise you on the course selections for the semester, review your DEGREE PLAN USING DEGREE WORKS and answer any questions you may have regarding your program. Advising hours fall between 8:00 a.m. and 4:00 p.m. Monday-Thursday and 8:00 a.m. \u2013 11:30 a.m. on Fridays. However, each faculty member has specific office hours dedicated to helping students. Therefore, please use the contact information provided below to call or email to set up an appointment. When sending an email, always use your Trenholm email account, indicate \u201CAdvising or Registration Request\u201D in the subject line and include your full name, student A- number and a good contact phone number. Your advisor will respond to your email or call during their advising hours. Please allow one business day for all return correspondence. Access your DEGREE PLAN using DEGREE WORKS! Degree Works is a web-based tool to help students and advisors monitor students' progress toward degree completion. Degree Works combines Trenholm State's degree requirements and the coursework completed into a degree plan. Degree Works allows students and faculty advisors to see how completed courses count toward degree completion. Degree Works is designed to aid and facilitate academic and career technical education advising. The degree plans produced by Degree Works are dependent on students' majors. If a major is not identified for a student, choose the \"What-If\" option, and select an anticipated major from the drop-down list to view a complete degree audit. Step 4 Register for Classes After you have contacted your advisor for course selection and degree planning you are now ready to log into your \u201CMyTrenholm\u201D Student Portal and register for classes. Login to \"My Trenholm\" Portal with your Trenholm email address and the same password as your Trenholm email address. Students must enter their Username (Trenholm State email address) and Password (Email password) to login to their Student Portal. Select the \u201CStudent\u201D tab at the top of the page. Select the \u201CStudent Landing page\u201D. Select \u201CRegister for Classes\u201D from the menu. Select the correct term and click \u201CContinue\u201D. Select \u201CAdvanced Search\u201D (the most convenient way to search courses by campus). Advanced Search allows you to choose your preferred search criteria. A suggested option is to select a subject, course number (if known), and desired campus. Then click on \u201CSection Search\u201D. A list of courses that meet the search criteria will be displayed. Please note that a course with a \u201CC\u201D to the left indicates the course section is \u201CFULL\u201D (maximum enrollment met). Courses with check boxes to the left have available space. To register for a course sections, check the box next to your selection and then, click on the \u201CADD\u201D button. Select \u201CNew Search\u201D to look up another course. Repeat as needed. To drop a course, go to the summary section of the registration form. Under the \u201CACTION\u201D column, select \u201CWEB DROP\u201D. Be sure to also, click on the \u201CSUBMIT CHANGES\u201D button to finalize. How To View Your Student Schedule Select the \u201CStudent\u201D tab, Select \u201CStudent Landing Page\u201D. Select \u201CRegistration\u201D, and then Select \u201CView Registration information\u201D. Review your schedule to be sure you are registered for the correct campus, day, times, and type of course. How To Pay Tuition and Fees Online Remember, registration is not complete until tuition and fees have been paid. Students with unpaid tuition and fees are subject to being dropped from their courses at any time. Select the \u201CStudent\u201D tab, Click on \u201CStudent Landing Page\u201D, Under the \u201CStudent Account\u201D section, click on \u201CMake a Payment, Deposit, and Payment Plan\u201D.
