Faculty advisors stand ready to assist you in understanding your degree plan and choosing your classes. Trenholm\u2019s faculty advisors are also your instructors and experts in the fields in which they teach. Their knowledge allows them to advise, guide, and help you navigate entry into your desired career field. Advising hours fall between 8:00 a.m. and 4:00 p.m. Monday-Thursday and 8:00 a.m. \u2013 11:30 a.m. on Fridays. However, each faculty member has specific office hours dedicated to helping students. Therefore, please use the contact information provided below to call or email to set up an appointment. When sending an email, always use your Trenholm email account, indicate \u201CAdvising or Registration Request\u201D in the subject line and include your full name, student A- number and a good contact phone number. Your advisor will respond to your email or call during their advising hours. Please allow one business day for all return correspondence.
