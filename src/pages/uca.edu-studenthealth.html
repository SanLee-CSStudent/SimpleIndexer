WHY use the Student Health Clinic (SHC)? The SHC provides patient-centered healthcare by highly trained professionals! Most services provided are included in your student health tuition fee! We will now accept insurance for faculty\/staff visits. WHAT is the SHC? The SHC is the campus medical clinic. WHERE is the SHC? Between the HPER and Baridon Hall, across from the cafeteria. Google link. SERVICES provided? We are pleased to offer a wide range of medical services to our students, faculty, and staff: General Family Practice services Lab X-Ray Immunizations Procedures Women\u2019s Health services COVID testing and vaccination Flu shots for students free of charge WHO? We have a Physician and multiple Nurse Practitioners to help diagnose, treat, and monitor your medical needs: Our Physician is a Board-certified physician in Family Practice and a member of the American Academy of Family Physicians. Our Nurse Practitioners are Board Certified in Family Practice as Family Nurse Practitioners and licensed by the State of Arkansas to practice as Advanced Practiced Registered Nurses. All providers have Prescriptive Authority. In addition to our providers, our staff includes: \u2013 Registered Nurse \u2013 Licensed Practical Nurses \u2013 Registered Medical Assistant \u2013 ARRT Registered X-ray Technologist \u2013 Operations Manager \u2013 Front Office Lead \u2013 Healthcare Specialist \u2013 Student Workers WHO WILL KNOW about my visit? The Student Health Clinic will not disclose your medical information to anyone unless YOU give your written permission for us to do so. This means we will not tell your roommates, your friends, the faculty, staff, or even family members about any of your appointments, phone calls to our nurses, or visits with our providers and staff unless we have received a signed Permission for Release of Medical Information from you.
