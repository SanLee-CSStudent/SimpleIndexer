Because it impacts your student experience and your career in a hugely positive way! Being part of a student club will help you: Learn more about yourself. Improve your communication skills, attitude and work ethic. Learn how to work on a team. Meet new students, make connections and build relationships. Gain practical experience and hone your skills in a safe environment. Engage with a diverse group of people and develop your skills in presenting and implementing ideas. Develop leadership skills that will be invaluable in all areas of life. Look good on your r?sum?. Find opportunities to give back to your community. Take a break from your studies and have FUN!
