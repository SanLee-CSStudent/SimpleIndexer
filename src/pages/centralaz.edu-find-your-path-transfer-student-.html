QUESTION ~ ASK HERE Welcome Vaquero\/Vaquera! A Transfer Student has previously attended college. Follow the steps below and get on your path! STEP 1 Areas of Interest: Areas of Interest are groups of related Pathways\/Programs that lead to a degree or certificate. \u2022Discover Your Career Pathway: MyMajors ~ Strong Career Assessment \u2022CAC\u2019s Areas of Interest and Pathway\/Program options: Arizona Residency Status Students are classified for tuition purposes under one of the following resident classifications: \u2022In-State Resident \u2022Out of State Resident (includes Non-resident and F-1 non-immigrant students) \u2022Western Undergraduate Exchange Program (WUE) \u2022New Mexico State Resident Tuition Waiver All students are required to complete the Arizona Residency Requirements as part of the CAC Application. Tips for an Accurate Residency Determination Payment Options: CAC In-State Tuition = $86 per credit hour FASFA \u2022Every year complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) form as soon as possible after October 1st. CAC\u2019s Federal ID Code is 007283. \u2022FAFSA will determine the student\u2019s financial need and aid eligibility for MOST financial aid. CAC Payment Plan We strongly encourage all students to apply for some form of financial aid. Learn about the different types of financial aid and programs below: \u2022 Federal Aid \u2022Scholarships \u2022Military & Veterans Educational Benefits STEP 2 Before You Apply: \u2022Have your social security number ready, if you have one. \u2022Have your non-expired, state-issued ID ready. If you do not have one call admissions at 520-494-5260 \u2022Review Campus Locations \u2022Review CAC\u2019s: Areas of Interest \u2022Must have or create a valid email address and phone number that you check frequently. Application follow-up information will be sent to this email. We do not recommend a school or work email address. Complete your CAC ~ Admission Application If you have questions regarding your Admissions Application: email admission@centralaz.edu After You Apply: \u2022MAKE SURE to check your personal email for admission confirmation. If you don\u2019t receive an email containing your CAC username\/password within 2 hours contact admissions@centralaz.edu \u2022Get your FREE Student ID on your next visit to any CAC campus. \u2022Students should log into their Student Email, Student Portal, Blackboard and more through : MyCAC Portal \u2022Enroll in Password Self-Serve On-Campus Housing\/Signal Peak Only ~ : Application and Information Placement Tools and Academic Advising Appointment: CAC recommends that students use Placement Tools to determine recommended English\/Reading and Math course placement prior to meeting with an Academic Advisor. Set an appointment with your Academic Advisor to discuss academic planning and course selection. Please bring the following to your appointment: \u2022Results from Placement Tool assessment \u2022Unofficial transcript(s) from each of the institutions you have attended. Official Transcripts MUST be received and evaluated by CAC before you can register for 2nd semester classes. Have Official Transcripts sent to: Central Arizona College\/Attention Registration 8470 N. Overfield Rd., Coolidge, AZ 85128-9030 STEP 3 Register for Classes and Pay Tuition and Fees Registration Options: \u2022Add\/Drop classes in Student Portal \u2022Complete Add Drop Form and email from your CAC Student Email to admissions@centralaz.edu \u2022or return form to any CAC campus admissions office All tuition and fees must be paid at the time of registration.
