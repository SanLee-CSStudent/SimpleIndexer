Rock University of Arkansas at Little Rock Search: Communications and Marketing Rock Region METRO Thanks to a partnership with the Rock Region METRO, UA Little Rock employees and students are able to ride the bus transit for free, using the Trojans U-Pass. With rising costs, environmental concerns, and the stress of commuting, public transit is on the rise. This partnership will benefit UA Little Rock employees and students and help provide easier access to both the university and the city. Ride for free Each employee and student can go to the I.D. Card Office \u2013 room 114 on the lower level of the Donaghey Student Center to pick up their Trojans U-Pass. The Trojans U-Pass is a sticker that will go on your UA Little Rock ID card. This sticker is all you need to use the bus system for free. Each semester, riders will need to get an updated sticker. A sticker issued in January will be good through the summer semester. Riders will need to get a new sticker for the fall semester. Getting started To find your route and view detailed information, view the schedules, maps and stops. With this live map, you can watch your bus or streetcar and click on any stop to get real-time arrival information based on GPS technology.
