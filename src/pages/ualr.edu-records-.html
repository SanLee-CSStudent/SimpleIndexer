The Office of Records and Registration acts as a registration resource for students and faculty alike and maintains the official academic records of all UA Little Rock students. The office provides a number of online services to students through their BOSS account, such as: registration, enrollment verification, degree verification and ordering official transcripts. Students can also print a copy of their unofficial transcript through BOSS.
