Demo Course - Blackboard Tuskegee University Online courses use Blackboard as the course management software system. Some academic units utilize other software platforms and instructions for accessing and using those platforms will be sent to you by your academic unit after you enroll. Blackboard A Blackboard course site provides you multiple resources including course materials; course calendar; student access to grades; course syllabi; lecture materials; email, chat and discussion tools and more. We strongly recommend that you take a look the demo course before your online class begins. Guests who are interested in learning how Blackboard works are encourages to use the demo course as well.
