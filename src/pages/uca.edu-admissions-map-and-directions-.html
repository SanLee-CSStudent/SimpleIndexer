From the East (Little Rock) Take I-40 West to Conway Take the first Conway exit (#129B) At the exit bear to the right and continue to Donaghey Ave Make a right at the light UCA campus begins immediately on the left and continues for .5 mi If you are coming for a campus tour, go down Donaghey until it intersects with Bruce St. The Welcome Center is located in Donaghey Hall on your right (hint, look for the bear on the side of the building).
