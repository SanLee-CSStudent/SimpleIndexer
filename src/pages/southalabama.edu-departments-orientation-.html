In addition to registering for classes, students become familiar with the campus services, support, and resources available for students at South. All new, incoming, undergraduate students must attend Southbound Orientation for the academic term in which they plan to enroll. This includes: Incoming freshman and adult freshman students Incoming transfer (including Pathway USA participants) and second bachelor\u2019s students All incoming undergraduate students that attended orientation for a previous academic term but did not take classes at South for that term Students will be assessed an orientation fee which is valid for the current term and will be reassessed if you change your enrollment term. Orientation registration must be completed online. Only admitted students may register for orientation. Note that non-degree or guest\/transient students are not required to attend orientation, but may do so if they choose. Students admitted to the professional component of nursing and graduate students will receive a specialized orientation through their departments. COVID-19 UPDATE USA is closely monitoring the coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic and prioritizing the safety and well-being of students and guests. At this time, orientation programming is scheduled to take place primarily online for incoming Summer 2022 students and in-person for incoming Fall 2022 students.
