LBW Community College is a great place to start or continue your journey to achieving your educational and career goals. LBW offers Associate in Arts degrees, Associate in Science degrees, university transfer courses, and career oriented certificates. APPLY NOW Ready to get started? Begin the free application for admission. Admissions We welcome you with our \"open\" admissions policy. There is no application deadline and no ACT or SAT score required for admission. Visit the Admission page to learn more about admission requirements and procedures. Financial Aid LBW offers a variety of programs for students seeking assistance in funding their education. This includes federal Pell Grant, Federal Supplemental Education Opportunity Grant (SEOG), Workforce Investment Opportunity Act (WIOA), Work Study, Veterans Benefits, private loans, and more. Scholarship Each year, LBW and the LBWCC Foundation offer financial assistance to students in the form of scholarships. Make sure to visit our scholarship page to get detailed information and deadlines. Student Housing Saints Hall student housing is located next to the Andalusia Campus. This two-building complex features a four-bedroom design with common areas of living room, dining room, and kitchen. Each apartment is fully furnished, including appliances in a full kitchen. Interested students must complete the Saints Hall Housing Application. Costs College is an investment in your future. LBW strives to be an affordable option for students choosing to pursue higher education. Testing & Assessments At LBW, we offer several testing options including the national ACT, ACCUPLACER placement assessments, and the General Education Development (GED) test. Visit Us We are proud of our college and what it has to offer, but don't just take our word for it. Schedule a visit and come see for yourself! College Counseling LBW offers counselors that can help you maximize your academic potential and achieve personal success! Counselors are available to meet with you to discuss success strategies, study tips, or plans for the future. LBWCC Experience The LBWCC Experience provides new students with the opportunity to get connected to the college early and a chance to learn more about LBW, academic advising, class registration, campus life, financial aid, tuition management, residential life, student records, campus safety, and what you need to know to have a successful start at LBW Community College.
