Nurture your talent. Develop your skills. Explore your curiosity. The College of Humanities, Arts, Social Sciences, and Education has exciting options to fuel your intellectual and artistic talents. Within our 14 departments and schools, we offer a wide range of certificates, undergraduate and graduate degrees. Our degree programs, applied learning opportunities, and community connections are designed to help you prepare for the demands of the fast-paced and ever-evolving job market of the 21st century. In CHASSE we encourage you to: Do what you love so you love what you do. We promote student connections with each other, faculty, staff, and our regional community. We will work with you to nurture, develop, and explore your talent, skills, and most importantly, your curiosity. We believe loving what you do will set a strong foundation for your future success in college and in life. Come join our Trojan family. Explore our degrees and discover your future at UA Little Rock.
