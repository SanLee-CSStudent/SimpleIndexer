About The Program This program provides students with basic electronics foundation. Students learn how to repair electrical equipment, understand safe operation of electrical and industrial equipment and gain knowledge in the field of robotics. Faculty Contact Edward Watts Biomedical, Electronics and Automation Instructor (870) 391-3189 edward.watts@northark.edu Learning Outcomes Enter the workforce and be gainfully employed. Understand safe operation and repair of electrical and industrial equipment. Interpret electrical and mechanical schematic diagrams. Correctly use basic electrical measuring instruments and apply readings to troubleshoot equipment. Perform basic repair operations and troubleshooting of electrical equipment. Including mechanical assembly, disassembly, and soldering of electrical circuits. Demonstrate the basic knowledge of automation equipment such as PLC's and Robotics in an industrial or hospital setting.
