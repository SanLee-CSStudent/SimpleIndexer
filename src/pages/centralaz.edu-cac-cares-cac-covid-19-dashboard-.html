Contact tracing is an important tool in reducing the spread of the coronavirus, and we strongly encourage you to cooperate with the contact tracing process should you be contacted. In addition to contact tracing conducted by the local Department of Public Health, Central Arizona College has contacted and advised those college-related individuals who have been identified as being in contact with the positive case. Any on-campus spaces affected have been thoroughly cleaned and sanitized. Please remember to wear a face covering, practice physical distancing, wash your hands frequently and stay home if you feel ill.
