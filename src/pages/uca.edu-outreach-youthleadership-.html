To learn more, watch this video from Conway Corp\u2019s TV show, Here and There. Our community is faced with many of the challenges that accompany the aspects of a growing area. The Conway Area Youth Leadership Institute (CAYLI) addresses those aspects and expose our county\u2019s youth to opportunities for community involvement and improvement. DESCRIPTION: CAYLI is a school-year program consisting of a two-day (overnight) retreat and seven monthly sessions, which include human services, education, media\/technology, healthcare, work world\/economy, government\/court system and environment\/agriculture. A community service project is a vital part of the curriculum. CAYLI is following strict social distancing guidelines as outlined by ADH and the CDC. PROGRAM CONCEPT: \u2022 The youth program represents a cooperative effort between UCA Outreach & Community Engagement, Faulkner County Leadership Institute and school districts throughout Faulkner County. \u2022 CAYLI is designed to help develop leadership potential within 11th and 12th grade students. \u2022 CAYLI addresses the challenges and opportunities of our community and exposes high school age young people to avenues for community involvement and improvement. \u2022 Enrollment is open to students who are currently high school sophomores and juniors in Faulkner County. Teachers, parents and others are encouraged to nominate students and encourage them to apply. Applications are open February 1st \u2013 March 1st with selections made in April. \u2022 Each class represents a broad cross-section of the community. Special effort is made to ensure a diverse group is selected. GOALS: \u2022 To develop leadership potential in high school students by promoting an atmosphere that will enrich students for leadership roles. \u2022 To broaden the community awareness of each participant. \u2022 To increase community involvement. \u2022 Provide mentoring and career exploration opportunities. BACKGROUND: The Conway Area Youth Leadership Institute is led by UCA Outreach, dedicated volunteers and graduates of the Faulkner County Leadership Institute. CAYLI was started in 1995 as the Faulkner County Youth Leadership Institute by that year\u2019s Faulkner County Leadership Institute (FCLI) adult class. Over 500 young people have graduated from the program. CAYLI receives its primary funding from fundraising and donations collected. To graduate from Conway Area Youth Leadership, a participant is expected to attend all sessions and complete the following requirements: \u2022 Summer Retreat \u2013 A two-day session (overnight) \u2022 Participation in seven monthly sessions \u2013 session times will vary \u2022 Conduct a community service project \u2022 Graduation in April Participation in the Conway Area Youth Leadership Institute is open to Faulkner County students currently enrolled in the 10th and 11th grades. Selection is based on: 1. Commitment to demonstrate leadership with peers in school and community settings. 2. Commitment to attend a retreat in the summer and all training sessions (September \u2013 March) during the 2021-2022 school year. 3. Ability to pay $50 enrollment fee. (Some scholarships available) For more information, contact Dr. Shaneil Ealy or Tracie Spivey at youthleadership@uca.edu or call 501-450-5267.
