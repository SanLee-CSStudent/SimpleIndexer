NW-SCC Transcript Request Are you needing to request your Northwest-Shoals Community (NW-SCC) transcript be sent to another college or university? NW-SCC has retained Credentials Inc. to accept transcript orders over the Internet. Please click on the appropriate option below to enter your order. If you are uncomfortable placing an order over the Internet, you can call Credentials Inc. at 847-716-3005 to place your transcript request. There is an additional operator surcharge for placing orders over the telephone. CURRENT NW-SCC STUDENT TRANSCRIPT REQUEST Official transcripts are available through OneACCS. Current NW-SCC students (active students and those enrolled for 18 or more months) will be able to conveniently order, send and track transcript orders directly through the OneACCS student portal (MY NW-SCC). Current Student Steps for ordering an official transcript: Log into your OneACCS Account (with your A# and password) Select Student Tab Select Student Records Select Order Official Transcript (follow on-screen directions) Select Begin Order and follow instructions on the screen. Unofficial transcript requests may also be made through OneACCS. In order to obtain an unofficial transcript, please select that option after selecting Student Records. FORMER NW-SCC STUDENT TRANSCRIPT REQUEST No NW-SCC ID\/email and password are required. This option is for former students who have not been enrolled for 18 or more months. CHECK NW-SCC TRANSCRIPT REQUEST STATUS Use this Link to check the status of your transcript request.
