Nearby mountains and lakes provide numerous outdoor activities, beautiful landscapes and abundant wildlife. Northland Pioneer College offers classes at four campuses and five centers across northeastern Arizona which includes the areas encompassed by three Native American tribes. As an HLC-accredited public community college, we serve over 5,000 students annually in courses and programs. Northland Pioneer College provides educational excellence that is affordable and accessible for the enrichment of communities across northeastern Arizona.
