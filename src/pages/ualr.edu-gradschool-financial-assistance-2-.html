Assistance for Graduate Students Graduate students attending UA Little Rock must be regularly or conditionally admitted and degree-seeking in order to be eligible for financial aid. The following forms of financial aid and assistance are generally available at the graduate level. Graduate Assistantships \u2013 only regularly admitted students are eligible for graduate assistantship positions. Federal Aid Programs Scholarships Tuition Deferment Plan (not available during summer sessions) Payroll Deduction \u2013 student must be a full-time employee in the University of Arkansas System. Please contact the Cashier\u2019s Office for more information. Veteran Benefits \u2013 information for current students and future students. Calculate your estimated cost of attendance here. NOTE: There are more restrictions for financial aid at the graduate level than the undergraduate level. The UA Little Rock Office of Admissions and Financial Aid provides information about federal aid as well as other program aids. Additionally, not all graduate programs offer financial aid. For more information, please visit the Financial Aid website. If you want to learn more about the restrictions or aid offered to graduate students, please contact Financial Aid at 501.916.3035.
