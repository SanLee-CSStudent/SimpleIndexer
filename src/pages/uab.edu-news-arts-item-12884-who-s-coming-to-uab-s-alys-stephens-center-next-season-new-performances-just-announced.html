New performances just announced by Shannon Thomason May 27, 2022 Print Email The 2022 season will feature rock, classical and world music, jazz legends, fun all-ages family, shows and much more. Watch for more performers to be added to the lineup. The Aluminum ShowNew artists and performances have been announced for the University of Alabama at Birmingham\u2019s Alys Stephens Performing Arts Center. The 2022-2023 season kicks off Sept. 16 with the Kenny Wayne Shepherd Band. On the schedule are all-ages family shows, classical and world music, jazz legends, and much more. Stay tuned as new artists are expected to join the current lineup. Since 1996, UAB\u2019s Alys Stephens Center has provided access to experiences that reach beyond sights and sounds, creating moments and memories that bring about positive change in the lives of individuals, families, students, artists and performers. The ASC presents a diverse cross-section of high-quality artists in one of the most acoustically sophisticated settings in the Southeast. For many shows, UAB employees can enjoy $15 off two tickets, and $10 student tickets are also available. To purchase tickets or for more information, visit AlysStephens.org or call 205-975-2787.
