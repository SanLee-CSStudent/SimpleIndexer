Except whether to be an honors student at UAB. You\u2019re smart. And one reason you\u2019re smart is because you\u2019re not afraid to ask questions. But there\u2019s really no question about whether you should pursue the many honors and awards available here at UAB. From the Honors College to scholars' programs to fellowships and more, we have all sorts of educational, career, and other opportunities for you. In other words, it\u2019s a good idea. So\u2014any questions? UAB Honors College At the Honors College, we want you to become, well, you. Here's how we make that happen: First, you do your part\u2014that is, be your driven, bold, and exceptional self. Then we\u2019ll add the engaging courses, the customized curriculum, the community of other inquiring minds, and the faculty who know your name in addition to your dreams and passions. The result? A confident, accomplished you. Admission to the Honors College To join the UAB Honors College, you must be admitted to UAB and have at least a 3.5 GPA and a 28 ACT or 1310 SAT score. If this is you, we\u2019ll automatically set you up with a personalized pathway, and you can choose to apply to a specialized program. If you\u2019re a bit short of the GPA and ACT\/SAT requirement, you can still apply at uab.edu\/honors. Personalized Pathway Everybody\u2019s different. So we\u2019re going to set you up with an honors advisor who can help you turn your interests and talents into goals. How? By tailoring a pathway just for you. With more than 30 honors courses per semester to choose from, we\u2019re going to help you craft a curriculum that will take you places you never imagined. Global and Community Leadership Honors Program This community isn\u2019t just local\u2014it\u2019s worldwide. When you join the Global and Community Leadership Honors Program (GCL), your responsibilities to work for social justice have an international reach. GCL prepares you to explore, understand, and lead in promoting positive social change while fostering connections all over the world. Science and Technology Honors Program Chomping at the bit to get inside a lab? Our Science and Technology Honors Program (STH) will prepare you to be a leader in that learning environment. STH equips you with the skills to investigate questions that pique your curiosity and share your discoveries with the scientific community. University Honors Program Our University Honors Program (UHP) lets you replace your core coursework with a unique and engaging arts and sciences curriculum. UHP courses and small seminars are led by teams of faculty from a wide variety of academic fields, so you\u2019ll gain diverse perspectives and form connections across multiple disciplines. Major-Specific Scholars Programs Numerous UAB departments offer major-specific honors or scholars\u2019 programs. You can also take advantage of competitive opportunities such as Chemistry Scholars, which pairs you with a faculty mentor, or the Phage Explorations Program, a biology research program for freshmen. Talk to your academic advisor about scholars' programs for your major. National and International Fellowships and Scholarships We can help you apply for prestigious national and international scholarships and fellowships. Our students have won awards such as Rhodes, Fulbright, Goldwater, Clinton, and Critical Language Scholarships, among others. Maybe you\u2019ll do the same. Learn More We're Here for You Our Admissions Counselors are here to help you every step of the way, from your college search to completing your application, and even through the enrollment process.
