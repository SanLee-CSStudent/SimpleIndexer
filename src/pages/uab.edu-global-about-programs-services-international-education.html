Mission \u201COur mission is to create and foster global partnerships with the aim of developing innovative, collaborative and interdisciplinary educational programs and establish UAB as a leader in the training of international visiting scholars, faculty, clinicians, researchers, technicians, and students.\u201D Our Goal Transform internationals professionals, scholars and students into global leaders in health, science and research to improve the quality of life for individuals in the Birmingham community and around the world.
