International students are encouraged to take advantage of Gadsden State\u2019s wide range of academic college transfer degree programs designed to lead directly to employment after one or two years of study. In addition, international students can enroll in the Alabama Language Institute (ALI), an intensive English language program founded in 1973. ALI, the oldest English language institute in the Southeast of the United States, has a tradition of preparing students seeking admission to Gadsden State and other four-year colleges and universities. Why Gadsden State? Find out why over 6,000 international students from 140 countries have chosen Gadsden State since 1968.
