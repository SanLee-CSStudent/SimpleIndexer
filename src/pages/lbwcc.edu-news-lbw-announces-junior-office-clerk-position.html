Wallace Community College announces the following position: Junior Office Clerk (Temporary: Summer Youth Employment Program) This position allows for the opportunity to acquire life and job skills and work experience while being reimbursed. This is also an opportunity for learning and growth in the skill gap areas Alabama employers have deemed an urgent need in the labor force. Work is performed under the close supervision of skilled craft personnel in accordance with department rules and regulations. The Junior Office Clerk will be employed temporarily through August 31, 2022, funded by the Alabama State Department of Human Resources Summer Youth Employment Project. Continued employment is not guaranteed beyond the funding provided through this project. SALARY: Compensation will be on the Lurleen B. Wallace Community College local Salary Schedule L at a rate of $12.00 per hour for a maximum of 29 hours per week. Eligible participants must be aged 16-24 and have a household income below 200% of the 2022 Federal Poverty Guidelines. In addition, participants must complete the Eligibility & Affirmation for Summer Youth Employment form (SYP-2074).
