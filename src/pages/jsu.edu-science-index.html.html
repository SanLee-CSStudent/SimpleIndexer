The College of Science and Mathematics at JSU offers a distinctive environment for investigation, learning, and discovery. In the classroom, laboratory, and field, our staff and faculty cultivate an atmosphere of inquiry. With programs of study in three academic departments, we prepare undergraduate and graduate students to join the grand enterprise of discovery we call Science.
