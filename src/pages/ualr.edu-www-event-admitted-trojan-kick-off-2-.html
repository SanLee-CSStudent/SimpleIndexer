Congratulations on being admitted to UA Little Rock! Celebrate your accomplishment at Admitted Trojan Kick-off, which is your first event as an official Trojan. To welcome you to campus, bring your family along for an outdoor BBQ. Meet new people as you play lawn games, enjoy the DJ, and grab snow cones. If you have documents to turn in or questions, you can also stop by offices\u2019 tents for help.
