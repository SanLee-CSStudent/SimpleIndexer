Info for Faculty & Advisors As faculty, advisors, and staff, you are crucial to making sure students know about the value of study abroad and the opportunities available to them. We\u2019ve gathered some resources to help you support and encourage UAB students to seek a transformative international education experience. Initiate a Faculty-Led Program Want to develop an international program for your students? We collaborate with faculty across campus to initiate and lead faculty-led programs. Become an Education Abroad Faculty Fellow Considering developing a faculty-led program, but not sure the next steps? Read about our innovative faculty fellows program and apply to become one today! Establish an Agreement We work collaboratively with faculty to establish Reciprocal Exchanges, affiliations with third party program providers, and Customized Program Agreements for faculty-led programs.
