IDI serves to help UCA become Arkansas\u2019 premier university for the tenets of diversity, belonging, inclusion, and equity. These principles are described below: DIVERSITY: UCA gives attention to representation as measured quantitatively and qualitatively BELONGING: It is the aim of UCA for all individuals to know they matter for their commonalities and their differences INCLUSION: UCA employs a diverse body of leaders who include diverse perspectives in decision-making that impacts policies, practices, procedures, and experiences EQUITY: UCA collaborates to alleviate gaps, disparities, and disproportionalities in representation, outcomes, and experiences We welcome your collaboration in promoting diversity, belonging, inclusion, and equity.
