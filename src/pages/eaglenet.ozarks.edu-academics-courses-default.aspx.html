The expectation is that students attend class every session in person. Synchronous Online This online course will require students and instructor to meet at the same time online for each class meeting. Students should plan on being available at the time noted on the schedule for each class meeting. Asynchronous Online This online course will not require students and instructor to meet at the same time online for each class meeting, and instead students can view the course materials and participate in online discussion groups (or other methods of engagement) at any time. No meetings will be held where students need to arrange to be online at the same time as the rest of the class. Mixed Online (synchronous and asynchronous) This online course will require the students and instructor to meet throughout the semester at preset times as a class and will also include recorded lectures and student learning that is asynchronous. You should plan on being available at the time noted on the schedule for class meetings as faculty might use that time throughout the semester for synchronous discussions and lectures.
