COVID-19 Notification and Mitigation Plans | COVID-19 Student Self-Notification Form Student life as a Matador will be an unforgettable part of your college experience, so be sure to get the most of it. Take your first steps or request info. Quality of Life Living on Campus Visit Residential Life. Living and dining on campus is convenient, economical and safe. Easy access to classes, the library, campus events, athletic events, the fitness center and pool are all great reasons to live at AWC \u2013 just ask the 300+ students who choose this option every semester. Campus Recreation Check out our student activities! Students at AWC have many ways to get involved outside of the classroom to enhance their college experience. We have over 50 clubs and organizations as well as Intramural sports, theater productions and many other fine arts opportunities. Food and Drink See what's on the menu at Dining Services. Tasty, health food (and lots of coffee) is available all over campus. Whether you\u2019re prepping for exams or hanging out with friends, you\u2019ll have options nearby for pizza, burgers, salads, baked goods, and more. Health and Safety We're concerned about your health and safety. From Health and Wellness Services to our Campus Police, you have access to the health and safety services you need to keep you focused on academic success. Life Together Clubs & Organizations Join a club! A great way to enhance and energize your college experience is by joining a club. Meet like-minded people, engage with faculty, and make a difference in your community. Student Leadership Interested in joining SGA? Whether you run for an office in student government, or step into a leadership role in a club, college is a great place to build your skills and your network. Cultural Diversity Visit AWC's International Program. AWC sits in a vibrant border region and attracts faculty, students, and presenters from all over the world. This is a great place to broaden your horizons, make new friends and learn about the world. Athletics Visit AWC Matadors. Our seven competitive athletic teams means there\u2019s always a home game to attend, a chance to show your school spirit, and a fellow student to cheer on.
