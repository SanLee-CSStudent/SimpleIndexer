Do not throw the 1095 form away. Although the 1095 form is not required to file your taxes, it should be kept as part of your tax documentation records. Additional information on the 1095 forms can be found on the Benefits FAQs webpage. Truth in Salary UAB will be providing a Truth in Salary Statement in compliance with a state law to reflect employer contributions to your benefits and salary\/wages reported in Fiscal Year 2020-2021. These statements will be available in the UAB for Me Portal by January 31, 2022. A copy will also be mailed to your home address. Total Compensation Statements The UAB Benefits Office is pleased to provide an updated Total Compensation Statement from UAB that will be available in March 2022. This statement will be housed in the UAB for Me system and will illustrate the value of your total compensation from UAB, including your salary and benefits. Together, salary and benefit programs represent a significant investment by UAB \u2014 designed to recognize your contributions to our collective success.
