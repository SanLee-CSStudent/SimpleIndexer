To keep our customers and our staff safe and to lessen the amount of physical contact, we will be implementing new techniques as suggested by the Arkansas Food Bank and the Centers for Disease Control (CDC). The Trojan Food Pantry is offering pick-up appointments only. All orders and appointments must be scheduled in advance and must be picked up at the scheduled time. If your order is not picked up at the scheduled time, we will send a follow up email asking you to reschedule your time. NOTE: If you are a first-time user, please complete the application before scheduling an appointment or completing your food order.
