Statement Division of Allied Health The Allied Health Division at Phillips Community College of the University of Arkansas provides the education and skills required to become successful in the health care field. Allied Health Programs are designed with employment of graduates as one of the major goals. Classes are small at PCCUA, guaranteeing individual and personal attention. The latest technology is used in: Classrooms Laboratories Hospital and other health care facilities. Graduates will find rewarding positions in medical facilities, such as hospitals, physicians' offices, laboratories, the Health Department, home health, ambulance services, and many more. The Career Services Office at PCCUA will provide assistance in finding the right position.
