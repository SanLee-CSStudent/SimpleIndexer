You can schedule your tour at our website uab.edu\/visit or by contacting our office at (205) 934-9098. How will my tour reservation be confirmed? You will receive an email and text message confirmation once your registration is complete. Can I change the date or time of my tour? Yes, you can call the Campus Visit Center at (205) 934-9098 to reschedule your tour. Prepare for your Visit Am I required to wear a mask? Masks are not required during indoor or outdoor portions of the tour. Masks are encouraged, but not required, while on the bus portion of the tour. For the most up-to-date information, please visit uab.edu\/uabunited. What should I wear for my tour? Attire for the tour is casual. Please remember, the tour is a walking tour, so wear comfortable shoes! Weather-appropriate attire is also recommended as tours take place rain or shine. Where do I park? We have designated reserved parking spots for visitors in Parking Lot 5A located at 1486 University Boulevard, Birmingham, AL 35294. Please refer to your confirmation email and itinerary for a helpful map and parking pass to print. When you arrive to your reserved parking spot, please stay in your car and call our office at (205) 934-9098. How long do tours last? A campus tour consists of a 15 minute admissions presentation, one hour walking tour led by our student TrailBlazers, as well as a 30-minute Dragon Wagon Bus tour. Please plan to be on campus for about 2 hours. What happens if it is raining on my tour day? Campus Tours still proceed during rain or shine as long as long as there is not lightning or thunder in the area. Please be sure to wear weather appropriate attire! What does the campus tour include, what will I see? The campus tour includes an Admissions overview presented by an Admissions Representative and a walking tour across campus that takes you to see our Campus Green, an academic building, the Hill Student Center, and our Campus Recreation Center. In addition to the walking tour, we have a riding portion of the tour as well that takes you around the magic city of Birmingham in our tour bus, the Dragon Wagon. Can we talk to an Admissions Counselor 1:1 on the day of? Yes, we have an admissions representative at the Campus Visit Center to answer your questions during the presentation. If you\u2019d like to schedule a follow-up meeting, those will be done virtually (via Zoom or phone call). Can we meet with someone in a department in which I am interested in? We will try our best to accommodate additional requests. You can also check our weekly interest session dates and times to add to your visit itinerary. Saturday Tours When are Saturday tours offered? Saturday tours are offered on select Saturdays throughout the year. You can see the available options at uab.edu\/visit or by calling us (205) 934-9098. How long do Saturday tours last? A full campus tour experience lasts approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes. Who gives the tour? Tours are given by our trained student tour guides, the TrailBlazers, who are able to give insight on their experiences as a UAB student. Can anyone come along on the tour with the student? We\u2019d love to host you and your guests! Due to limited capacities on our tours, we cannot guarantee space for guests who are not pre-registered. When registering, please select how many guests will be attending with the student. You may reserve up to two additional spaces with each student\u2019s registration. Other A member of our party has a mobility restriction. What resources are available to us while visiting? You can indicate that a member of your party has a mobility restriction by submitting an accessibility request when you register for your tour. We have an accessible route that is intended to accommodate those using wheelchairs, strollers, scooters, crutches, etc. while still showcasing our beautiful campus. We are unable to provide any medical equipment. Will there be Wi-Fi available while I am visiting the campus? Yes \u2013 guest Wi-Fi is available on our campus. Can I eat lunch while on campus? Due to COVID-19 restrictions on our campus, our campus dining options for guests are limited. We\u2019d be happy to provide you with some recommendations in the Birmingham area.
