Please schedule an appointment using our online scheduler; walk-ins are only permitted on a case-by-case basis and won\u2019t guarantee same day testing. Scheduling an appointment is easy though! Go directly to our scheduling site here: ualr.at\/exam. Live outside of Little Rock? You can check out our Remote Proctoring option through Honorlock, or you can download our Examination Proctor Agreement and discuss with your local testing center on how to set up your appointment there. You can check the Consortium of College Testing Centers (CCTC) to find a proctor, or simply ask your local accredited institution. Honorlock is only available to current UALR students for classroom exams if approved and set up by the professor. Students using Honorlock for classroom exams should not make an appointment. Feel free to browse our Exam Directory for more information on specific tests; alternatively, feel free to contact us at proctor@ualr.edu if you have any questions about what exam you should take for placement or to receive credit by examination. You should expect a response within one business day. Test Score Placement Guide The Academic Literacy and Composition Placement Guide and the MATH Placement Guide are great tools to get an idea on where you will be placed based on your exam results. Contact your academic adviser, dean, or department chair to determine your official placement in a course. Certification UA Little Rock Testing Services is certified by the National College Testing Association.
