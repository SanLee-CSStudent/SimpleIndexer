The Foundation was organized and incorporated in 2002 for the purpose of stimulating voluntary private support from alumni, parents, friends, corporations, foundations and others for the benefit of the College. The Foundation holds two major fundraising events each year \u2013 the Mustang Scramble and the Holiday Gala. The Foundation can receive contributions through Sponsorships, Major Gifts and Planned\/Estate Giving, Company Matching Gifts, and Payroll Deduction. The Foundation can also receive securities, patents, copyrights, and other forms of property. The Foundation Board is a group of 19 great community leaders from Jackson and DeKalb County. They volunteer their time and resources in supporting Foundation events and opportunities for the College and community. \u201CWe greatly appreciate the work of our Foundation Board members in supporting the college,\u201D stated Dr. David Campbell, NACC President. \u201CIt is our students who benefit so much from this support, particularly in regard to scholarships that they receive through the Foundation and its activities.\u201D Board Members Events How to Donate Photo Gallery Foundation in the News Home and Garden Tour Announcment NACC Foundation Announces Return of Holiday Gala | Northeast Alabama Community College NACC Foundation Announces Hilda Thornbury Fears and Carol McKown Fears Memorial Teacher Scholarship Northeast Alabama Community College 138 AL Hwy 35, Rainsville, Alabama 35986 | P.O.
