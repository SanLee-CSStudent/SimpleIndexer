Menu Close Parent & Family Relations Mission The mission of Parent & Family Relations at Arkansas Tech University is to engage with parents, family members, and supporters of our undergraduate students to promote a partnership that supports and encourages academic achievement and personal success of each of our students. Learning Outcomes Increased knowledge of Arkansas Tech\u2019s procedures, resources, and timelines. Awareness of trends, topics, and issues affecting students throughout their academic career. Connection to the news, events, and happenings of Arkansas Tech University.
