Have you considered becoming a recurring donor? We invite you to consider joining a special group of people that help sustain gifts by making a regular contribution through Mesa Community College. You can opt to give monthly, quarterly, or annually. Recurring gifts can help you fulfill your philanthropic goals by fitting giving into your budget on a steady basis. Even small gifts have a great impact, and there\u2019s no better time to extend your commitment. Before you make your secure gift online, there are a few things we would like you to know: Once you\u2019ve made your gift you\u2019ll receive an e-confirmation from the Maricopa County Community College District Foundation that your contribution has been received as well as a thank you for your contribution. If you wish to donate to a fund not listed in the form, please select \"other\" and then specify the fund or cause in the comment section. Should you encounter any problems, please contact the Office of Philanthropy and Alumni Engagement directly at 480-461-7200, or development@mesacc.edu and we\u2019ll be happy to work with you. Thank you for supporting MCC and its students. The Maricopa County Community College District Foundation (MCCCDF) is the 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization designated by the Maricopa County Community College District to receive and manage gifts on behalf of its 10 colleges.
