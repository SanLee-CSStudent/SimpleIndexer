Spouses and dependents of the above primary members may also join. It is not open to the general public. The membership fees are below. Currently enrolled students do not have to pay an additional fee in order to use the facility. It is already included in the activity fees. Non-student memberships are a 12-month commitment, with the exception of students taking one semester off or a dependent summer\/holiday pass. Members gain access by typing their Jag number or membership number at the front desk. USA employees and students may also swipe their active USA ID card\/Jag Card. If you are a spouse, dependent or plus one member and would like an ID card, there will be a $5 charge for the card. Membership forms may be turned in to the front desk of the Student Recreation Center or emailed to Shannon Rhodes. If you have any questions regarding membership, please call Shannon Rhodes, Assistant Director of Membership and Development, at 251-460-6293.
