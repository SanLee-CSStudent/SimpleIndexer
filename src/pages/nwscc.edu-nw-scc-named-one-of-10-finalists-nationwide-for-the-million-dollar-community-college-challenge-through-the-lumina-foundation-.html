More Info NW-SCC named one of 10 finalists nationwide for The Million Dollar Community College Challenge through the Lumina Foundation MUSCLE SHOALS & PHIL CAMPBELL, ALA. \u2014 Lumina Foundation announced this week that Northwest-Shoals Community College (NW-SCC) was selected as one of 10 finalists for The Million Dollar Community College Challenge, a national grant opportunity supporting brand building and marketing for community colleges. \u201CThe Million Dollar Community College Challenge is all about promoting the opportunities at community colleges and connecting adults to higher education,\u201D said Shauna Davis, strategy director for community college participation. \u201CThe 10 finalists each submitted a compelling vision for making vital campus to community connections and creative ideas for promoting their college\u2019s brand.\u201D \u201CIt is so exciting to be selected as one of 10 finalists from hundreds of submissions across the country,\u201D said NW-SCC President Dr. Jeff Goodwin. \u201CWe have a passion for educating our community, and we want our prospective adult students to know that college is for everyone and we are working to remove any barriers that might hold them back.\u201D The 10 finalists are: College of Eastern Idaho Community College of Rhode Island County College of Morris Great Falls College Montana State University Houston Community College Long Beach City College Madera Community College Madison Area Technical College Minnesota State College Southeast Northwest-Shoals Community College Once the grant process is completed, one college will receive $1 million, plus technical assistance to develop actionable brand building and marketing strategies. Nine colleges will receive $100,000 each, plus technical assistance. Round two and the final stage of the grant competition will require each college to create a brief video narrative that communicates what their college promises to adult students. The million-dollar winner will be named in August. For more information about the challenge, please visit luminafoundation.org\/challenge\/.
