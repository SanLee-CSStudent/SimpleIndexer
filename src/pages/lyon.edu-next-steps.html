Congratulations! You've been accepted to Lyon College. Now, what should you do next? We've outlined the next steps of your admissions process in the list below. Complete each action in the order listed, and you'll be ready for college in no time! If you have any questions throughout this process, please contact admissions@lyon.edu. 1. File your FAFSA Complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid online at fafsa.gov using your FSA ID for electronic signatures (available at fsaid.ed.gov). (US Citizens ONLY) file your fafsa 2. Pay your deposit We are thrilled that you've decided to come to Lyon College! Your $250 deposit guarantees a place in next year's class. All deposits are non-refundable. Deposit online Visit lyon.edu\/deposit now! Deposit by phone Call (870) 307-7250 today! Deposit by mail Lyon College c\/o Office of Enrollment Services 2300 Highland Road Batesville, Arkansas 72501 3. Complete the AR Challenge\/GDS Application (Arkansas only). Free money for Arkansas Students! Receive up to $14,000 over 4 years! APPLY HERE! 4. Log in to Lyon's online community! After depositing, you will receive an email with information about logging into Lyon's online community. You'll use your Lyon email to access essential tools for your success. student email Your campus email is your lifeline while at college. MyLyon Through MyLyon, you can review your grades, request transcripts, and more. Lyon App Keep up with what happening at Lyon! If you misplace any of this information, please contact the Office of Admissions at (870) 307-7250 or admissions@lyon.edu. 5. \"Become a Scot\" and attend SCOT WEEK! Advising for the Fall 2022 semester will begin in late Spring 2022. Once you deposit, start watching your email for notices on how to join Becoming a Scot, an online experience for all new students. Becoming a Scot will help you learn more about Lyon, connect you to your advisor, and also prepare you for Scot Week! Scot Week takes place right before classes begin and will help you get oriented to campus and meet your fellow classmates. More details about Scot Week will be shared during the Becoming a Scot experience. 6. Fill out your housing form. Secure your spot on campus now! If you want to live in premium housing, like the newly constructed or pet-friendly residence halls, you'll need to act fast! Those residence halls have limited spaces. Housing information will be available starting on May 1. You can apply beginning June 1, and will receive your housing assignment July 1. If you have questions, please contact the Director of Residence Life. 7. Submit Your official final transcript and test score. Please send all official test scores and official finalized transcripts from High School and College(s) to the Office of the Registrar as soon as possible, but not later than Friday, July 22. 8. Health and Wellness Submit your required immunization records. Health and Wellness will safely store your required immunization records. If you need them in the future, we can easily provide them for you. Once you are on campus, visit us for your Health and Wellness needs. submit your records Questionnaire *The last day to deposit for the Fall 2022 semester is August 5. Are you playing on a sports team for the Lyon College Scots? Congratulations! You will be a part of the Fighting Scots! Along with the above steps, student-athletes are required to complete these additional four steps. Make sure to complete these, so you can play for the Scots! For more information, check out the Student-Athlete Checklist. Go Scots Go! 1. Register with the NAIA. Time to make it official! Register with the National Association of Intercollegiate Athletics (NAIA). This will include submitting your official transcripts and ACT\/SAT scores. Register 2. Complete the athletic insurance form. Please include a copy of your insurance card if applicable. complete the athletic insurance form 3. Fill out the new Lyon student-athlete questionnaire. We are excited to have you on campus and want to learn more about you. This questionnaire helps. Complete the student-athlete questionnaire 4. Complete and submit your physical form. Lyon College requires all student-athletes to have a completed physical form and insurance form on file in the athletic trainer's office before participating in any organized physical activity, like practice or games. Your physical must be dated after June 1, 2022. Submit physical form Lyon College Start Your Future Lyon is more than just a college. It's a community distinguished by its academic curriculum, unique honor and social systems, and award-winning professors.
