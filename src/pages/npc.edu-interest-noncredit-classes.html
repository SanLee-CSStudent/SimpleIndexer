NPC offers fun, noncredit classes throughout our service area. Some classes run a full semester while others are just a week or a day or two. We offer classes in art, crafts, computers, cooking, dance, finance, health\/fitness, home\/garden, jewelry, music, quilting, sewing, Yoga and much more! Enjoy a fun, educational day trip or enroll your kids in special classes created just for them. Free Virtual Courses Access the complete library of locally produced pre-recorded courses. One registration allows you to access the entire library of online courses, at no cost! Topics include music, arts, computers & technology, finances, local history, and so much more. Plus, new videos are released weekly. Each video is produced by a local expert and available to watch at anytime from your computer or mobile device. Register Now! Personal Interest Classes At NPC our mission is to provide, support and promote lifelong learning. That's why we offer all kinds of noncredit, leisure learning classes ranging from exercise and fitness to expanding your computer skills or learning how to belly dance! New classes are always being added so check our course list frequently. Learn More Learning adventure excursions Adventure is calling you! Enjoy an educational short day-trip that allows you to explore different locations in northern Arizona: history, culture, scenic sights and new experiences. NPC will provide the adventure, reservations, instructor and transportation from Show Low, Snowflake, Holbrook and Winslow campuses. Learn More Kids College Your kids will have so much fun they won't even know they're learning! Students can even participate in our children's theatre production. Learn More NPC Kids stem van Expanding minds and having fun, that's what this traveling van is all about!
