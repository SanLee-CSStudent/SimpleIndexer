Division Overview Whether you are preparing for college and want to brush up on your math skills, or you are a dislocated worker needing to retrain for a better job, we have a program for you. Our programs are FREE! We offer Adult Basic Education (ABE), General Adult Education (GAE), and English as a Second Language (ESL) classes, the W.A.G.E program, and GED Testing. Program Structure The Adult Education Center offers a wide range of classes that are available to any adult over the age of 18 who is a resident of the area. Flexible class schedules provide day and evening sessions to meet the unique needs of adult learners.
