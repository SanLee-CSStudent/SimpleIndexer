Wallace Community College has three campuses. From our campuses, we serve the populations of Butler, Covington, Crenshaw, Coffee, and Geneva Counties. Our service area also reaches into the panhandle of Florida serving Jackson, Holmes, Okaloosa, Santa Rosa, and Washington counties. The Andalusia Campus is located on Hwy 84 East at 1000 Dannelly Blvd., Andalusia, Alabama. The campus is comprised of approximately 160 acres. The Campus joins the LBWCC Golf Course and the City of Andalusia's Dream Park. The campus includes a public walking trail and tennis courts. Andalusia Campus Map The Greenville Campus is located at 750 Greenville By-Pass in Greenville, Alabama. The Greenville Campus is conveniently located near Interstate 65 and is only 50 miles from Montgomery. Greenville Campus Map The MacArthur Campus is located on Hwy 331 North at 1708 North Main Street, Opp, Alabama. The campus is comprised of approximately 100 acres. The campus joins the Opp Public Library\/LBWCC MacArthur Library and includes a new walking trail. MacArthur Campus Map The Luverne Center is located at 886 Glenwood Road, Luverne, Alabama, in the former National Guard Armory. The Luverne Center houses Adult Education, Ready to Work program, Training for Business and Industry classes, continuing education classes, and university transfer courses, such as English 101, Algebra, and Western Civilization.
