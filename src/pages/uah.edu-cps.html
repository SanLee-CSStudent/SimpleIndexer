Degree Programs Learn about our Bachelor of Science in Engineering Technology (BSET) or Bachelor of Arts or Science in Professional Studies (BPS) degree programs. The BSET allows students to explore the integration of engineering principles and modern technology through a hands-on applied program. The BPS interdisciplinary degree offers concentrations to best match your professional and personal goals. Concentration areas include: Organizational Studies; Leadership Strategies and Dynamics; Technology, Science and Society; and Engineering Technology.
