Hammerschmidt Lecture Series Reports and Policies Vendor Information North Arkansas College announces Fall 2021 Dean's List by Northark Office of Public Relations Mon Jan 3, 2022 at 09:59 AM A total of 261 students have been named to the Dean\u2019s List at North Arkansas College for the 2021 Fall semester. That number includes 201 students who were named to the Dean\u2019s List for full-time students and 60 students who made the Dean\u2019s List for part-time students. In order to qualify for the full-time Dean\u2019s List, a student must complete 12 or more semester hours with a grade point average of 3.7 or higher for the semester. To make the part-time Dean\u2019s List, a student must post a GPA of 3.7 or higher for six to 11 hours during the semester and must have accumulated 12 or more hours with a GPA of 3.7 or above.
