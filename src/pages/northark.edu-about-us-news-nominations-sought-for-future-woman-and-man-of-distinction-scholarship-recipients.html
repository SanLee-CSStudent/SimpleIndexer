Hammerschmidt Lecture Series Reports and Policies Vendor Information Nominations sought for Future Woman and Man of Distinction scholarship recipients by Northark Office of Public Relations Mon May 2, 2022 at 01:15 PM The 2022 Men & Women of Distinction Committee is asking for your help to identify and nominate potential scholarship award recipients who will be recognized at the Women and Men of Distinction Luncheon held this June. The Committee will be awarding two $1,000 Northark scholarships to a male and female student who have exhibited the following characteristics: Has provided leadership By their actions made our community a better place to live Are unsung heroines working quietly to improve our area Inspires others Brings issue to the forefront for the betterment of our community Works with one or more organizations to bring about positive change impacting the lives of others Sets a positive example for others to follow. Students who reside in the counties of Boone, Madison, Newton, Searcy, Carroll or Marion and who plan to enroll at North Arkansas College in the fall of 2022 or spring 2023 are eligible. There are no income limits as this unique scholarship is designed to reward men\/women for their good deeds in helping others while also assisting them in achieving their educational goals. To apply for the scholarship, complete the scholarship form and send it to Micki Somers at msomers@northark.edu. Submit the Scholarship Information Form and include a narrative about the ways the nominee has exhibited the characteristics listed above. If you prefer to mail the information, please send it to Men & Women of Distinction Scholarship Committee, North Arkansas College, ATTN: Micki Somers, 1515 Pioneer Drive, Harrison, AR 72601 or by email to msomers@northark.edu. Nominations are being accepted until Tuesday May 24, 2022. North Arkansas College is committed to providing an educational and work environment for its students, faculty, and staff that is free from sexual discrimination including sexual harassment, sexual assault, and sexual violence. No form of sexual discrimination will be tolerated. See the full North Arkansas College Title IX Policy and Procedures for the specific definitions of sexual harassment and sexual violence, including examples of such conduct.
