Ready to Work Ready to Work provides FREE training in basic skills required by many leading businesses and industries. Non-Credit Skills Training The NW-SCC Office of Strategic Partnerships & Industry Training provides flexible and customized training. Customized Manufacturer Training Working with the industrial manufacturers in our service area to provide worker training is the primary focus of the NW-SCC Office of Strategic Partnerships & Industry Training. By working together, companies are able to meet minimum class sizes and obtain quality training locally at lower cost than sending employees away for training or by bringing in expensive consultants. Courses are offered on a variety of topics including PLC Troubleshooting, Hydraulic Training, Pneumatics Training, Welding, Electrical Maintenance, Alignment, and Blueprint Reading. Computer Software Training Because of the increased use of computers in every aspect of the world of work, we offer classes in the most popular software. We offer Microsoft Office 2010 or Microsoft Office 2013 training as well as training in other areas such as AutoCAD\/SolidWorks. If you are interested in taking any computer course, call us at the number below. We will work with you to schedule the course to meet your needs at a time convenient to your schedule. Alabama Technology Network at the Bevill Center Northwest-Shoals is a partner with and the home of the Alabama Technology Network (ATN) at the Bevill Center located on the Shoals Campus. It is one of a network of centers of technical excellence located across the State of Alabama.
