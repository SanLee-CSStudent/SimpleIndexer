Early decision deadline for academic scholarships effective for first-time freshmen beginning fall 2022 is January 15. Priority deadline is March 1, unless funds are depleted prior to this date. Each award has a limited number available. Grade point is cumulative and will be accepted on a six-semester high school transcript or later. Note: See website for retention, renewal and other criteria. University academic scholarships are not stackable. Superscoring will be considered. Retention criteria for all incoming freshman scholarships (excluding Arkansas Academic Challenge and Art, Music, and Theater Scholarships) include: 30 hours with a 2.75 GPA each year. Must enroll in a minimum of 15 hours each semester. Complete 27 hours with a 2.75 GPA by the end of the spring semester for the first year. Complete 30 hours with a 2.75 cumulative each year thereafter (summer classes at SAU may help meet deficiencies each year). At the end of each fall semester, you must have earned a minimum 2.25 cumulative GPA to continue receiving the scholarship the following semester.
