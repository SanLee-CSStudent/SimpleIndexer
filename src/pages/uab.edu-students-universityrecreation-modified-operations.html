Please be sure to read and familiarize yourself with the UAB University Recreation\u2019s COVID-19 Modified Operations plan, below, in its entirety. In order for our team to safely serve everyone, we ask YOU to follow the all University Recreation\u2019s Policies and Procedures while in the facility. Only TOGETHER we can reduce the spread of COVID-19 and remain open. Due to the nature of the COVID-19 pandemic, rules and regulations are constantly changing. We recommend that you continue to monitor the UAB University Recreation COVID-19 Modified Operations plan daily for updates. In alighnment with UAB's updated Masking Policy effective at 4 p.m. March 4, 2022, UAB University Recreation no longer requires masks to be worn at the Campus Recreation Center.
