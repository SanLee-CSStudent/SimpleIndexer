Now Offering Virtual Personal Training Are you interested in personal training, but you don't want to leave the comfort of your own home? Great news! We now offer Virtual Personal Training so you don't have to! When completing the Personal Training Request form, simply indicate that you have a preference for virtual training, and we will get you set up. Simple as that! All that is required for virtual sessions is a reliable phone or laptop with video so your trainer can efficiently correct and coach you through the movements to help you achieve your goals. The results of these tests are reviewed by a certified Personal Trainer and are used to help personalize a tailored workout plan specifically for you. To schedule your Fitness Assessment please complete the Health History Questionnaire as well as the Fitness Assessment Form prior to the appointment and email at This email address is being protected from spambots. Appointments will take about 45-60 minutes and will be held in Studio V with a certified Personal Trainer. Proof of purchase is required by showing of receipt. Clients are requested to wear comfortable athletic clothing, shorts and T-shirt. The completed Health History Questionnaire is required prior to beginning the Fitness Assessment.
