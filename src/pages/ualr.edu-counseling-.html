Little Rock Counseling Services Counseling Services is currently providing services virtually and in person. Please call 501-916-3185 to schedule a counseling session, or to reschedule or cancel an appointment. You may also email us at counselingservices@ualr.edu. Check out our Instagram @UALRwellness Online Therapy Students, faculty and staff have free access to completely private online self-help for stress, anxiety, and depression that fits around your busy life \u2013 anytime, anywhere \u2013 with TAO (Therapy Assistance Online). TAO provides online tools to help you conquer the day-to-day struggles around general stressors. The interactive sessions, mindfulness exercises, and practice tools all aimed at helping you achieve your goals. If you are experiencing a mental health emergency off campus, please call 911. If you are on campus, please call 501-916-3400. UA Little Rock Counseling Services is located in the Student Services Center, Suite 118.
