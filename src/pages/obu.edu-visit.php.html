Register Quick Links: Personal Campus Visit Maps & Directions Tiger Days Preview Days Applying to Ouachita Virtual Tour Virtual Visit Personal Campus Visit When you come to campus for a personal visit at Ouachita, you will get a glimpse into life as a student and spend the day learning more about what makes our campus and community unique. A typical visit takes a little over 2 hours and includes a full, 1 hour and 15 minute walking tour of campus and residence halls as well as a meeting with a dean or professor in your field of interest. Your admissions counselor will also spend time explaining the admissions process and answering any questions you might have about financial aid. We encourage you to schedule your visit at least 1-2 business days in advance and on weekdays when professors are available. Please note that personal campus visits are not available on days when we are hosting other events or on holidays when our university is closed. Register now TIGER DAYS Tiger Days are group events that give you a broad view of life at Ouachita! You\u2019ll get an insider\u2019s perspective on campus by hearing directly from the president, admissions counseling staff, faculty and current students. You\u2019ll also have the opportunity to visit in small groups with the dean or faculty member in an academic area that interests you. We want you to have the opportunity to connect with members of the Ouachita community face-to-face, to get your questions answered and to see what is so special about the Ouachita experience. While Tiger Day is a group event, it is designed for individuals and families. For those interested in bringing a church, social or school group to campus, please contact the admissions counseling office so we can plan a personalized experience designed to meet the needs of your group.
