With full-time and part-time classes forming every three to six months, the Arkansas Coding Academy offers you many opportunities to get started on a new career as a software application developer or computer programmer. Community Education Community Education assists in fulfilling a vital part of the University\u2019s mission by educating the citizens of Conway and surrounding communities through non-credit, personal enrichment courses that are available to the public during the fall and spring semesters. Contact Community Education at 501.450.5811 or email communityed@uca.edu. Community Development The Center for Community and Economic Development (CCED) houses the award-winning Community Development Institute Central (CDI) that has trained thousands of professionals for over three decades. The institute builds the capacity of individuals and communities to positively impact economic and social development. This is achieved by expanding an individual\u2019s ability to identify community assets, set goals, encourage collaboration and partnerships with stakeholders and bring together groups of people to respond to a broad range of economic and quality-of-life issues. Contact CCED at 501.450.3460 or email cced@uca.edu. Conference Services The UCA Conference Services team is here to assist with your event needs. We offer full-service event planning by our skilled staff of event coordinators and have a selection of spaces that are sure to fit your need. For more information about event planning and renting our venues, contact us at 501-852-2699 or conferenceservices@uca.edu. Culinary Classes Outreach is excited to bring a new culinary experience to Conway. From the novice chef to those who are more skilled and want to grow their culinary abilities, we offer something for everyone! UCA Downtown has state-of-the-art culinary stations that are equipped with the latest cooking utensils, and we also have the finest instructors who are ready to lead you through an exciting culinary experience. All classes are held at UCA Downtown. For more information, contact us at outreach@uca.edu or 501.450.3118. Professional Development UCA and the Division of Outreach and Community Engagement believe that professional development plays an essential role in your personal success. Outreach and Community Engagement serves to bridge the gap between your educational development and your professional success. Equitable access to such professional development opportunities is imperative. Contact Professional Development at 501.450.3118 or profdev@uca.edu. Online Training and Learning Outreach offers a wide range of highly interactive web-based courses and training programs that you can take entirely online. All of these courses include expert instructors, many of whom are nationally known authors. We offer programs for career training, skill development, professional certifications, continuing education, academic readiness, lifelong learning and recreational courses. Our online courses are affordable, fun, fast, convenient and specialized just for you. Some programs also qualify for special funding through the Workforce Investment Opportunities Act (WIOA). Contact us at 501.450.3118 or outreach@uca.edu to learn more. Service-Learning and Volunteerism Service-Learning at UCA is a course-based teaching and learning strategy that integrates meaningful community service with instruction and reflection to enrich the learning experience, teach civic responsibility and strengthen communities. Resources are available for students, faculty and community partners by contacting Dr. Lesley Graybeal at lgraybeal@uca.edu or 501.852.7416.
