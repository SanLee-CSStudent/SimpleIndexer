May 23, 2022 Over the past 21 years, the North Arkansas College Foundation 3-Person Scramble Golf Tournament has raised more than $510,000 for scholarships and instructional technology to benefit Northark students. There are several ways you can help us with this important fundraiser on May 23, 2022 at the Harrison Country Club. Along with teams, we need support as a Tournament ($1,000), Prize ($500), or Hole ($150) sponsors. Without sponsors, the tournament wouldn\u2019t be successful. The cost for a 3-person team is $300.
