New Student Orientation Congratulations on being admitted to UA Little Rock! We are so excited for you to start your journey as a Trojan. Orientation is required for new students and it is the best way for you to learn more about being a Trojan. During orientation, you will learn about where to study on campus, where to go for help both financially and academically, how to actually meet people, and so many other things to make your experience as a Trojan amazing. Orientation for Fall 2022 New Student Orientation for fall 2022 has two parts. Both will help you get ready for the upcoming semester. Part 1: The first part must be completed online through Go2 Orientation. You can access it by logging in with your UA Little Rock email address and password. Use this step by step guide to login. If you have trouble, call 501-916-3000 or email newstudents@ualr.edu. Part 2: The second part of orientation is held in person at UA Little Rock. We invite you to join us on either June 3, June 17, or July 8. During the on-campus portion of Orientation, you will learn more about the university and different resources available to you as a student. To register for an Orientation date, please click on one of the dates above. Make sure you\u2019re ready to start classes by completing the New Student Checklist. You can also follow @ualrnewstudents on Instagram for reminders, student organization spotlights, social opportunities and more. For more important dates and deadlines, view the academic calendar. Advising Academic programs at UA Little Rock are grouped within three colleges. You will be advised in the advising center of one of these colleges. Before registering for classes, freshmen will meet with their advisor. Your advisor will contact you about making an advising appointment, so check your email and\/or phone for a message. International Student Services If you are an international student studying at UA Little Rock, you will have Orientation with the Office of International Student Services. You can view the New Student Guide, as well as details about orientation. For more information, contact internationalservices@ualr.edu or 501-916-3566.
