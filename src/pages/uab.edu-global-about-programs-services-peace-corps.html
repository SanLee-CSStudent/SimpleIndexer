Its official mission is to provide social and economic development abroad through technical assistance while promoting mutual understanding between Americans and populations served. UAB has two distinct university Peace Corps programs available to its students. Peace Corps PREP Certificate Program The Peace Corps PREP certificate program provides undergraduates an opportunity to focus their coursework, extracurricular leadership, and community engagement activities to prepare them for international service.
