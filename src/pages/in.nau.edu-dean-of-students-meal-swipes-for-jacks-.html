Eligibility Criteria To participate in the Meal Swipes for Jacks Program, you: The applicant does not have an active meal plan during the semester for which they are applying for Meal Swipes for Jacks. The applicant is currently enrolled in 1 or more classes at NAU. The applicant can access the Hot Spot or Dubois Center dining areas at Flagstaff Mountain campus to utilize the free meal swipes.
