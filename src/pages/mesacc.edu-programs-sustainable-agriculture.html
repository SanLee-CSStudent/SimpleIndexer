Field of Interest With alternative food sources and new methods for sustainable farming cropping up every day, the need for skilled individuals who understand farming, sustainable methods for producing goods, and how to improve economics in agriculture roles are valued more than ever. The Agriculture program focuses on sustainable agriculture in rural and urban farm settings, agribusiness planning and management, and essential farming principles and practices that can transfer to the agriculture sector and agriculture-related roles, such as agribusiness, organic farming, agricultural and greenhouse management, beekeeping, and more!
