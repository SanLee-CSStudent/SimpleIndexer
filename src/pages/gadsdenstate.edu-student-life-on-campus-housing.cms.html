This on-campus housing facility is convenient to classes and adjacent to the cafeteria and recreation areas. Rooms and Amenities Fowler Hall is a 110-bed, co-ed facility that has a diverse community of residents, including international students and athletes. The facility is staffed from 7 a.m. to 10:30 p.m. Monday through Friday. Gadsden State Security maintains Fowler Hall after hours. Policies All Fowler Hall residents are expected to abide by the Sexual Misconduct Policy as well as the rules and regulations detailed in the Fowler Hall Handbook.
