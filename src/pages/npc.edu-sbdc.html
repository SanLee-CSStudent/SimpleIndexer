Northland Pioneer College's SBDC has individual guidance, educational programs and resources that help both prospective entrepreneurs and existing business owners be successful. All services are provided at no-cost and your information is held in the strictest confidence. Our award-winning business consultants are an objective resource, focused only on your best interests. We can help you start, grow, sustain or even sell a small business. No matter where you are in the business cycle, we can customize a program based on your specific needs. Top 5 Benefits of Working With Us 1. Objective Feedback and Advice from small business experts focused on YOUR best interests. NPC's SBDC staff have nothing to sell or promote Our goal is to give you accurate, timely and objective feedback 2. Save Time and Errors \u2013 clarifying confusion, solve problems, and get questions answered! Don\u2019t waste time or risk costly errors by acting on issues you don\u2019t understand We're like a concierge service, contact us whenever you encounter questions or problems of any type, and we will work to point you in the right direction When needed, we can help link you to other experts or organizations that are best equipped for your circumstances 3. We're Connected to critical resources, highly qualified professionals, and business organizations. Our award-winning staff members each have over 15 years of experience in small business issues We are very familiar with the issues businesses in northeastern Arizona face NPC's SBDC helps businesses make connections to organizations and other businesses that might be helpful for networking or business development We can help you with government contracting and Veteran opportunities 4. Financial Insights \u2013 our experts can help you optimize your cash management and\/or prepare stronger loan or line of credit applications All businesses can benefit from working with our business analyst who will examine your cash flow and provide guidance and feedback that can improve your profitability. Tracy Mancuso has many years of banking experience and can guide you on presenting your strongest case to loan officers. We may even be able to help you find additional funding sources you're not aware of. 5. FREE, Confidential and No Obligation We are here to help you! We've helped many local businesses succeed.
