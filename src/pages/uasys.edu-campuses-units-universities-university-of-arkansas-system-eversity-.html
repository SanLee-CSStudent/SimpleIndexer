The core principles of the institution include providing high-quality courses, affordable tuition and workforce-relevant degree programs, along with promoting student success in programs. The mission of eVersity is to provide high-quality, accessible, affordable, online education relevant to the modern workplace. Faculty from across the UA System develop and deliver rigorous certificate and degree programs that utilize rich data analytics to enhance student success and achievement. A commitment to continual improvement of the craft of teaching through practice and scholarship and to serving our public communities by providing timely, interesting and useful educational material are hallmarks of eVersity.
