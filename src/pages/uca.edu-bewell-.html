The university is committed to helping employees thrive in the workplace and at home by providing resources and incentives to encourage faculty and staff to lead a healthy lifestyle. BeWell is UCA\u2019s employee wellness program. It is a voluntary program available to all benefits-eligible employees and retirees enrolled in UCA\u2019s health insurance. Employees who meet the eligibility requirements can qualify for a discount on health insurance premiums and earn BearBucks. Components of the UCA BeWell program: \u2022 Biometric Screening \u2022 Health Risk Assessment Survey \u2022 Lifestyle Rewards (logging healthy habits and activities) \u2022 Resources and Encouragement for Healthy Living The annual BeWell cycle is November 1 to October 31. All participation components must be completed and submitted by the October 31 deadline. Ready to BeWell?
