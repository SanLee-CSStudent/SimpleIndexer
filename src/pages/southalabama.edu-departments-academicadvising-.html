Academic advising is the process where you obtain the critical information you need to make thoughtful decisions about your college career, such as deciding upon your major or solving any roadblocks you may encounter along the way. An academic advisor\u2019s purpose is to encourage students to develop as self-directed learners and decision makers. Good academic advising also supports the mission of the University of South Alabama through encouraging student academic success and persistence towards graduation. Advising Appointments: Advising appointments for current and prospective students can be conducted in person or virtually. You may schedule your advising appointment online via Navigate South which can be found on the student log in page or call AATS at (251) 341-4017. Have a quick question or need immediate assistance? You can also Chat us up!! using the chat button on this page. INFORMATION FOR NEW STUDENT ORIENTATION, ADVISING AND REGISTRATION: All NEW incoming students are required to register for new student orientation. Once you have registered for new student orientation, an academic advisor will reach out to you through your JagMail to schedule an appointment. During your appointment you will have the opportunity to discuss your program course requirements and enroll in courses. You have the option to meet with your advisor in person or via zoom. We will not contact new students for advising appointments until April 26th.
