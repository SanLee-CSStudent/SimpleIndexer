A Visiting Student will take a class or two. Follow the steps below and get on your path! STEP 1 Arizona Residency Status Students are classified for tuition purposes under one of the following resident classifications: \u2022In-State Resident \u2022Out of State Resident (includes Non-resident and F-1 non-immigrant students) \u2022Western Undergraduate Exchange Program (WUE) \u2022New Mexico State Resident Tuition Waiver All students are required to complete the Arizona Residency Requirements as part of the CAC Application. Tips for an Accurate Residency Determination Payment Options: CAC In-State Tuition = $86 per credit hour For more information on CAC Tuition and Fees \u2022For more information on the CAC Payment Plan \u2022Payment is due at the time of registration and can be made through Student Portal or in person at any CAC Location. \u2022Third Party Billing: Email student.accounts@centralaz.edu or call 520-494-5093 Visiting Students are not eligible for CAC financial aid unless using University Consortium Agreement. STEP 2 Before You Apply: \u2022Have your social security number ready, if you have one. \u2022Have your non-expired, state-issued ID ready. If you do not have one call admissions at 520-494-5260 \u2022Review the campus locations \u2022Must have or create a valid email address and phone number that you check frequently. Application follow-up information will be sent to this email. We do not recommend a school or work email address. Complete your CAC Admissions Application \u2022Choose Non-Degree seeking as your program of study. \u2022If you have questions regarding your Admissions Application: Email: admission@centralaz.edu After You Apply: \u2022MAKE SURE to check your personal email for admission confirmation. If you don\u2019t receive an email containing your CAC username\/password within two hours contact admissions@centralaz.edu \u2022Get your Student ID on your next visit to any CAC campus. \u2022Students should log into their Student Email, Student Portal, Blackboard and more through MyCAC \u2022Enroll in Password Self-Service \u2022If you have a consortium agreement, deliver this to CAC Financial Aid. \u2022Review Placement Tools and Academic Advising information on our Advising webpage. On-Campus Housing\/Signal Peak Only ~ Application and Information How to Enroll in your Course(s): Email ALL of the following information from your CAC Student Email account to: admissions@centralaz.edu (Please allow 48 business hours for a response) CAC Business Hours: Monday -Thursday, 8 a.m. \u2013 6 p.m. \u2022Include the following statement in your email \u201CI give you permission to register me for the following course(s):\u201D \u2022Your first and last name \u2022Your CAC Student ID # (880\u2026\u2026) \u2022Program of Study: Non-degree seeking \u2022Unofficial college transcripts \u2022Unofficial high school Transcripts if you have graduated within the last three years \u2022Course\/Courses you would like to enroll in, include the Course Code and Section Number. Example\/ ENG101, 20FA1234. \u2022Class Schedule ?ALL of the above information MUST be provided through your CAC Student Email for an Academic Advisor to evaluate for prerequisites and registration approval. Emails lacking any of the above information will be returned and registration not completed. Above information should be emailed to admissions@centralaz.edu STEP 3 Paying for College: All tuition and fees must be paid at the time of registration: CAC\u2019s Tuition and Fees \u2022Check amounts due on Student Portal If you have financial aid pending, you must pay in full or sign up for the payment plan. \u2022Pay Online: Login to your Student Portal to pay your tuition \u2022CAC Payment Plan \u2022Pay In Person: \u2022Pay by Phone: 520-494-5240 New Student Orientation Registration and More information Orientations are an opportunity to connect with other students, faculty, and staff. Topics covered include: Working with Advising, Utilizing Financial Aid, Using Blackboard\/Email and more. Final Details \u2022Become familiar with Blackboard where you access your courses, clubs, organizations, technology tutorials, tutoring and more. \u2022Courses may not show up in Blackboard until the scheduled course start date. \u2022Review\/print your schedule anytime in your Student Portal \u2022Find Textbooks at CAC Campus Bookstores or online (bring your schedule and photo ID to purchase books in-person) \u2022Student Success Resources APPLY To CAC AREAS OF INTEREST ACADEMIC ADVISING Share on Facebook Share on Twitter Share on WhatsApp Share on Pinterest Share on LinkedIn Share on Tumblr Share on Vk Share on Reddit Share by Mail District Office 8470 N.
