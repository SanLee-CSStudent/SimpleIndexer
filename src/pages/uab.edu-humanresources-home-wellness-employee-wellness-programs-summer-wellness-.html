During the month of June, you\u2019ll gain free access each Thursday to the UAB University Recreation Center (five days total). Participating gives you access to weekly physical activity and nutrition content, information on weekly events and more. Visit the UAB University Recreation center at least two of the five Thursdays to earn a free month membership, 100 points in your My Health Rewards account and more!
