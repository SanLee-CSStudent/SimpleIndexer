Let us know what you find useful or needs improvement: Feedback Form Scholarly Publishing & Open Access Publishing research in the academic environment has changed dramatically. Electronic publication and open access of scientific materials has created new and sometimes challenging process of submitting peer-reviewed articles. We have created this guide to help you find the information you need to meet these challenges and publish findings.
