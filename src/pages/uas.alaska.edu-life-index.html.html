Living On Campus New Student Orientation: On-Campus and Online Student Engagement and Leadership Online Campus Life at UAS: On-Campus & Online Living On Campus The Juneau campus offers residential student housing in suite and apartment configurations, including all services and utilities. Be close to your classes, get involved with study groups, activities, and clubs, and let us cook for you with a variety of meal plan options. JPH Freshman Residence Located in the heart of the campus, with amazing views of Auke Lake, JPH (John Pugh Hall) is a hub of activities, social connections, and access to educational services exclusively for entering freshmen. Apartments and Family Housing Two- and four-bedroom apartment are located on-campus, with connecting paths and a shuttle. Enjoy apartment-style living with the benefits of campus life, including socializing and activities at the central Housing Lodge. Housing Campus Dining First Year Experience (FYE) Make the most of your first year at UAS! During FYE, first time freshmen, transfer, and exchange students learn about all of the exciting things that they should be engaging in during their First Year. Alaska Leadership Initiative (AL-I) Getting involved with your campus and community helps teach leadership skills and can make students eligible to receive $2,000 to apply toward the cost of housing. He is the official spokeswhale for Whale Wednesdays\u2014the first Wednesday of every month to wear our love (and UAS logo wear) on our sleeves. Spike loves people and parties: watch for Spike at student events and town parades!
