Programs Sexual Assault Covid-19 Updates Click for Current Policy and Information Campus Parking UA Little Rock students and employees who wish to park a vehicle on campus must first register their vehicle with the UA Little Rock Police Department. To register your vehicle, you will need to know your T-number, make, model, basic color of your car, and your license plate number. You can get your parking permit online at Parking Permits Online or in the Parking Office on the first floor of the Donaghey Student Center. Commuter Parking Permits are available for online purchase to UA Little Rock Students, Faculty, and Staff only. eStem students or employees, Sodexo employees, and visitors\/guests who wish to purchase a Commuter Parking Permit must do so in-person at the Parking Office located in the Donaghey Student Center. Resident Parking Permits are not available online. Resident students who wish to purchase a Resident Permit must do so in person at the Commons Building on move-in day or at the Parking Office in the DSC. Once a parking permit is obtained and placed on a registered vehicle, you may park in any of the \u201Copen parking\u201D lots. This includes lots 12, 13W, 13E, 14, 15, 16, and the parking lot on the east side of University Plaza. Parking is also available in the metered lots or the parking deck. The fee for parking in the meter lots is $1 per hour using coins or, if paying by credit card it is $3 minimum for two hours and additional hours may be added at $1 per hour at initial point of sale. The fee for the parking deck is $1 per exit. View the parking map for more information. Student Reserved Parking Students may apply for reserved parking in one of the gated parking lots on campus. Reserved parking fees are $165 annually. Lot choices are lot 2 and the lower level of the parking deck. Reserved parking is available on a first come, first served basis. Employee Reserved Parking Employees may apply for reserved parking in one of the gated parking lots on campus. Reserved parking fees are $165 annually. Lot choices are lots 3, 4, 5, 7, 8, 9, and the lower level of the parking deck. Reserved parking is available on a first come, first served basis. Evening Parking Any faculty member, staff member, or student with a valid UA Little Rock parking permit can park in the gated reserved lots after 4 p.m. in non-numbered, non-handicapped spaces by swiping his\/her UA campus ID in the proper slot at the gate. If you look on the back of the ID, there is a black stripe from one side to the other. You will swipe the card in the reader with this stripe up. Visitor Parking Visitors may park in any of the metered parking lots, the fee for parking in the meter lots is $1 per hour using coins or, if paying by credit card it is $3 minimum for two hours and additional hours may be added at $1 per hour at initial point of sale or they may park in the parking deck for a fee of $1 per exit. All special event parking must be arranged with UA Little Rock Police Department one week in advance of the scheduled event. Please call Jesse L.
