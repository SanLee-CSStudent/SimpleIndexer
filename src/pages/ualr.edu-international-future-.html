The timeline features optimal dates, which vary according to your situation. Who is an international student? UALR defines international students as any non-U.S. citizen without permanent residency. All international visa holders will be charged the international student fee and can use the resources of the Office of International Services. The majority of the information on this website is directed toward students with an F-1 or J-1 immigration status.
