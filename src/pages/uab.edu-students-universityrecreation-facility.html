LEARN MORE Parking & Directions Campus Green The UAB Campus Green is an open green space designed to be accessible, safe, attractive, and useful to the University community and their guests. The Green was designed as the grassy area bounded by sidewalks, with large open green spaces for picnics & concerts, shady trees & walking paths. The Green is comprised of five areas that can be reserved individually or as a whole. The areas consist of the North Green, the South Green, the North Plaza, the West Plaza, and the East Plaza. UAB Intramural Sports Complex UAB\u2019s Intramural and Club Sports Complex is the perfect spot for students to compete and show off their athletic skills outside of the Campus Recreation Center. The field is made of natural grass and is perfect for intramural sports such as flag football, soccer, ultimate Frisbee as well as club sports. Although the Intramural and Club Sports Complex is not open recreation, groups and individuals are welcome to make reservations by filling out the rental request form. Reserve now!
