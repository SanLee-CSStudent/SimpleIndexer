EMAIL THE DIETITIAN Simple Servings UAB Campus Dining is committed to providing delicious dishes to students with allergies and dietary needs or restrictions. That\u2019s why we have a dedicated separate kitchen at our Simple Servings station in the Commons on the Green to provide menu options that are free from dairy, eggs, wheat, shellfish, soy, peanuts, tree nuts, and gluten. Simple Servings accommodates individuals who are looking for gluten-free menu items and also appeals to students who prefer simple foods. DOWNLOAD BITE Mindful UAB Campus Dining places a high priority on creating healthy environments for our customers. So if you\u2019re looking for healthy food that tastes great, look no further than Mindful! You\u2019ll know it's Mindful when you see the green apple heart icon on the Bite menu and in our dining hall. Mindful is all about providing choice - the choice to make healthier selections, the choice to learn about food, the choice to start living a healthier lifestyle.
