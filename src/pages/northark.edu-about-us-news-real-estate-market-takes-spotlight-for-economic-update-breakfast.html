Hammerschmidt Lecture Series Reports and Policies Vendor Information Real estate market takes spotlight for Economic Update Breakfast by Northark Office of Public Relations Mon Mar 7, 2022 at 02:41 PM Doyle Yates, Executive Vice President of Coldwell Banker Harris McHaney & Faucette, will be the speaker Wednesday, March 30, 2022, in the North Arkansas College (Northark) Economic Update Breakfast Series (EUBS). The event, sponsored by The Point Financial Group and Northark, will be held in the John Paul Hammerschmidt Conference Center on the college\u2019s south campus in Harrison. Breakfast will be served starting at 7 a.m. in the JPH Conference Center. The program starts at 7:30 a.m. Mr. Yates\u2019 presentation will be presented in-person. Tickets to attend are $15 each and can be purchased online at https:\/\/secure.qgiv.com\/for\/nacf (choose [Other] when selecting direction of support and note EUBS) or by contacting Rodney Arnold at (870) 391-3229. Seating is limited and reservations will be required to attend. Doyle Yates is the current Chair of the Arkansas Real Estate Commission, serving in his third appointment by Governor Asa Hutchinson. He is a past president of the Arkansas REALTORS Association and was the 2021 Region 9 Vice President for the National Association of REALTORS. Additionally, Doyle serves as NAR\u2019s Federal Political Coordinator to Senator John Boozman. In 2015 he served as the president of the Northwest Arkansas Board of REALTORS which currently comprises over 3600 members and is the largest REALTOR board in the state. Having acquired much experience in real estate over the past 40+ years, Mr. Yates will be presenting a real estate market update for local, state, and national markets. His presentation will highlight current economic factors along with trends that impact respective real estate markets. While the real estate market has been very robust the past few years, Mr. Yates will also address the possible impact future interest rate hikes may have on our local economies as it relates to the real estate market. Mr. Yates is regarded by many local REALTOR agents and comes highly recommended in helping the Economic Update Breakfast Series return to a normal schedule at Northark. The College is pleased to have Doyle Yates as part the Economic Update Breakfast Series and the public is encouraged to attend.
