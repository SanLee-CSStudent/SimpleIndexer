Search Educational Talent Search (ETS) identifies, prepares, and assists students in the 7-12th grades with pre-college and career preparation. Services include, but not limited to, assistance with finding and applying for financial aid, scholarships, and college admission; ACT testing preparation; personal, career and academic counseling; leadership and STEM summer program opportunities, visits to 2yr. -4yr. college campuses; financial literacy and connection to tutoring. ETS schools served include: Baxter County \u2014 Cotter and Norfork; Boone County \u2014 Alpena, Bergman, Lead Hill, Omaha and Valley Springs; Fulton County \u2014 Mammoth Spring and Viola; Independence County \u2014 Midland; Izard County \u2014 Melbourne; Johnson County \u2014 Lamar, Oark and Westside; Lawrence County \u2014 Hillcrest, Hoxie and Walnut Ridge; Marion \u2014 Bruno-Pyatt and Yellville-Summit; Newton County \u2014 Deer, Jasper, Mt.
