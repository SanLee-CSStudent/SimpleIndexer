of Attendance (COA) is not what Northwest Technical Institute (NWTI) will charge a student. The COA purpose is to provide an estimated figure of all costs a person might have during the 9-month academic school year (August-May) and includes items like housing, food and living expenses. To determine the actual tuition and fee structure at NWTI for the 2021\/2022 year, please review the 2021\/2022 Catalog and Student Handbook.
