The Pioneer Esports team competes against other colleges and is a member of National Junior College Athletic Association Esports (NJCAAE). Esports provides benefits such as teaching students how to function as a team, how to focus and to handle pressure and problem solving in a fun and competitive atmosphere. Northark has built a glass-enclosed esports arena on its South Campus that will house team practices and competitions.
