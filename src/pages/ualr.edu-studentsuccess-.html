Success Coach Downloadable PDF Helping Trojans Succeed Student success is the number one strategic goal for the university, the campus community, and our students. We know that success for a UA Little Rock student requires a significant commitment from the student and the University. Success is defined by students with the goal of degree attainment and the development of life-long learning skills. As Trojans, we are committed to providing you with experiences that will help you develop the skills needed to actively engage as a member of an inclusive community, develop personal competence, agency, resilience, and live a healthy and balanced life during and beyond college.
