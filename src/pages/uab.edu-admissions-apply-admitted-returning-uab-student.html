Complete this checklist, created especially for students returning to UAB, to help make your transition smooth and simple. Step 1. Activate your BlazerID You\u2019ll need your BlazerID to register for classes, pay your tuition, view your financial aid, and much more. If you have forgotten your BlazerID, click on \u201CDo I Have a BlazerID from the menu on the left side of the page. If you know your BlazerID, you will most likely need to reset your password by clicking on \u201CChange BlazerID Password\u201D from the menu on the left side of that page. Once you have reset your password, you should be ready to log in to BlazerNET. The BlazerID and password may take up to one hour to reactivate. Activate BlazerID Step 2. Explore BlazerNET BlazerNET is the official portal to the UAB community and the most reliable source of information for UAB students. It\u2019s convenient, secure, and provides easy access to your UAB email, class schedule, financial aid status, transfer credits, Graduation Planning System (GPS), important announcements and more. Use your BlazerID and password to login. Start Exploring Step 3. Apply for or Accept Financial Aid All it takes is just one form. Complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) at fafsa.gov. Use Code 001052 to send your application to UAB. Priority Deadlines Summer\/fall students: December 1 Spring students: Not applicable If you receive financial aid award, accept or decline the offer at uab.edu\/blazernet. Submit Your Immunizations Enrolling students must show proof of the required immunizations with either an official certificate of immunization, a photocopy of an immunization certificate, or written documentation from their physician. If you were previously enrolled at UAB some of your immunizations may be on file. Before you get any new vaccinations, log in to the UAB Patient Portal and check your immunization requirements. Immunization records for Alabama residents that are already on file with the state registry are added automatically.
