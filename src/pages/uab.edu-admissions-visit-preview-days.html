Virtual Visits Virtual Campus Tour Virtual Information Sessions ChooseUAB IGTV Tours Visit Preview Days Preview Days UAB offers three types of preview days for prospective students: UAB Day for high school students looking to enter UAB after graduation, Transfer Day at UAB for college students who may be interesting in transferring to UAB, and Homeschool Day specifically for homeschool students looking to attend UAB after graduation. All three events offer a great overview of what life is like as a UAB student. UAB Day Prospective high school students are invited to join us for UAB Day, which features a browsing fair of academic departments and student services, academic breakout sessions, special interest sessions, campus tours, and more. Transfer Day at UAB Students interested in transferring to UAB are invited to Transfer Day, where you can speak with representatives from academic departments, learn more about student services specifically for transfer students, and hear from a panel of current UAB students who also transferred to UAB. Homeschool Day at UAB Homeschool students are invited to Homeschool Day, where you\u2019ll have the opportunity to discuss the process of applying to college, browse an academic fair, take a campus tour, and speak with current UAB students.
