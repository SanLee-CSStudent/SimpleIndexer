News News A- A+ Northeast Alabama Community College Visual Arts Division to Hold Student Exhibition April 27, 2022 NACC Art students are pictured here preparing for their Spring Exhibition. NACC\u2019s Visual Arts Department presents a Spring 2022 Student Art Exhibition from April 26th to May 6th. This exhibition will feature artworks made by our current NACC students from this semester and is on display in the Learning Resources Center (LRC) on campus. The objective of this show is to showcase the students\u2019 creative talents and celebrate their accomplishments with the NACC family and the community at large. The exhibition will be on display at the Learning Resources Center first floor art gallery. The Visual Arts Department will have an opening reception for the show on April 26th from 12:00-2:00 pm. Light refreshments and drinks will be served. There will be an outside art professional to jury the show, and awards will be presented afterward. The award ceremony announcement will begin at 1:00 p.m. NACC Art Instructor Jair Chen is especially proud of these students and hopes the community will come view their work. \u201CWe encourage everyone to come and join us to celebrate their accomplishments,\u201D said Chen. For more information concerning NACC\u2019s Visual Arts Department or the Student Art Exhibition, please contact Jaia Chen at chenj@nacc.edu.
