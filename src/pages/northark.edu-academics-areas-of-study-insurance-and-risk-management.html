About The Program This program was designed in collaboration with local insurance agencies to meet the needs of insurance agencies. The program is designed to provide the entry-level skills necessary to become employed in the insurance and risk management field. The program will touch on the basics of all types of insurance. Faculty Contact Cheryl Keymer Department Chair of Business and Information Technology (870) 391-3225 ckeymer@northark.edu Learning Outcomes Graduates will: demonstrate the ability to communicate effectively both orally and in writing. identify the importance and need for risk management and insurance. identify the careers available in the risk management and insurance field. demonstrate knowledge of insurance contracts and provisions for health and property and casualty insurance. identify and analyze ethical and legal issues in business.
