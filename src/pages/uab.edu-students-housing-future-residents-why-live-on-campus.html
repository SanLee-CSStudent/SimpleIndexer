Plus, you can get some exercise from walking to class the next day. Even better \u2013 you get to sleep later with all the time you save from not commuting to campus, so you won\u2019t look like a zombie going to class. VIEW CAMPUS MAP Birmingham in Your Backyard! Living on campus puts you in the heart of downtown Birmingham - conveniently located near restaurants, fine arts & entertainment, and a host of volunteer opportunities. EXPLORE BIRMINGHAM You\u2019re more connected It\u2019s a lot easier to get involved and make friends when you\u2019re actually on campus. You can meet up with friends for lunch at the Hill Student Center or find a spot on the green to hammock. GET INVOLVED You\u2019ll do it if you want to graduate OK, that\u2019s not entirely true. But studies show that students who live on campus have higher GPAs and are more likely to graduate. So why not make the odds a little greater? Parking won\u2019t put you in a bad mood You won\u2019t have to deal with the hassle of finding a parking spot with only five minutes before class. You also won\u2019t need to leave twenty minutes before necessary just to account for the time it\u2019ll take you to find parking. PARKING INFORMATION You\u2019ll only pay one bill - No hidden costs Rent, electricity, water, cable, Wi-Fi \u2013 even furniture \u2013 are all included for a single, set price. Calculating your expenses is easy. VIEW HOUSING RATES Campus is Safe! Safety is a top priority at UAB. We have an accredited police force that patrols our campus 24 hours a day. A supervisor and several officers from the UAB Police Department\u2019s special Student Housing Police Precinct routinely patrol the residence halls and parking areas on foot and on bicycle. SAFETY & SECURITY It\u2019s part of the experience Each residence hall sponsors fun activities \u2013 from ice cream socials, to roommate compatibility improv sessions, to dodge ball tournaments, and lots more. Many people say the best benefit of living in student housing is getting the chance to meet all the other students.
