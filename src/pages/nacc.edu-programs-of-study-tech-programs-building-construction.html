The program includes the following options: Associate in Applied Science (AAS) degree in Building Construction with the following options: Building Construction - Carpentry Building Construction - Electrical Building Construction - General Building Construction - Heating and Cooling Building Construction - Masonry and Concrete Building Construction - Plumbing Certificate in Building Construction with the following options: Building Construction - Carpentry Building Construction - Electrical Building Construction - General Building Construction - Heating and Cooling Building Construction - Masonry and Concrete Building Construction - Plumbing Short-Term Certificate in Building Construction with options in: Building Construction - Acoustic Ceiling Insulation Building Construction - Building Maintenance Building Construction - Carpentry Building Construction - Concrete Building Construction - Construction Building Construction - Construction Equipment Building Construction - Drywall Building Construction - Electrical Building Construction - Exterior Finishing Building Construction - Heating and Cooling Building Construction - Masonry Building Construction - Plumbing Building Construction - Roofing Building Construction - Stone and Tile The job market for building construction is very favorable with an expected growth of 378 construction-related jobs within 40 miles of the NACC campus between 2020 and 2025. Many construction-related jobs are among the fastest growing in the north Alabama region and beyond. Opportunities abound in the construction industry.
