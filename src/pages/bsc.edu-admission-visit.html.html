Schedule Your Visit Admission Events Daily Campus Visits In a world where you can research stats and read about programs on the web, coming to visit campus allows you to hear stories and meet the people who make the College a community. During a 20-minute information session and student-led tour, you will experience a snapshot of the Birmingham-Southern community. We offer visits to campus Monday through Friday and on select Saturdays. Plan Your Visit Group Visit Are you a high school counselor looking to bring a group of students to visit campus? We offer group visits on select weekdays at at 11:00am and 1:00pm. Group visits include an Information Session with an Admission Counselor and a walking tour of campus where groups will have the opportunity to see our academic buildings, Norton Campus Center, and our residence quad.
