To request Course Forgiveness email registrar@nacc.edu with the following information: 1. NACC A# 2. The course(s) in which you would like to apply course forgiveness Academic Bankruptcy Academic Bankruptcy is the removal of one to three semesters of grades from the calculation of a student's cumulative GPA. To qualify for Academic Bankruptcy, a student must have never implemented the policy and must have completed 12 semester credit hours with a grade of 'C' or higher at NACC since the most recent semester for which the bankruptcy is requested To request Academic Bankruptcy email registrar@nacc.edu with the following information: 1. NACC A# 2. The term(s) in which you would like to apply Academic Bankruptcy Transfer Transient Students What is a Transfer Transient Student? A transient student is a student enrolled at NACC who would like to take a class at another college with the purpose of transferring the credit back to NACC. Request Transient Letter To request a transient letter (sometimes called a Letter of Good Standing), email your request to registrar@nacc.edu and include: 1. NACC A# 2. College needing your transient letter 3. Course(s) you would like to take at the college FERPA What is FERPA? FERPA stands for the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (of 1974). FERPA protects a student's education record. What are my FERPA rights? Current and former students FERPA rights include: 1. The right to inspect and review your education record 2. The right to request to amend your education record if you believe it is inaccurate or misleading 3. The right to provide written consent before NACC discloses personal identifiable information from your record except to the extent that FERPA authorizes disclosure without consent (i.e. directory information or subpoenas) 4. The right to file a complaint with the U.S. Department of Education concerning alleged failure by NACC to comply with the requirements of FERPA What is directory information? Directory information is defined by FERPA as information contained in an education record that would not generally be considered harmful or an invasion of privacy if disclosed. Directory information may include: \u2022 Name \u2022 Home address \u2022 School email address \u2022 Telephone number \u2022 Date of Birth \u2022 Major field of study \u2022 Enrollment status (i.e. full-time or part-time) \u2022 Withdrawal status \u2022 Honors, degrees, or awards received \u2022 Dates of Attendance FERPA video Contact Us Email: registrar@nacc.edu Phone: 256.228.6001 Extension 2439 Mailing Address: Registrar's Office \u2022 PO Box 159 \u2022 Rainsville AL 35986 Northeast Alabama Community College 138 AL Hwy 35, Rainsville, Alabama 35986 | P.O.
