In Person Visit the Pence Center Lobby, at the Signal Peak Campus, Monday through Thursday from 8:30 am \u2013 12:30 pm or call to schedule an appointment. The Box Office in the Pence Center is open for ticket sales one hour prior to the events held there. By Phone Call (520)477-SHOW Click for a list of the 2019-2020 events. For more information, see our digital brochure or visit our ticket website: www.EventsAtCAC.com Specials Ticket Discounts All discount tickets may only be purchased with cash or check. Season Tickets: Season Tickets for Premier Events are $95 each \u2013 That\u2019s a $15 savings! Other Discounts are available \u2013 please call the Pence Center Box Office at (520) 494-5511. New Premier Plus Series: We\u2019re excited to offer a new series of events! The \u201CPlus\u201D is for the 2 for $20 discount we offer if you also purchase Premier Show tickets. For every two Premier Show tickets you purchase, you may purchase Two Premier Plus Tickets for only $20. Early Order Discount: Purchase any Premier Show tickets more than a month before the show and receive a $2 discount per ticket! (Not to be combined with any other discounts). Group Discount: Tickets for groups of 10 or more can enjoy a discount! Group ticket discount is not available for all events. Call (520) 494-5511 to see if a group discount applies and for more details on savings. Seating At The Events Seating will begin approximately 30 minutes before the show. The majority of the Pence Center accessibility seating is accessed from the east entrance, which is closest to the parking lot. Call (520)477-SHOW for more information about parking, ramps, and accessibility. Volunteer Become a Volunteer Usher Ushers are greatly appreciated, and for their service are able to attend the event at no cost. Send us an email at the address linked below if you are interested in volunteering. In the email please provide your name(s), address, & email address. Also, let us know whether you are a fulltime resident or a winter visitor; &, if a winter visitor, the months in which you typically arrive\/depart. Contact CAC\u2019s Community Events Coordinator at events@centralaz.edu with any questions. * Community events offered by CAC are enrichment entertainment options for the community. Although some events may be viewed as educational events, they offer no college credit to the attendee. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us at (520) 477-SHOW.
