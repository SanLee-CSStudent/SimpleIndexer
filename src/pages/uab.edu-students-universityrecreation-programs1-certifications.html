You will learn the following specialized problems: Extended Patient Management - Situations when many hours or days stand between the injured person and definitive hospital care. Extreme Environments - Heat, cold, altitude, and other environmental factors that pose a significant threat to the safety of patients. Limited Resources, Equipment, and Personnel - Learn to successfully improvise with available equipment to create tools such as splints or litters for the safe evacuation of patients.
