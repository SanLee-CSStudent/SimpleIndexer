Field of Interest Humans are made to explore. As a student of the Astronomy program, you will get to explore space \u2014 from our sister planets in the solar system to the farthest reaches of the universe. Our experienced instructors will teach you where aliens might exist in our galaxy and how to actively search for them today. You'll study our current generation of space probes and how stars are created, live their lives and die, then give birth to the next generation of stars. You will also study black holes \u2014 where space and time itself is twisted and warped. As part of the Astronomy program, you will gain hands-on experience using telescopes and computer software to learn the actual techniques used to discover all of these things. The Astronomy program will provide the necessary foundation to succeed in careers in the life, physical, environmental, and social sciences and related teaching positions. You could be a part of what you've only dreamed about in science fiction. Join the Astronomy program today and discover the explorer in you. Transfer Degree Want to pursue a bachelor\u2019s degree?
