SUMMER 2022 Scholarship Opportunity: Alabama Committee on Credentialing and Career Pathways (ACCCP) Scholarship This scholarship provides assistance for students pursuing a credential in Air Conditioning & Refrigeration (Evening Classes at the Valley Street Campus), Automotive Service, Auto Collision Repair, Construction, Diesel, Electrical (East Broad Campus only), Industrial Automation (East Broad Campus only) or Welding. Students can receive tuition help for up to 12 hours during the summer semester. Those interested should contact the program instructor to sign up and to fill out a brief application. Adult Education learners and dislocated workers without a high school diploma or GED are encouraged to apply for approved programs.
