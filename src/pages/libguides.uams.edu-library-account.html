As a faculty, staff, or student at UAMS, a central Arkansas health professional, or part of the ARKLINK program, you can borrow items from the circulating collections at the UAMS Library. As a faculty, staff, or student at UAMS you also qualify for borrowing privileges with the Central Arkansas Library Systems or any affiliate library of the ARKLINK program. In this guide you will find information about how to set up an account for these borrowing privileges and how to access each account online.
