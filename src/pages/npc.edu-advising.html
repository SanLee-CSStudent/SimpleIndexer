Have a question, ask an academic adviser! Their job is to ensure your success by putting you on the quickest path to graduation so you don't waste money and time. Advisers can help you with: exploring career options, course selection, prerequisites, information about new student orientation, placement testing, establishing a degree\/certificate plan so you take the right classes, dealing with the 12-credit limit, and more! Meet with an NPC academic adviser at any NPC location. You can call, email them or schedule an appointment. (Personal information cannot be given out over the phone or through email. If you need to take a placement test please set up an appointment. If you need to meet with an advisor outside of regular business hours, please contact your advisor via phone to schedule an appointment.
