Maricopa Community Colleges Foundation scholarships include privately funded opportunities that are open to all MCCCD students and in some cases, non-Maricopa colleges and universities, as well. A FAFSA and verified lawful presence are not eligibility requirements for the majority of Maricopa Foundation scholarships. External Scholarships Scholarship funds that are available to students through private foundations or external organizations. Tribal Scholarships These scholarships are offered through various local and national tribal agencies. Students must apply directly through the tribal agency\/tribal education department they are affiliated with. For additional information, you must contact the tribal agency directly. Scholarship Process Scholarship Recipients If you've been awarded a scholarship, the information is sent to the Financial Aid and Scholarship Office. The scholarship will be processed and added to your student financial record. If there are any additional criteria you must meet before receiving your scholarship funds, you will be notified by the Financial Aid and Scholarship Office and\/or the scholarship donor group or organization. If the scholarship allows a refund, you will be notified by email. Scholarship Refunds When your student account has been updated, you will receive notification that your scholarship money is available as a disbursement (known as a refund). By signing up for the Maricopa Student Refund Program (MSRP) you can have your refund sent to you by direct deposit or you can elect to receive a paper check. For additional information on the MSRP please visit https:\/\/my.maricopa.edu\/msrp. Scholarships have specific requirements for disbursement, and some scholarships may not allow a refund. Scholarship Checks If you have a scholarship check that needs to be submitted to MCC, our address is below. If the check is made out to you and MCC, please sign the back of the check before mailing it. You might also want to take a picture of the check for your records. Mesa Community College Attn: Financial Aid 1833 W Southern Ave Mesa, AZ 85202 Additional Information Please note that public assistance, transit subsidies, federal financial aid, grants, loans, and other stipends can be affected by the amount of scholarship funds you receive.
