Cost of attendance based upon student status, housing, and meal plans. Cost figures assume that the student is dependent and are based on a full-time, undergraduate student taking 12\u201318 credit hours per semester during the day, living on campus in Pittman, Smith or Vail Hall, and holding a carte blanche meal plan. Students taking less than 12 hours will pay per credit hour. Students taking more than 18 credit hours will pay the tuition for full-time students as well as an additional per credit hour rate for each credit hour above 18 hours. Freshman are required to live on campus and hold a 19-meals\/week plan regardless of earned credit hours. Transfer students and upperclassmen who have earned at least 32 credit hours or are in second year of residency are required to hold a 12 meals\/week plan or higher. Students who have earned at least 64 credit hours or are in third year of residency have their choice of any of the meal plans. All on-campus residents are required to purchase a meal plan. Commuters may choose to purchase any meal plan. 2021-2022 Total Direct Cost 2021-2022 Direct Cost (Anticipated) Annual Charge Amount Tuition and Fees $35,360 Room and Board* $11,260 Total $46,620 2021-2022 Undergraduate Tuition Worksheet 2021-2022 Undergraduate Fees Worksheet 2022-2023 Total Direct Cost 2022-2023 Direct Cost Annual Charge Amount Tuition and Fees $36,724 Room and Board* $11,626 Total $48,350 2022-2023 Undergraduate Tuition Worksheet 2022-2023 Undergraduate Fees Worksheet The stated annual cost of attendance is made up of estimated direct costs.
