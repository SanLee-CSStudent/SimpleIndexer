Services The Office of Academic Services (OAS) coordinates support services to faculty, staff, and students in the five UAMS colleges, graduate school, University Hospital, and the northwest campus. Support Services provided are related to classroom and laboratory teaching, space management and scheduling, and instrumentation repair. Academic Services is composed of three support divisions: Room Scheduling, Laboratory Support and Instrumentation Repair.
