Start your healthcare career today! The Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA) Training Program prepares students to provide direct patient care to the healthcare industry. Upon successful completion of the program, students will be eligible to sit for the state board exam, become certified, and listed on the Alabama Nurse Aide Registry. 6-week course Dates: June 13 \u2013 July 22, 2022 Time: 5:30 pm \u2013 9:30 pm Days: Monday through Thursday Location: Huntsville Campus Cost: Course Fee $799 (Includes textbook, one set of scrubs) The program includes a 20-hour clinical rotation at a long-term care facility. Required Clinical rotations will be held the last week of class, Monday through Friday from 5:00 pm \u2013 9:00 pm. Additional Fees Students are responsible for the following costs (estimated): 2-Part TB skin test \u2013 $50.00 Background and drug screen \u2013 a minimum of $83 COVID Vaccinations documentations \u2013 fee varies- must be fully vaccinated and turn in COVID card by July 10, 2022. State Certification Exam Fee \u2013 $75.00 Requirements Students must complete the 2-part TB skin tests or chest x-ray. Documentation of results must be received by July 10, 2022. Background check and Drug screen COVID Vaccination \u2013 must be fully vaccinated (booster not required at the present time) The fee for the state certification will not be due until after the end of the class. Payment must be made at the time of the testing via debit or credit card. The state exam will be held in the Health Sciences building on the Decatur campus. Ready to Enroll? Follow these steps to enroll Click on the registration button, register, and pay for the program If you are funding through a Sallie Mae Loan or a scholarship, please contact the CWS Healthcare office at 256-890-4786 Students will then be sent a confirmation email and dates for a mandatory Enrollment Session Students choose one of the scheduled enrollment sessions (dates listed on the order confirmation) and send the date via email to diane.peck@calhoun.edu. Students must attend one of the mandatory Enrollment Sessions During the enrollment session, students will sign the required paperwork and try on scrubs Register Now All refund requests must be received in writing by May 24, 2022.
