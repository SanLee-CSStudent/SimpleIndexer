Traditional Student Scholarships Non-Traditional Student Scholarships Athletic Scholarships Dual Enrollment Scholarships Transfer Student Scholarships Additional Scholarship Opportunities: Summer Incentive Grant The LBWCC Foundation Summer Incentive Grant is available to assist students during the summer 2022 semester. This grant provides students the chance to take one three-credit-hour class and get a three-credit-hour class paid for by the LBWCC Foundation. The incentive applies to current LBW students, new incoming or returning students, 2022 high school graduates, transient students (defined as those who attend a four-year institution and are home for the summer), and rising high school juniors and seniors participating in academic dual enrollment or accelerated programs. The grant will be awarded on a first-come-first-serve basis and can only be applied after all other forms of aid have been depleted. Applicants will be notified of awards through their LBW Saints email. If a student receives a summer incentive grant, they must register for classes and pay their portion of the tuition and fees prior to May 26, 2022. The LBW Foundation Summer Incentive Grant application has closed. We received far more applications than available awards. Notifications will be sent next week to applicants' Saints Email.
