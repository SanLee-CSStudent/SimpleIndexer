Troy University's Virtual Graduate Fairs feature webinars discussing various graduate programs, giving you the opportunity to learn more about what program is best for you. Past webinars have discussed master's programs in criminal justice, management, nursing, applied behavior analysis, psychology, and more. You can view some of these webinars below. We hope that you will join us for the next Virtual Graduate Fair on June 6-10, 2022! 2022 Virtual Graduate Fair Master of Social Work (MSW) Dr. Amy Cox of the College of Health and Human Services MSW program will discuss graduate student success and program delivery in TROY\u2019s Master of Social Work program. Topics include an overview of the program, career options, online learning, admission requirements, tuition and more.
