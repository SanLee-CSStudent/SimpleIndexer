The UAB Passport Office was recognized as the top facility for Innovative Customer Service in 2019! As an official U.S. Department of State Passport Acceptance Facility, we are happy to help you apply for your passport or for your child's passport. The UAB Passport Office is open to the public. You do not have to be a UAB student or employee to apply for a U.S. passport at our facility. Announcement Limited passport appointments are available Monday through Friday from 8:30am-4:30pm!
