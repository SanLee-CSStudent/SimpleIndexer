Box 159, Rainsville, AL 35986 For course descriptions and program requirements: Catalog For program requirements: Transfer Guides\/Degree Sheets For course syllabi: Syllabi For tutoring: Tutoring English Writing Lab The English Department at Northeast Alabama Community College offers an Associate in Arts degree and an Associate in Science degree focusing in English and designed for those students planning careers in secondary education or majors\/minors in English. The course descriptions link on this page allows the students to access the college catalog which provides a list of courses offered by the department as well as programs of study for students pursuing a degree in English. Additional information can be obtained from the department office, college ext. *2231. The department also administers the college's Writing Center. The Writing Center is equipped with thirty computers and a laser-jet printer. An English tutor is available to help students in English 092, 093, 101, and 102, and with literature classes. The tutor's hours vary from semester to semester but are posted on the college website.
