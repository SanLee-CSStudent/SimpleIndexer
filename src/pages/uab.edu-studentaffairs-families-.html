MISSION & PURPOSE The Office of Parent & Family Services is dedicated to creating and strengthening the relationship between parents and family members of current UAB students and the University by creating intentional programming and providing critical resources and regular communication to support student persistence and success towards graduation. For some students, personal, emotional, psychological, academic, or other challenges may hinder their ability to succeed both in and outside of the classroom. When a student encounters a barrier to their success, it is important to know who to turn to; who can help. Please let us know if there is a student you believe may need additional support.
