Fields of Interest Do you have an appreciation for fashion and the popular and unique trends we see across the internet and in fashion magazines? Do you have an artistic eye when putting together an outfit? If you want to turn your passion into a career and get a fashion degree, our Fashion Design and Merchandising program can help you start off on the right foot. Upon completing this program, you can enter the exciting world of fashion merchandising as a wholesale and retail buyer, sales rep, online merchant, merchandise displayer, window trimmer, and more! With continued education, you\u2019ll be qualified to become a purchasing or supply chain manager. This Fashion Design and Merchandising program strategically places you at the intersection of fashion, business, and marketing. Our experienced instructors will provide you with the foundation of fashion theory, the societal and technological aspects of the textile and apparel industry, and the creation, promotion, distribution, and retailing of consumer goods in a global environment. Our curriculum stresses the importance of leadership, teamwork, creativity, and effective communication throughout the product life cycle process. Make it happen today! Enroll in the Fashion Design and Merchandising program. The Right Pathway for You MCC offers Certificates of Completion and Associate Degrees for this program. Your interests and goals will guide you in selecting the pathway that is ideal for you. You may find a combination of certificates or degrees is the fastest way to realize your dreams. Certificates The knowledge and skills acquired while earning a Certificate of Completion (CCL) can help launch you into a new career or energize your current job. Generally requiring fewer credit hours than degrees or transfer programs, a CCL may be your best first step into higher education.
