With over 175 groups, students are able to share interests, network, volunteer, plan programs, and \u2013 most importantly \u2013 have FUN! The University's student organizations cover the spectrum of activities and interests, from the Pep Band and the Space Hardware Club to Student-Run eSports and the Medieval Combat and Culture Group. Service UAH offers Service Opportunities for students to develop life skills and give back to the community as a whole. As part of this effort, the Volunteer Chargers help through a variety of campus events and service activities, such as Alternative Fall and Spring Breaks. A list of local service organizations and charities is also available for anyone looking to volunteer in the Huntsville area. Leadership The Office of Student Life encourages personal growth through participation in university traditions, events, and leadership programs. Government The Student Government Association (SGA) provides a forum for the free and open expression of student ideas and opinions, is a liaison between the students and university administration, works to instill a sense of community on and off campus, and assists in creating an atmosphere conducive to academic achievement. The SGA provides a voice for opinions and concerns of students and also helps enhance the student experience. Recreation University Recreation builds camaraderie and character through outdoor recreation activities, competitive intramural leagues, and club sports. Outdoor recreation trips to locations in the Tennessee Valley and beyond are planned periodically throughout the year, while intramural and club sports take place during the fall and spring semesters. Greek The Office of Fraternity & Sorority Life promotes community service, philanthropy, scholarship, and social interaction among the 350+ members of its seven fraternities and six sororities. Fraternities: Alpha Phi Alpha, Alpha Tau Omega, Delta Chi, Phi Beta Sigma, Phi Kappa Psi, Pi Kappa Alpha, and Sigma Nu. Sororities: Alpha Kappa Alpha, Alpha Omicron Pi, Delta Sigma Theta, Delta Zeta, Kappa Delta, and Zeta Phi Beta Charger Union The heart of the UAH campus is at Charger Union (CU). With a 100,000 square feet building, Charger Union provides work-spaces, entertainment, and other meeting spaces. With a 300+ seat full Dolby surround sound cinema theatre, the CU is the center of student life engagement. Let the Student Life staff help you find your niche at UAH!
