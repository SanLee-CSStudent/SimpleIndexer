Ph.D. People Resources News & Events Community Alumni Give The College Home Student Resources Math Learning Lab Welcome to the Math Learning Lab (MLL) The MLL is a computer lab in Heritage Hall room 202 where UAB students enrolled in MA 094-227 can work on the computers and get math help from tutors who are graduate students in mathematics. Questions or concerns? Contact us: Email: This email address is being protected from spambots. You need JavaScript enabled to view it. Phone: Math Department Office, (205) 934-2154 Tutors WILL: help you learn and understand concepts through questions and examples. They are trained to guide you so that you can complete your work independently. Be sure to bring your notes, work, and materials. Tutors WILL NOT: help you with graded assignments, do all of your homework with you, teach you everything that will be on a test, teach you the material that was covered in a missed class, or work with you for extended periods of time. MLL Hours Monday-Friday from the first day of classes to the last day of classes. No appointment is needed, but Calculus and MA 180 tutors are only available during designated times. CLOSED during holidays and breaks. NO TUTORING during Final Exam week. NO FOOD or DRINK allowed except bottled water. Go to UAB United for the latest information on COVID-19 safety and requirements.
