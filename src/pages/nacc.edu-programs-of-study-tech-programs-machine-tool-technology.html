Students will learn to read technical drawings and use multiple computer aided design (CAD) and computer aided manufacturing (CAM) software applications, cutting tools, measuring tools & inspection gages to produce and inspect precision component parts made from a wide array of metals and various other materials. The MTT program provides hands-on experience using Advanced Manufacturing techniques in our state-of-the-art shop which consists of 2,3,4 & 5 axis machines and a Zeiss coordinate measurement machine (CMM).
