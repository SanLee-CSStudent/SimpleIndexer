She and her three children are soon to be the proud homeowners of the 2019 UAB Habitat House located at 7031 2nd Avenue South in East Lake (zip 35206). UAB\u2019s sixth annual Habitat House Build runs September 26 \u2013 November 9, 2019. Through gifts to the UAB Benevolent Fund, employees contributed $50,000 to sponsor the house and will volunteer approximately 600 hours to build the house from the ground up. If you are interested in volunteering alongside Quaci and her family, please sign up through BlazerPulse! Quaci was born in Birmingham, is a graduate of Woodlawn High School and Mackie Brown College. Her goal is to return to school to become a nurse. Mykerya is a high school senior, makes good grades and loves talking on the phone. Robert, a high school junior, loves to play football, basketball and video games. Nardia is in middle school, works in the front office as a teacher\u2019s Aide, enjoys talking on the phone and playing outside. \u201COwning my own home will be the happiest moment in my life!\u201D said Naquaci. In the past 10 years, Greater Birmingham Habitat for Humanity has built 50+ houses for members of our UAB family. Visit Habitat\u2019s website for information on becoming a Habitat Homeowner.
