Important Information for your Housing Application All incoming students under the age of 19 prior to the first day of registration are required to reside on campus unless living at your stated home address with your parents. October 1 is the priority deadline for Spring applications and March 1 is the priority deadline for Fall applications. Applications received after those dates are placed on a wait-list for housing. It may take up to 48 hours to access the housing application once you are admitted to the University. The Fall 2022 application is now open. A $100 non-refundable application fee is required to apply for Housing. This fee can only be paid using a Visa or MasterCard debit\/credit card. No Gift Cards can be accepted.
