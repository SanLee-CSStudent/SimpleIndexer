The URAC is a recommending body, as such, shall serve in an advisory capacity to the Director and staff of University Recreation. Council Make up and Appointment The URAC will strive to have 60% (at least 51%) students with remaining members representing other membership eligible entities. Interested individuals will fill out URAC application expressing specific points of interest in joining and what they would bring to the council.
