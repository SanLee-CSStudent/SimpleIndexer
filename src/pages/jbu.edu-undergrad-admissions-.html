Login to your portal Admission requirements and process For Regular Admission, we require a 2.5 high school GPA (on a 4.0 scale) and either a 20 ACT, 1020 SAT or 66 CLT score. We do not score the writing portions of these tests. We also offer Test-Optional Admission. If you are a transfer student, please see the Transfer Admissions page for your unique admission requirements. We have a rolling application deadline for the fall semester. If you're applying for the spring semester, the deadline is December 1. We do not use a superscore (taking the best scores if students take the ACT or SAT multiple times) for acceptance. However, once accepted, we will calculate your superscore for academic scholarships.
