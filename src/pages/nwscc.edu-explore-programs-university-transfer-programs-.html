More Info Explore Programs All Programs Adult Education Career Technical Programs Dual Enrollment for Dual Credit Health Studies Special Programs University Transfer Programs General Education English Math Natural Sciences Computer Information Systems Virtual Learning Workforce Training University Transfer Programs Are you looking to get started toward your university degree without the financial burden? Northwest-Shoals Community College (NW-SCC) can help! You can start at NW-SCC and take the same core classes (English, math, science, history, speech, etc.) and save over 50% in tuition and fees you would pay at a four year college or university. NW-SCC offers a variety of degrees and programs in an attempt to meet the needs, interests, and abilities of our area students. NW-SCC offers academic programs of study leading to an Associate in Arts Degree, Associate in Science Degree, and Associate in Applied Science Degree. You can make your transfer process easier with the STARS transfer guide agreement. Create your customized guide TODAY! Associate\u2019s Degree Offered 100% Online NW-SCC offers students the ability to earn an Associate in Arts or Associate in Science degree completely online. These degrees prepare students to transfer to another institution to pursue a four-year degree. Visit the Virtual Learning page. Apply for NW-SCC Today Each semester NW-SCC offers a variety of online\/distance options as well as traditional classroom offerings. Use the course schedule view this semester\u2019s class offerings. Financial aid is available for eligible students. For More Information For more information on the academic transfer option at NW-SCC, contact our Advising Center office at (256) 331-5221 or send email to advising@nwscc.edu. The Advising Center is located in the Administration Building (Bldg. 100) of the Shoals campus in Muscle Shoals.
