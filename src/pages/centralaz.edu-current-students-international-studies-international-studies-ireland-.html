The lush Irish landscape is your backdrop to all the cultural, artistic and historic riches this country has to offer. Students will participate in a literature class during the spring semester, exploring stories, poetry & novels from around the world with a special focus on Irish authors, including W.B. Yeats. While in Ireland, students will be treated to daily lectures from Dr. Niamh Hamill of Institute of Study Abroad Ireland and excursions to historical sites to tie lessons to real-life experiences. This program takes place over Central Arizona College\u2019s spring break, so students will enjoy exciting outside activities, many of which are included in the program costs. Activities may include: Horseback Riding Surfing High-ropes Course Cliff Jumping Archery Plus, students may participate in a St. Patrick\u2019s Day parade with locals in the charming town of Bundoran. Our Irish hosts will also take students to Dublin for an overnight excursion in this magical, modern city. DATES: To Be Determined for 2021 REQUIRED COURSE: To Be Determined for 2021 Program Cost: TBD Program cost includes housing, ground transportation, most activities and meal in Ireland, plus trip\/cancellation insurance. Tuition (3 Credits): $258 In-state tuition, out-of-state tuition will be higher. Estimated Airfare: $1100 Airfare is estimated, students are responsible for booking and paying for airfare, but not until approved by International Studies. Recommended Pocket Money: 200 Euros Students will need spending money for personal items, some lunches and gratuities.
