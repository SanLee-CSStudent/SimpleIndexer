At Calhoun Community College, we prepare our students for competitive careers. Our goal is for our graduates to earn the credentials that match or exceed industry expectations and that they are prepared to be a productive employee for you. Please feel free to peruse our partnership opportunities. If you have any questions, please give us a call or click the \u201CContact Us\u201D button. On behalf of the Career Services department, we would like to thank you for choosing to conduct business in the Tennessee Valley and for partnering with Calhoun. Below you will find ways in which we work with employers to reach our students: Cooperative Learning Program \u2013 The Cooperative Learning Program is an opportunity for employers to build a talent pipeline while filling a current need and for students to gain valuable work experience in their field while earning college credit. hireCalhoun \u2013 A FREE online portal and the most efficient way for employers to reach students and alumni with job opportunities. You will find our Employer Guide which highlights all of the portal\u2019s features and instructions to create a hireCalhoun account. You can also use the \u201CResume Book\u201D feature to search through resumes to contact qualified candidates directly. Job Fairs \u2013 Join us for the opportunity to interact with hundreds of candidates. Career Chat Events \u2013 We can work to identify current students and\/or alumni who match the skill set you are seeking. From there, we invite them on campus to network with representatives from your company to discuss company culture, employment opportunities, and the hiring process.
