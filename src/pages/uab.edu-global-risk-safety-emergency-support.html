Emergencies Abroad: Even when using safe behavior, anyone can be a victim of a random accident or theft. Emergencies vary in magnitude and type, and for that, we enroll all UAB students in our International Travel Assistance Program so that you may get the attention you need abroad. It is important to know what steps to take in the event of an emergency.
