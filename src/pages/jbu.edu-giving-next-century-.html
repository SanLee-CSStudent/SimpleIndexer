Health Education Building & Nursing Endowment This $12 million project built the Health Education Building, a state-of-the-art nursing education facility and endowed a robust Bachelor's of Science in Nursing program. Simmons Great Hall This first-class banquet hall welcomes thousands of guests each year for everything from conferences to weddings as well as providing space for activities central to the core mission of JBU. Mayfield Hall Renovation This $6 million renovation upgraded this historic women's dormitory with modern rooms and bathrooms and increased sustainability with new HVAC and energy features. Northslope Apartments With the phasing out of the 60s-era Broadhurst Village, the $3 million Northslope Apartments fill the need for non-traditional housing options on campus. J. Alvin Brown Renovation The $5.5 million renovation of JBU's historic men's residence hall upgraded it to a modern dormitory while preserving the historic qualities of the building. Peer-Andrus Studio and Project Barn This new $3.25 million building provides nearly 17,000 sq. ft. of studio and project space for art and engineering students to expand their hands-on education opportunities. Sheridan Townhouses The $1 million renovation of the Sheridan Townhouses provides updated housing for up to 84 upperclassmen. Programs and Operating Support Over $33.5 million in gifts will go to JBU programs and organizations that play a significant role in shaping JBU culture and strengthening JBU's impact around the world. Endowment for Scholarships Over $13 million was received for new endowed scholarships and to boost previously endowed awards; helping keep Christian higher education affordable for students into perpetuity. Annual Scholarships The $29.4 million raised for annual scholarships provides direct aid for students now, creating an immediate, positive impact. Endowment for Academic Excellence Over $10 million in gifts toward the endowment for a variety of projects will enhance the academic excellence of JBU faculty and programs. Estate Gifts for the Future This is the first JBU campaign to include a goal for estate gifts to help offset the cost of JBU\u2019s mission in our next century.
