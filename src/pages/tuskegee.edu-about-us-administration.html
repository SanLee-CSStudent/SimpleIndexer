Administration ADMINISTRATION Daily operation of the university is under the purview of the university president, who serves as the institution's chief executive officer. The president is assisted in these responsibilities by senior administrators who oversee functional areas that include academics and instruction, student services and campus life, research and outreach, marketing and communications, fiscal and business management, legal affairs, institutional advancement, and human resources management. Click here for the Presidential Appearance Request form. Take a look at our Organizational Chart. Overall governance of the university falls to its Board of Trustees, which establishes the university\u2019s organization and structure, and which delegates operational authority to the university president.
