Focused on the role employees play in managing UAB\u2019s financial resources, the course will take approximately 15 minutes or fewer to complete. Beginning this year, the training will be required annually. If assigned the course, employees will receive an email from the UAB Learning System with a direct link to participate; the deadline for completion is June 30.
