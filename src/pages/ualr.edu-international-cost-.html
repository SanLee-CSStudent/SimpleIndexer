Estimated Cost of Attendance Total costs will vary based on educational level, number of courses and personal living expenses. The Office of International Student Services calculates the following estimate based on the minimum number of credit hours required by immigration regulations. Tuition and fees are subject to change without notice. Estimated Cost of Attendance per Academic Year Category IELP Undergraduate Graduate Law Tuition and Fees $14,400 $15000 $14000 $17400 Living Expenses $12000 $9000 $9000 $9000 Health Insurance, Books, and Miscellaneous $1,450 $5000 $4000 $3600 Total Estimate $27850 $29000 $27000 $30000 All international students are considered as non-resident for tuition purposes. Costs specific to international students include the $150 International student fee per semester and the $755 insurance fee per 6 months. The university reserves the right to change the cost of tuition and fees at any time if such action is deemed necessary. Proof of financial support for the estimates above are required for F-1 and J-1 students to receive the Form I-20 or DS-2019 (respectively). Dependent Forms I-20 or DS-2019 require additional funding ($4500 for spouse, $2500 per child). Contact Office of International Student Services University of Arkansas at Little Rock 2801 S.
