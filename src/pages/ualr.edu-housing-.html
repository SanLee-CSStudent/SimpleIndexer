UA Little Rock\u2019s Campus Living is a community where you can live, learn, and grow. When you live on campus, you\u2019ll get to know your classmates better as you study and attend classes together, go to campus events, and learn more about life as a Trojan. Plus, you\u2019ll have the convenience of being just steps away from your classes and campus resources. Live & Learn On Campus Our on-campus housing options include private rooms, suites, apartments, and pod-style living. We offer full kitchens, laundry facilities, WiFi, a theatre, study rooms, reserved parking, and even an outdoor pool. All bedrooms come furnished with a bed, desk, and dresser and all residents participate in Campus Dining. If you\u2019d like to check out any of our residences, please schedule a tour through the Office of Admissions. You can also contact us if you have any questions.
