The Bulldog Mail Center, Samford University\u2019s state-of-the-art mail and package center, is now open. The retail service center, operated by Ricoh, is located at the first floor of the University Center, adjacent to the food court. It offers cutting-edge services, expanded hours of operation, and an upgraded retail space, including automated electronic notifications for letters and packages, shipping options using all major carriers, as well as mailing and shipping supplies. Credit cards and Bulldog Bucks are accepted. Services Whether you need to send a package or print a picture, the Bulldog Mail Center can help with the following services and printing options. Mail Services First-class mail Priority mail Campus mail drop-off Shipping Services USPS FedEx UPS In addition to our mail and shipping services, customers may purchase envelopes, padded mailers and boxes from The Bulldog Mail Center. Printing The Bulldog Mail Center offers the following quick print options: Black-and-white copies Color copies Picture printing from the Kodak kiosk Hours of Operation The Bulldog Mail Center is open Monday through Thursday from 8 a.m. to 6 p.m., Friday from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Special arrangements may be made to pick up mail outside of normal operating hours by contacting 205-726-2933. Mailbox Numbers All undergraduate students are assigned a campus box number, which serves as their official university mailing address. Box numbers can be found in the Samford Portal under personal information. Receiving Mail Samford mail is no longer stored in mailboxes. In order to pick up mail after you have been notified, visit the Bulldog Mail Center, then swipe your Samford ID card at one of the kiosks located at the entrance and your mail will be retrieved for you. Sending Mail to a Samford University Student To send mail to an undergraduate student at Samford University, you may use either of the following formats: Student\u2019s Full Name 800 Lakeshore Drive Box 29XXXX Birmingham, AL 35229 Student\u2019s Full Name 800 Lakeshore Drive 29XXXX Birmingham, AL 35229 Forwarding Mail Students who are not enrolled at Samford University during Jan Term or summer terms must leave forwarding addresses in the Bulldog Mail Center prior to departure.
