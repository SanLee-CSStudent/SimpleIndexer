News News A- A+ NACC's Summer Semester Begins May 31st May 23, 2022 Pictured are NACC students enjoying an afternoon on campus. Registration is underway and the beginning of NACC's summer 2022 semester is just around the corner. Summer courses will begin at Northeast on Tuesday, May 31, 2022. Students may register until Wednesday, June 1st. Current NACC students should register through the registration process. Students may make an appointment with their advisor to register using AdvisorTrac, by phone\/email or may register in person with their advisor. Instructions for using AdvisorTrac are located on the Academic Advising page. Once students have been advised by their advisor, students can register with their advisor or online using their OneACCS account. Dual Enrollment Students must register with their advisor, Staci Miller, and can email dualenrollment@nacc.edu. Transient Students should contact the Admissions Office at admisions@nacc.edu. First-time students must complete an Application for Admission; send a high school transcript or present evidence of GED; complete orientation and take the ACCUPLACER placement test. Once completed, students can register through the registration process. Semester kickoff will begin Thursday, May 26th, and will continue through Wednesday, June 1st. Students will have the opportunity to purchase books, secure a parking pass and a Student ID, and visit offices such as Financial Aid if necessary.
