CDL Truck Driving Programs of Study A- A+ Health Science Division\/Nursing Mathematics Division Skills Training Summer Bridge Program July 11-22, 2022 Interest Form CDL Truck Driving OSHA WorkKeys Non-Credit Workshops Business and Computer Science Division English and Fine Arts Division Natural Sciences Division Social Sciences, Speech and Foreign Languages Division Tech Programs Athens State University at NACC Academic Maps Class Schedule Catalog The CDL Truck Driving Program at Northeast Alabama Community College will prepare you to safely and effectively operate a tractor trailer and pass the commercial driver's license (CDL) exam. The 160-hour program runs for four weeks. Education verifications please email georgej@nacc.edu or fax 256-228-3309 Program Entry Requirements Valid driver license At least 21 years of age for a Class A CDL Successfully pass a DOT physical Successfully pass a chain-of-custody drug screen Present an acceptable Motor Vehicle Report (MVR) Expected Expenses Payable to the College Before Class Starts Tuition $3,000 Payable to Outside Sources Before Class Starts DOT Physical & Drug Screen $175 (approximate) Motor Vehicle Report $5.75 Payable to Various Vendors During Class CDL Written Test $26 CDL Learner\u2019s Permit $38 Certification Exam $300 (not including $100 for retake, per test) CDL Driver\u2019s License $68.90 (Class A) CDL Endorsements $15.60 (each\/ 5 possible) Estimated Total Program Cost $3,630 Registration Steps Step 1 \u2013 Funding Payment is due before the first week of course. This program is funded with Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act (WIOA). To see if you qualify for WIOA funding, please contact your local Alabama Career Center. Tell them you are interested in attending the CDL at Northeast Alabama Community College. It is imperative that you follow through with all the career center requirements. Once you have been screened as WIOA-eligible, apply online (see link below) or at the NACC Skills Training Office. If you are not WIOA-eligible, you may proceed with the program by paying the required tuition and fees. Employer's seeking information, contact the Skills Training Office at georgej@nacc.edu or 256-638-4418, ext. 2214. Step 2 \u2013 Apply to Program Program Application Link. Must have a working email address for enrollment purposes and communication. Step 3 \u2013 Attend an Orientation Session Orientation sessions are requried. At this orientation, you will learn the steps necessary for you to secure your seat in the training program. At the orientation session you will receive a list of requirements and tenative class start date. You must submit all items to the Skills Training Office at least seven days (but no more than 21 days) prior to your class start date. Bring the following items with you: Social Security Card Driver\u2019s License Pre-course items (if already obtained- permit, or following requirements) Step 4 \u2013 Complete Pre-Course Requirements These items must be completed at least seven days before but no more than 21 days prior to the first day of class. Long Form DOT Physical - Click this link to find a certified DOT examiner near you. Chain-of-Custody Drug Screen Motor Vehicle Report \u2013Obtain a copy of your Motor Vehicle Report (MVR). Driver records may be purchased through the mail using the Driver Record Request, using a debit\/ credit card by phone at 334-242-4241, or in person at Department of Public Safety, 1115-A Church Street, Huntsville, AL 35801 (256-239-0682). Supply your physical and proof of drug screen and MVR report by email to georgej@nacc.edu or at orientaion but no later than 12:00 noon on the Friday before your class begins the following week. If all items required for your class are not received by that time your slot in the class will be forfeited. You will incur the other following expenses during training: CDL Written Test $26 CDL Learner's Permit $38 CDL Driver's License $68.90 All CDL students and instructors are subject to random drug screens. 2022 Orientation Schedule All orientations location given with online registration.
