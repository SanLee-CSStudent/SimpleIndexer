Field of Interest There are many rewarding careers, but it might be hard to top the feeling a real estate agent gets when connecting people with their new home. Plus, your workplace doesn\u2019t have to be an office \u2014 it can be someone\u2019s new home. And while a future in real estate and real estate appraisal can be challenging, it can also be lucrative \u2014 and we can help you get started. If you plan on entering this worthwhile profession, start with our Real Estate and Real Estate Appraisal program. You\u2019ll get an in-depth look at real estate principles, practices, appraisals, and sales so you\u2019ll be ready to hit the housing market. Upon successful program completion, you will be prepared to take the next steps to become a real estate agent or a certified or licensed residential appraiser. Start your career in real estate today. The Right Pathway for You MCC offers Certificates of Completion and Associate Degrees for this program. Your interests and goals will guide you in selecting the pathway that is ideal for you. You may find a combination of certificates or degrees is the fastest way to realize your dreams. Certificates The knowledge and skills acquired while earning a Certificate of Completion (CCL) can help launch you into a new career or energize your current job. Generally requiring fewer credit hours than degrees or transfer programs, a CCL may be your best first step into higher education.
