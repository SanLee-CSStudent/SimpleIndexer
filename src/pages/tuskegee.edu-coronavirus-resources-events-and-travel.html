Events and Travel Events and Travel Intramurals and Recreation Intramurals and Recreation will continue online connections through social media, Zoom, Blackboard, Campus Labs and other technologies to promote and encourage health and hygiene, diet and nutrition, physical activity and exercise. We're excited to maintain direct contact through email and text messages. Our program will provide the following services regularly during the week: Monday Madness: stress management and relaxation techniques, such as online yoga and meditation, health and hygiene tips, and healthy meal recipe contests. Wednesday Hump Day: online workouts, including online aerobics classes, American Muscle Fitness with Denzel Veal, and healthy snack recipe contests. Friday Feeling Normal: \u201CThank God it's Friday\u201D devotional workouts, online yoga and meditation, \u201Ccount your steps\u201D (take a walk), online games and activities, and healthy smoothie recipe contests. If you need help connecting with any of these programs, email studentaffairs@tuskegee.edu. TU Fall Sports On July 11, 2020, President McNair released a statement regarding the suspension of Tuskegee University Fall Sports. Virtual Student Activities The Office of Student Life will host a variety of virtual student activities on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram. Student Life will continue weekly call-ins with SGA on Tuesdays at 6 p.m. \u2014 so, if you have concerns or ideas to share, contact your SGA leadership on Twitter and Instagram. Stay tuned for our SGA Virtual Town Hall and our weekly Student Life Success Series \u2014 both coming in April. We will also host weekly Instagram shout-outs to fraternities and sororities, the Royal Court, SGA and the International Students Association. Join us for some of our upcoming virtual events: Fried Chicken Wednesdays between 11 a.m. and 1 p.m. central time each week \u2014 share your pics, tag us and use #TUVFCW in your posts. Fun Fridays each week \u2014 upload pics of yourself in your favorite Tuskegee or organization apparel. Contact Ladrena Taylor, administrative assistant, Student Life at (334) 727-8155 or ltaylor@tuskegee.edu for more information on these virtual student activities.
