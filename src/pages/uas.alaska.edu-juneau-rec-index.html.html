Campus recreation at The University of Alaska Southeast offers diverse recreational programs that promote and facilitate physical fitness and an active student life. About the REC The UAS Recreation Center is your source for recreation on the Juneau campus. The REC offers a weight room, rock climbing wall, studio, and a suspended running track with an assortment of cardio equipment that overlooks a full court gym. Recreation happens both at the REC and outside in our beautiful Alaska environment. Rent outdoor equipment at the REC, from lake kayaks and paddle boards to x-country skis and snowshoes in the winter. Try a change in venue and study in our lounge before or after exercise.
