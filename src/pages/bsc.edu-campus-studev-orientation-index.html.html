Congratulations on your acceptance to Birmingham-Southern College! We are thrilled you have chosen to join the Birmingham-Southern community and can't wait to have you on the Hilltop soon! During the summer, you will have the chance to attend Summer Orientation where you can meet other new students, get your Fall class schedule, meet with an academic advisor, learn more about BSC, and get excited about life as a college student at Birmingham-Southern. In August, all new students will attend Fall Orientation, an experience where you will learn more about campus life, help you get ready for academic life on the Hilltop, and start to build community with your classmates. Summer Orientation 2022 Dates: First-year students will attend one of these dates: June 10, 11, or 17. Transfer students will attend July 18. Dates are subject to change. If you have specific questions please contact Dana Bekurs at [email protected] . Summer Orientation for both freshmen and transfer students is a one-day experience that will run from 8 am to 4 pm. We hope this site will provide all necessary information and instructions to prepare you for orientation and your first year at Birmingham-Southern College.
