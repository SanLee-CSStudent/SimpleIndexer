Programs of Study Programs of Study A- A+ Health Science Division\/Nursing Mathematics Division Business and Computer Science Division English and Fine Arts Division Natural Sciences Division Skills Training Social Sciences, Speech and Foreign Languages Division Tech Programs Athens State University at NACC Academic Maps Class Schedule Catalog 2021-2022 Programs of Study TRANSFER SUCCESS GUIDES Students who plan to transfer from Northeast Alabama Community College to a 4-year college or university will earn an Associate in Arts (AA) or Associate in Science (AS) degree. You may obtain the first two years of a four-year degree at Northeast in almost any field of study. The success guides are located here. The Northeast POS have been developed after a careful analysis of the 4-year college requirements to which most students transfer. All students planning to transfer, however, must obtain a STARS Guide which will show a detailed list of courses to be taken at NACC. Your academic advisor will help you with this and for further assistance, please contact Holly Williams, the Transfer Advisor, at extension 2372 or transfer@nacc.edu or visit her office in the Wallace Administration Building, Rm 112. Transfer of Credit Policies and Articulation Agreements CAREER\/TECH (NON-TRANSFER) SUCCESS GUIDES NACC offers many programs of study that are geared toward getting you ready for a career as quickly as possible. The Associate in Applied Science is a two-year degree in a specific field such as nursing (ADN and PN), engineering technology, drafting and design technology, emergency medical services, medical assistant technology, industrial systems technology, office administration, computer information systems, welding, child development, criminal justice, machine tool technology, salon and spa management, industrial electronics technology, cosmetology, and business management and supervision. Most of these programs have short-term (STC) certificate and certificate (CER) programs available. For more information about these career\/tech programs contact Seferina Valey at extension 2254 or valeys@nacc.edu or visit her office in Rm 148 Pendley Administration Building.
