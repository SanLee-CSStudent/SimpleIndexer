College Preview Days are scheduled in the fall and spring semesters to provide prospective high school juniors, seniors, parents, high school teachers, and administrators to visit campus. The day\u2019s activities include interactive breakout sessions with faculty in our academic programs. Information will be shared about student activities, financial aid, scholarship opportunities, and the admission process. We will wrap up with lunch at our new health and wellness center SEARK@Seabrook, where students will have the opportunity to play video games, check out the fitness room, play basketball or just hang out. You must be registered to attend. Click here https:\/\/seark.elluciancrmrecruit.com\/Apply\/Events to create an account and register for the event. For more information, contact the SEARK Recruitment Team at recruitment@seark.edu.
