Dean, College of Human Sciences Vision The College of Human Sciences is a dynamic, challenging learning environment where students become professionally competent, globally aware, and socially engaged as they develop into 21st century leaders. This academic paradigm that reaches beyond the classroom to open students' minds and broaden world views, differentiates CHS graduates in the global marketplace. It further enables them to positively impact the health and well-being of individuals, families, and consumers in pursuit of a sustainable world. CHS students and faculty will excel in teaching, research, and outreach locally and internationally, thus elevating the reputation of Auburn University with the College of Human Sciences setting the quality standards by which competing programs are measured.
