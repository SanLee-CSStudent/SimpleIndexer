Campus The South Campus is home to most general education and allied health courses as well as the offices of admissions, financial aid, IT services, registrar, safety, student support and veterans\u2019 affairs. The advising center, bookstore, library and president\u2019s office also are located on the South Campus. An 11,000-square-foot, $3 million Allied Health Addition opened in 2010 on the south campus. The new area houses classrooms and labs for medical laboratory technology, surgical technology, and nursing, plus a computer lab for allied health students. The Blue & You Foundation Health Education Simulation Center opened its doors at Northark's South Campus Allied Health Wing in 2017. The center provides a realistic hospital-type setting for students to learn and enhance their critical thinking and clinical decision-making skills. The Bradley Student Center offers a \u201Cone-stop-shop\u201D for student services. It also provides an updated, more comfortable student lounge, including the College Grill, as a place for students to relax between classes. In 2014, the Science Building, which houses two labs and a 60-seat auditorium-style classroom, opened its doors. The Bill Baker Amphitheater is home to several events including the Summer Movie Series and concerts. The college\u2019s gymnasium, Pioneer Pavilion, baseball and softball fields and a disc golf course are also at the South Campus.
