Organizations \/ Phi Theta Kappa \/ PTK Officer Duties Officer Duties To become an officer, you must be an active member who aids in chapter activities such as Honors in Action, College Project, and scheduled officer meetings. Once an officer position is vacant, the officer team will vote to promote the active member to officer status with the advisor\u2019s consent and approval. You must be a member of the Beta Phi Phi Chapter, in good standing with Phi Theta Kappa, and maintain a 3.2 or higher GPA to be eligible. Don\u2019t be discouraged if you do not understand some to the responsibilities associated with each of the positions. The goal is to learn and grow as leaders. There is a PTK Chapter Advisor guiding and helping you every step of the way. So take a look and decide take your membership one step further and become more involved as a PTK Officer.
