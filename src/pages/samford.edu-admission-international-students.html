Samford is a global community with students from 29 countries. We offer support from the application process and throughout your Samford experience. You will find information below on the application checklist, activities at Samford and more. Have questions? Email Andrew Maines at amaines@samford.edu. Undergraduate Applicant (Freshman) Application Checklist Completed Undergraduate Application Official government documentation (passport, visa) Documentation for financial support (bank statement, minimum balance $42,120) Personal essay Letter of recommendation Official high school transcript The following is required for transcripts in a language other than English: Translated and notarized transcript A \"Course by Course Evaluation\" by either WES or ECE) English proficiency tests: TOEFL\/TOEFL iBT (Test Code 1302). Competitive admission will be offered to students with the following minimum scores: iBT: 90 or Paper: 575 IELTS: Favorable admission will be offered to students with a minimum score of 6.5. ACT\/SAT scores (student may request waiver) Undergraduate Applicant (Transfer) Application Checklist Completed Undergraduate Application Official transcripts from every college\/university attended and\/or currently attending.
