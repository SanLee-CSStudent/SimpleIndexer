Students must meet all of the following requirements to be automatically considered for this award upon admission to UAB: Graduate from a secondary or high school (up to 2 years prior to intake) with a minimum grade point average of 3.0\/4.0 Have not enrolled in post-secondary work Anticipate holding an F-1 visa The examined GPA will be the cumulative GPA at the time of admission. All scholarships are awarded on a first-come, first-served basis as funds are available and are renewable for a total of four years (or eight academic semesters). Students who are receiving governmental scholarships or other external support are not eligible to receive this award. Scholarship Eligibility Deadlines Students must be accepted to UAB by the following deadlines to be considered for scholarships: April 1 for the June intake May 1 for the August intake November 1 for the January intake What About Additional UAB Scholarships? UAB is pleased to offer IB Scholarships, departmental scholarships, performance scholarships, and more. Students interested in these scholarships should complete the general scholarship application in the Blazer Scholarship Management and Resource Tool (BSMART). Completing the general application submits student information to many departments on campus for scholarship consideration. Scholarships in BSMART will be posted on September 1, but students can complete the general application beginning August 1. Important: Some scholarships may require a supplemental application, resume, or letters of recommendation in addition to the general application. Deadlines for these requirements vary and can be found in BSMART.
