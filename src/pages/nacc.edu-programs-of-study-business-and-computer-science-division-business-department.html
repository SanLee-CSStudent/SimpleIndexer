The division also offers an Associate in Applied Science degree in Business Management and Supervision. We offer courses in Accounting, Economics, Statistics, Management, Finance, Retailing, Marketing, and Salesmanship. The Business Department is proud to sponsor NACC\u2019s Rotaract Club. They are involved in many campus and community activities. It is a service-based organization sponsored by the Scottsboro Rotary Club. If you are interested in joining please see Haley Johnson or Keith McBride for more information.
