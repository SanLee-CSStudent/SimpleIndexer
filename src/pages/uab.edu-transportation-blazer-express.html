Frequently Asked Questions: I left a personal item on the bus. How can I get it back? Call us at 205-934-3514 and we will try to retrieve it for you. We will need your name, phone number, item description, and the bus route you were riding. You will need to present a photo ID when you retrieve your item should we be able to locate it. Who can ride the bus? UAB students, employees, and authorized visitors can ride the Blazer Express by showing their valid UAB ID or visitor pass. Is there a cost to ride the bus? No. Blazer Express is a fare-free transit system for UAB students, employees, and authorized visitors. How many bus routes are there? There are six bus routes. The routes are marked by color code -- Blue, Green North, Green South, Gold, Silver, and Purple. Where are the bus stops located? There are 30+ bus stop locations strategically placed throughout the campus. Most buildings on campus are less than a five-minute walk from a bus stop. View route map. Are the buses wheelchair accessible? Yes. All Blazer Express buses comply with the Americans with Disabilities Act accessibility standards. Will the buses operate during official UAB holidays? Visit uab.edu\/transportation or follow us @UABTransport at Facebook and Twitter for updates on schedules. Where is the bus depot terminal? The Blazer Express bus depot is located in Zone C at 608 8th Street South. The depot is the small stand-alone building with the green awning near the 7th Avenue South entrance. What must I have to board a bus? Passengers must present a valid UAB ID badge or authorization card. Passengers with a valid UAB ID are allowed to bring guests on the bus. How do I contact the UAB Blazer Express Safety Escort Service? Call 205-934-8772 or visit the UAB Safety Escort Page. What are the operational hours of the Blazer Express transit system? More information may be found on the Routes tab. How can I view the bus routes in real-time on my computer? View a bus map using your browser. How can I get the smartphone app so I can view the buses in real time on my phone? Download the DoubleMap app for Android or iPhone and select \u201CUniversity of Alabama at Birmingham\u201D from the list of systems.
