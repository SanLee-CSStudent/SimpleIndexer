Our standard of service to students is embodied in SEARK\u2019s Vision Statement: \u201CEmpowering Students and Changing Lives\u201D. As an educational institution invested in the success of our students, we recognize our responsibility in fostering positive change not just on our campus, but throughout our entire community. Our highly qualified, dedicated faculty members continuously demonstrate their passion for student learning, providing modern curriculum, and innovative teaching methods. Our enthusiastic, professional staff members help ensure you receive all the information and support services you need throughout your educational journey at SEARK College. We are especially proud of our friendly, inclusive environment where you can be yourself and feel safe to learn and grow during your time with us. As a first-generation college student, I know how intimidating it can be to take that first step on a college campus. Rest assured, at SEARK College, we are committed to offering support both inside and outside the classroom. We will be with you every step during your educational journey! Please know we are here to serve you. Our faculty and staff have a goal of \u201CEngaging Every Student.\u201D Whatever your needs, we want to work with you on an individual basis to be successful. We could not be happier to have you as a part of the SEARK family. If I can personally be of service, please feel free to contact my office. Welcome to Southeast Arkansas College!
