The Central Arizona College Police Department (CACPD) is a fully functioning police department and is here to serve our students, employees, and members of the community. Our department consists of certified Arizona Peace Officer Standards and Training (AZPOST) sworn police officers. Our mission is to serve the CAC community with professionalism, integrity, and fairness by working closely with students, faculty, and staff in a cooperative effort to make all CAC campuses safe to help enhance the learning environment. As the Chief of Police, I am committed to a proactive approach to relationship building, community policing, enforcement, crime prevention, networking, and collaborating with area law enforcement partners and our CAC team.The CACPD will continue to evaluate ourselves, develop strategies to meet the security and safety needs for CAC, and enhance partnerships with a team-first philosophy. Please feel free to reach out to myself or a member of CACPD if assistance is needed. Sincerely, Gregory L.
