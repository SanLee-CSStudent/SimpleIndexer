New Associate in Arts (AA) Degree, Emphasis in Counseling and Applied Psychological Science MCC now offers an Associate in Arts with Emphasis in Counseling and Applied Psychological Science degree. The new program provides the first two years of a four-year curriculum for students wishing to specialize in counseling and applied psychological science or who are preparing for graduate programs in counseling or related fields. The curriculum includes a foundation in counseling theories, counseling skills, career and lifespan development, ethics and research methods and multicultural competence.
