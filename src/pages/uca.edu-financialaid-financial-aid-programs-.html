Financial Aid Mission Statement In support of the university mission statement, the Office of Student Financial Aid is dedicated to assisting our diverse student population in accessing aid programs to achieve their education goals. The office strives to remove financial barriers for prospective and current student by providing financial resources in a positive and professional manner while complying with federal, state and institutional guidelines.
