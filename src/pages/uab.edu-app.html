UAB better. Everything you need, all in one place. The new UAB mobile app is built to help you get around, get hired and get things done - whether you're a student, employee or a guest. UAB is a big place, and everyone has different needs. Students want their classes, assignments and deadlines front and center. Staff need to access crucial reports on the go. Visitors are looking to find their way around, and discover what sets UAB apart. The redesigned app lets you select profiles that get you all the information you need - on mobile, tablet and desktop. ... Forget FOMO You'll never again have to fear you're missing out on something around UAB. We've added a tile to keep students informed on upcoming events happening on campus. Click on the specific event for more detailed information, or tap \"More Events...\" at the bottom of the tile to instantly view the full calendar. Canvas Integration Get quick, up-to-date information about each of your courses*, and direct access to your full Canvas profile and assignments. The app also gives you a live look at your class schedule, so you\u2019ll always know where you need to be next. *Up to 4 of your favorited Canvas courses will appear on the live tile. Improve the App What can we add to the UAB app to make your life easier? We want to hear voices from all around campus. Go to uab.edu\/appideas to submit new feature ideas for the app. Then log back in later to check the review progress. Make Connections Looking for someone around campus? The updated directory for students, faculty and staff will get you the information you need, from email addresses to office numbers. Not sure where to start? Plug some keywords into the app\u2019s built-in search feature and it will automatically find the most appropriate information. Social Get a live look at what\u2019s happening on campus with feeds for Instagram, Twitter and more. Find It Fast Our new map gives you the fastest route to any campus location, from buildings to athletics facilities to parking. Trying to find an administrative office? Highlight buildings that only deal with administration. New to campus? Scroll through a handy category list to find dining options, campus services, student buildings and more. UAB 101 Select the Guest profile in the app for a detailed visitor\u2019s guide to UAB and beyond. Quick tips help you navigate campus, login to UAB wifi, find parking and contact information and explore all Birmingham has to offer. What\u2019s Happening in Sports Keep up to date with the latest news and sports headlines from across campus. Stories are organized by categories so you can focus on the news that\u2019s most important to you. Location & Hours Wondering what time the Rec Center closes tonight? Looking for the closest library for a study session? Searching for today\u2019s menu at The Commons? Everything you need to know is all together in one place. Admin Systems Check your payroll information, find forms and locate key reports. Faculty and staff can get quick access to all UAB Administrative Systems databases \u2014 including Banner, Oracle, and IRAP \u2014 no matter where you are. Desktop Version Are you on your desktop or laptop? You can now access the app without having to pick up your phone. Just go to m.uab.edu in your browser and find the same personalized features and functionality that you\u2019re used to on your smartphone or tablet. BlazerNET Find your advisor, search exam calendars and check your financial status with the direct BlazerNET integration. Stay Safe An emergency plan is built into the app to provide detailed instructions for critical situations such as severe weather, building evacuations, power outages or medical emergencies. Get Hired Find an internship, part-time position or a new career with a one-touch link to HireABlazer, UAB\u2019s comprehensive jobs portal. Tell Us What You Think We\u2019re always looking to improve the app. If there is anything that could be better, or new features you\u2019d like to see, please let us know. The app has several feedback choices built in \u2014 make your voice heard! ...and more ... Need further assistance?
