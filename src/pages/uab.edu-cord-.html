CORD offers grade K-12 students and teachers from all areas in-depth, hands-on, inquiry-based science experiences both during the school year and in our more intense summer programs. Students experience these programs at their Birmingham area schools, at CORD\u2019s GENEius and LabWorks classrooms at the McWane Science Center and in CORD\u2019s laboratory classrooms at UAB. CORD offices are located on the fifth floor of the Community Health Services Building 19 (CHSB19), 933 Nineteenth Street South, Birmingham, Alabama 35294-2041; Phone (205) 934-5171; Fax (205) 934-5158; Email: ccord@uab.edu.
