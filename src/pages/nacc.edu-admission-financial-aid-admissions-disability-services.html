The college is committed to providing reasonable accommodations in accordance with the Americans with Disabilities Act of 1990, Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, and all other applicable regulations. Special needs or requests for assistance beyond what can be readily provided by the point-of-contact personnel should be directed to the ADA Compliance Coordinator. Leslie Reyes, ADA Compliance Coordinator Student Center, room 115 256-638-4418 ext. 2222 reyesl@nacc.edu Steps to Receiving Reasonable Accommodations 1. Please contact the ADA Compliance Coordinator to self-identify as a student with a disability and to schedule your intake appointment. Leslie Reyes, ADA Compliance Coordinator Student Center room 115 256-638-4418 ext. 2222 reyesl@nacc.edu 2. Complete the \"Accommodations Request\" packet. Packets may be requested by: Email or Mail - please call 256-638-4418 ext. 2222 or email Leslie Reyes at reyesl@nacc.edu to request a packet be emailed or mailed to you Pick up - stop by the Admissions Office in the Student Center, room 115, to request a packet Northeast Alabama Community College has adopted and internal ADA Compliant Process providing for prompt and equitable resolution of any complaints.
