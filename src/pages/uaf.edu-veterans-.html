UAF support for military, veterans and their families UAF offers a variety of academic programs, scholarships and waivers to veterans and eligible dependents, including in-state\/resident tuition for all active duty service members, veterans and their dependents. Military-affiliated spouses and dependents may also qualify for certain VA education benefits. Learn more about which VA education benefits you and your family can use Military pricing Use tuition assistance to cover the full cost of your education, with little to no additional fees or expenses. UAF's discount military tuition pricing eliminates most out-of-pocket expenses, offering both in-person and online undergraduate courses for just $250 per credit hour. Learn more about military pricing Tuition assistance (active duty service members, spouses and national guard) Tuition assistance offers eligible members of the U.S. Armed Forces $250 per credit hour (with a maximum yearly benefit of $4,500 per year or 16 credit hours). Active members of the Army and Air Force Reserve members and Army National Guard can receive up to $250 per credit hour towards undergraduate or graduate tuition up to $4500 for Federal Tuition Assistance and up to an additional 12 hours each semester using State Tuition Assistance. Alaska Air National Guard can receive State Tuition Assistance up to 12 hours each semester.
