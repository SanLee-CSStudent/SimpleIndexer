We work with students, alumni, and employers daily to bridge the gap between education\/training and employment. Our staff is available to help you at the beginning of your college career, throughout your time as a college student, and after you complete your credentials. We are always here to help you at any stage of your education and career. Our knowledgeable, dedicated staff is here to assist and encourage students, whether they have yet to enter college, or they have already selected a major and need some guidance. Our EMSI Career Coach software is an incredibly valuable tool, enabling you to take a career assessment and find job opportunities in our area. If you have questions about what the College and Career Planning Center can do for you, one of our staff will gladly assist you.
