NPC's Early College Programs Jump-Start Your College Degree at NPC! NPC\u2019s Early College Programs Department oversees all dual and concurrent enrollment partnership programs with our local school districts. We offer a diverse range of course subject, course modality, and program degree\/certificate pathways. Students 14 years and older can apply! Best part of it: FREE COLLEGE TUITION! Students are able to participate in Early College Programs and incur little to no cost to earn real college credits. LEARN MORE ABOUT OUR DIFFERENT DUAL AND CONCURRENT ENROLLMENT PROGRAMS! Dual Enrollment Dual Enrollment: courses taught by your qualified high school teachers. Counts towards both high school graduation and college degree requirements! NAVIT NAVIT: direct-to-work training programs through the Northern Arizona Vocational Institute of Technology. Counts towards both high school graduation and college degree requirements! TALON TALON: unique technology-enabled active learning classrooms housed within your high school. Counts towards both high school graduation and college degree requirements! Early College Waiver Early College Waiver: looking to take classes outside of what\u2019s offered in your high school? NPC's Early College Waiver allows for you to take additional courses to help you work towards your degree faster. REGISTRATION STEPS: Set academic plan with EC Advisor. Work with high school counselor Dual Enrollment and TALON participation. Reach out to your high school CTE Director or Career Tech for participation in NAVIT courses. Review dates for registration on the academic calendar. Register through MyNPC. Register for STU018 Early College Pre-session course! Order textbooks (**Some high schools cover the cost of textbooks, verify with your high school guidance counseling team) Visit NPC's Online Bookstore!
