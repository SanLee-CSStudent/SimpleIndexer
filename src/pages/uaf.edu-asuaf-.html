ASUAF gives students an opportunity to develop leadership skills while advocating for all UAF students. All UAF students who pay the consolidated fee are members of the association. While ASUAF technically refers to the entire association and its membership which encompasses almost every student at UAF, in colloquial use, ASUAF typically refers to the student government officials of ASUAF. ASUAF's constitution establishes a government of ASUAF, consisting of \"a President and Vice President elected by the student body on the same ticket, a Senate elected by the student body, and a Chairperson of the Senate elected by the Senate.\" Join ASUAF Getting involved with ASUAF is easy and there are positions available for all levels of leadership experience, time commitment and interest. In addition to elected positions, ASUAF appoints students to serve on a number of boards and committees. To learn more about current openings, head to our Join ASUAF page.
