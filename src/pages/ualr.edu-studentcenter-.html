Services & Programs DSC Help Desk Submit a Request Located at the heart of the campus, the Donaghey Student Center (DSC) supports UA Little Rock in its dedication to development, service, and community. The DSC provides facilities and services unique to university life. It is one of the few buildings in the nation that combines a traditional student center with a fitness and aquatics center. The Fitness and Aquatic Center also houses central Arkansas\u2019s only wheelchair-accessible Olympic-sized pool. The DSC also houses the Trojan Cafe & Food Court, the Information Desk, Conference Services, the UALR Bookstore, the Disability Resource Center, Health Services, the Student Experience office, and the Office of the Vice Chancellor for the Division of Student Affairs and Dean of Students. We invite all students, faculty, and alumni to explore the many activities, programs, and services that are available within the Donaghey Student Center.
