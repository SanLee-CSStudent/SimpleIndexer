Mathematics Military Science Pre-Health Professions Physics Minor Requirements Physics Courses Pre-Engineering Math & Science Faculty Computer Science Major Computer Science is a rapidly changing and exciting field. The major focuses on the foundations of the computer sciences, including areas such as software and hardware design, mathematical foundations of computer science, and complexity of computation. The program is designed to give students an enduring foundation for future professional growth. It blends theory and practice into a learning experience that gives students the capability to apply computer and information systems technology to a wide range of disciplines. 100 % of Computer Science majors are employed or in grad school within 6 months of graduation 12 :1 student-to-teacher ratio named a Best College for Social Mobility by U.S. News and World Report Meet the Faculty Details Courses Careers Admissions Internships Scholarships Why study Computer Science at Lyon College? Being a good computer scientist is more than just having technical competence. Employers want professionals with excellent oral and written communication skills and the ability to interact effectively with people. Computer science minors understand the world not just from taking computer science courses. They understand it because the minor is nestled within Lyon\u2019s core curriculum required of all students, no matter their major.
