The Payroll Department reports employees\u2019 W-2 earnings to the Social Security Administration annually. Each year, a number of employees cannot have their earnings properly credited due to discrepancies in the name and\/or social security number being reported. Due to our concern that employees receive proper credit for retirement earnings, the Payroll Department is requiring a copy of the employee\u2019s Social Security Card at the time of hire. a When do I get paid?
