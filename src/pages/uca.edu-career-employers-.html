Recruit at UCA Employers can take advantage of several programs at the Career Services Center, including the opportunity to recruit at five major career fairs during the school year: STEM Careers Fair (October), Health Career Fair (October), Fall Career Fair (November), Nursing Job Fair (January), Teachers\u2019 Fair (February) and Spring Career Fair (March).
