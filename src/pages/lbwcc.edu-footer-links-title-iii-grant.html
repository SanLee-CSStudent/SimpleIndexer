Wallace Community College (LBWCC) is a public two-year college accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges. The College offers university transfer associate degrees as well as career certificates and associate degrees. The College is proposing one comprehensive activity to resolve problems of low retention and completion by developing a college-wide student success coaching program and by improving student advising and orientation. The activity also addresses problems of low enrollment and low employment of career technical students by developing high-wage, high-demand career programs.
