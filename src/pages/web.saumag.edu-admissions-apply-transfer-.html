smooth transition for a successful SAU experience. We provide initial transfer reviews and advising for new transfer students. We help coordinate initial contact with the academic dean\/chair for evaluation of academic credit. Our designated transfer recruiter makes site visits to area community colleges and attends transfer college fairs. Also, we facilitate the development and updates of articulation agreements with numerous community colleges.
