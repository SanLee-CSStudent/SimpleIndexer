development within the context of the university\u2019s mission statement, while serving students in a manner consistent with the example of Jesus Christ and our calling as Christians. PRIMARY GOALS OF STUDENT DEVELOPMENT To promote the growth of the whole person intellectually, physically, spiritually, and socially; To assist students in connecting with the university community; To enhance the overall quality of the student life experience at Ouachita; To provide assistance to students in need or crisis; To increase understanding of the developmental needs of students; To foster a community characterized by respect for the institution, for others, and for oneself; To promote Christian ideals and character through emphasizing integrity, personal responsibility, and accountability; To provide essential services in support of the primary academic function of the university; and, To support the efforts of all students to earn a degree.
