UA Little Rock School of Social Work Our program has a long-standing commitment to social workers and citizens in Arkansas. In addition to being the longest-running program in Arkansas, the School of Social Work graduates more social work students than any other social work program in Arkansas and is one of the fastest-growing programs on the UA Little Rock campus. In addition, we are the only university in Arkansas to offer both a bachelor\u2019s and a master\u2019s program in online and face-to-face formats. Gain Real-World Experience Through Field Practice Some of the most important learning you will experience happens outside of the classroom. Field practice allows our students to gain valuable hands-on training with people of need while using their progressive skills in social work. It is a chance for students to learn and make mistakes in a supportive environment where they can apply their expertise and intellectual perspective with new clients. MidSOUTH provides leadership, training, and product support in the areas of addiction, child welfare, technology, distance learning, and organizational development.
