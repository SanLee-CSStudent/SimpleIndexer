The deadline for this payment is April 1. Invitations may be purchased online, HERE, at an additional cost. Parents and Guests Von Braun Center Directions and Parking FAQ When is Commencement? Commencement is Saturday, May 7, 2022, at 8:00 p.m. Are masks required? Yes, all persons entering the venue are required to wear masks and maintain social distancing protocols. Where will Commencement be held? Commencement will be held at the Von Braun Civic Center, Are tickets required for graduates or guests?(TBD) Will there be a rehearsal for the Commencement Ceremony? No. Candidates for graduation will receive instructions immediately preceding the Commencement Ceremony. When and where do I go for the processional line-up? Due to adjusted protocols there will not be a procession. Graduates will check in at Von Braun Civic Center at East Hall 2. Where can I leave my belongings during the Commencement ceremony? Please be aware that Oakwood University is unable to provide storage space to hold candidates\u2019 personal belongings on the day of Commencement. Please only bring your cap, gown and tassel. Will I be recognized individually at Commencement? All graduation candidates will process across the stage to be recognized individually. Actual diplomas will be mailed at a later date. Will my name be in the Commencement Program? The program will list the names of all students who have completed the degree application and been approved for graduation by April 1. The Commencement Program is sent to the printer several weeks prior to the ceremony, and changes to the program will not be accepted after that time. Will my picture be taken at Commencement? Yes. Oakwood University has contracted with an independent photography company (LifeTouch) that will take pictures of graduates as they receive their diploma covers. Following graduation, graduates will receive information directly from LifeTouch about the option to purchase photos. Photos will be online at www.events.lifetouch.com one week after the ceremony. Which side do I wear my tassel on? Before graduating, your tassel should be placed on your right. After all graduates are individually recognized, you will be instructed to move your tassel to the left. Are there any special accommodations for guest? Graduates attending the Commencement Ceremony who need special accommodations should contact the Registrar Office at (256)726-7353 or registrar@oakwood.edu by May 6, 2021. Individuals needing special parking and seating accommodations, should plan to arrive 30 minutes prior to commencement as this space is limited. (Add VonBraun Civic center info) What are the Driving Directions and where should I park? Visit the Von Braun Center (VBC) website for driving directions, http:\/\/www.vonbrauncenter.com\/. Parking is available in lots adjacent to the VBC (Nominal fees of: City Parking Deck $5-Across from the VBC; South Hall Parking $10.00;and North Hall Parking $10.00). What are diplomas mailed? Diplomas will be mailed out on June 30th. Please call the Registrar\u2019s office for more information.
