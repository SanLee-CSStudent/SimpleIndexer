TRIO programs are designed to increase success in college for disadvantaged students. North Arkansas College has three TRIO programs that work with adults wanting to enroll in college, middle school and high school students looking for financial aid and college admission options and current North Arkansas College students who need support to graduate or transfer to a four-year institution.
