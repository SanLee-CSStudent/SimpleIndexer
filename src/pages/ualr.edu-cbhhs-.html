Preparing Students for Professional Careers In the College of Business, Health, and Human Services, you can choose from a range of programs designed to educate, inspire, and prepare you for a fulfilling career. Our college consists of more than ten departments and schools which offer academic programs at all levels \u2013 minors, undergraduate certificates and degrees, master\u2019s certificates and degrees, and doctoral. Our students benefit from hands-on and project-based teaching, which will give you the practical skills and experience needed to stand out from your competitors in the job market. Whether you want to study business, health, or areas of human service, we will surround you with support, immerse you in experiences that shape you, and challenge you to go further.
