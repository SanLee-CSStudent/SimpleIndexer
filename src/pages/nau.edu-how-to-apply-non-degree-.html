How to apply Apply as a non-degree-seeking student\u2014as an undergraduate or a graduate\u2014if you are not ready to apply to a degree program or you want to take classes for personal enrichment. The application process for non-degree-seeking students is streamlined and does not require a submission of transcripts or test scores. Undergraduate You can take eight or fewer credits as an undergraduate non-degree-seeking student. Graduate Graduate non-degree-seeking students are allowed to apply up to a maximum of 12 units of appropriate and approved coursework toward a graduate degree upon admission to a graduate program.
