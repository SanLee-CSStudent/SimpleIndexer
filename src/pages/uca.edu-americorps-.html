UCA engages students, graduates, and community members in national service opportunities through two national service programs housed in the Division of Outreach & Community Engagement: BearsServe Leaders are current UCA students who commit to serve 450 hours over the course of an academic year and are placed with one of our community partner host sites. BearsServe Leaders are quarter-time AmeriCorps members and provide direct service in education, food insecurity, or financial empowerment. BearsServe Leaders are selected each spring and serve from September 1st to April 30th. UCA VISTAs are current graduate students, alumni, or local community members who commit to 12 months of full-time service and apply directly to a position with one of six community partner host sites. UCA VISTAs are full-time AmeriCorps VISTA members and provide capacity-building service for a nonprofit or community. UCA VISTAS are selected each spring and serve for 12 months beginning in July each year. VISTA Summer Associates is a new program launched in 2022! We are still recruiting for ONE Summer Associate to join our team and serve full-time from June 9th-August 3rd with Bear Essentials Food Pantry and the campus garden\/produce stand. This positions earns a living allowance of $575 every two weeks and an education award of $1300+ at the end of the service term. Apply here by May 15th.
