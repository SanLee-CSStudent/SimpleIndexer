It offers competitive pricing with the convenience of on-campus printing, copying, and mailing services. Mail services Students will be notified by email when mail is delivered to your campus post office box. You may stop by anytime during normal operations, Monday-Friday 8 a.m.-5 p.m., to pick it up. Bring your student ID card for identification. Mail is not delivered to BSC on weekends, but will be available after it is delivered on Monday. Package delivery When a package from a carrier such as FedEx, UPS, or USPS arrives, you will receive an e-mail notifying you. You may stop by anytime during normal operation, Monday-Friday 8 a.m.-5 p.m., to pick it up. Bring your campus ID card for identification. Packages are not delivered to BSC on weekends, but will be available after they are delivered on Monday Shipping You may also ship through FedEx, UPS and USPS from this location. The Bookstore has free boxes when available and packing supplies can also be purchased there. Student copiers and printing Students are provided $30 on a declining balance account for printing and copying. If you run out, you will continue to be able to print; overages will be charged to your student account. Each time you print or copy, your balance will be displayed as part of the transaction. Overages will be processed on a monthly basis to your student account. The Pharos system is used for managing the declining balance account. This includes printing from BSC-provided computers and mobile printing. To access mobile printing go to MyBSC and select Mobile Print, login with your username and password, or email items as an attachment to [email protected] When printing using BSC-provided computers, a pop-up will display for you to enter your username and password. To access copy functions, swipe your campus card to gain access to the copier and use the touch screen to select your options and make your copies. Materials sent to a copier\/printer remotely will remain in the queue for up to four hours. Information for BSC Faculty and Staff Ricoh Supplies For toner on both printers and copiers call 205-226-4735 or e-mail [email protected]when the \"Low toner warning\" is displayed to schedule a delivery and provide: model located on the front of the equipment and serial number located on the rear or side of the equipment. Note: When the \"Low toner warning\" appears, you still have toner and the device will operate several more days without issue. However, this is your prompt to schedule a delivery. The toner will then be delivered from Ricoh on campus through the normal course of mail delivery or package delivery times soon thereafter. For paper on both printers and copiers call 205-226-4735 or e-mail [email protected]to schedule a delivery and provide: location \u2013 building and room quantity Ricoh Service For all service needs related to the hardware please call the Ricoh Service Dispatch number 888-456-6457 and provide the ID number from the ID tag located on the front of the device. The service phone number is also on the ID tag. The response time of a service technician will likely be in 2 to 4 hours after the call is placed. Panther Printing Students, faculty, and staff have access to Ricoh printing services in the newly renovated space of the Norton Center. These services are available Monday-Friday, 8 a.m.-5 p.m. A kiosk printer is available when the Norton Campus Center is open, usually 24 hours a day during regular terms.
