The College of Arts and Sciences lies at the heart of UAB. We are the foundation of the undergraduate experience and increasingly, the home for more and more exceptional graduate students. We are the school in which every UAB undergraduate student begins their academic experience, and more than 50 percent of them complete their degrees with us. We are the home away from home for more than 6,000 students and over 30,000 alumni. We cherish our role as the anchor for this institution, and we value our alumni who have gone on to represent UAB with pride and enthusiasm. Arts and Sciences Alumni Board The UAB Arts and Sciences Alumni Board welcomes all College of Arts and Sciences alumni regardless of major or background.
