Who should join a Pathway? Students whose English ability is close to the required admissions score but who cannot yet demonstrate the proficiency needed for direct admission Students whose GPA is close to the required admissions standard and who would like to prove their academic capabilities to UAB Students who are ready to begin undergraduate or graduate coursework in their major while also building English language skill competency Students who would like to ensure academic success by joining in tutoring for their undergraduate or graduate coursework, as well as workshops on American academic culture and study skills Pathway students benefit from language coursework carefully designed to help English language learners prepare for academic study. Pathway students also benefit from joining mainstream academic classes with American students that will fulfill degree requirements in the future. Pathway students have full access to the INTO UAB Learning Resource Center, a dedicated service for personalized tutoring and group workshops to enhance and support studying. Pathway students can build relationships with instructors and students all across UAB\u2019s campus from their initial semester at UAB. Pathway students have ample academic advising support from the INTO UAB team. Pathway students are UAB students, and they have access to every service and student benefit that UAB has to offer.
