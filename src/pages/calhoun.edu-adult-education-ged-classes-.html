Go Further! Four Free Credits! GED Vouchers Contact Us Welcome to Calhoun Community College GED Program. We offer FREE GED classes and testing for learners within our service area. Each GED learner works at his or her own pace and all instructional materials are available at no cost to the learner. Any adult who is at least 18 years of age, and not presently enrolled in school, may participate in the GED program. Learners ages 16 \u2013 17 may take the GED exam only with proper approval and paperwork (see \u201CGED Testing for 16 & 17 Year Olds\u201D on the GED Testing page). A picture ID and Social Security or Tax ID number is required for orientation and two forms of ID are required for GED testing services. Walk-in registrations are welcome! Greater Opportunities for Adult Learners GOAL is a partnership between Calhoun & Drake State Take advantage of FREE adult education programs so you can receive quality training and learn the life skills you need to achieve your goals! Online Classes In response to our community needs Adult Education is conducting online classes. Someone will contact you within two business days. Alabama Career Centers Alabama Adult Education is a workforce partner and GED classes are located in all Alabama Career Centers. After students complete the required orientation they are assigned to a GED instructor who will work individually with the student to help create a personalized learning plan and facilitate online instruction.
