We are committed to helping people reach their full potential and become successful after college life. For more information about Greek Life at BSC please contact Stephanie Schroeder, Assistant Director of Student Involvement. [email protected] or 205-226-4730. Our chapters are governed by the Panhellenic Council. The Panhellenic Council strives to develop women through academic success and service. We promote women's leadership, diversity, and strong relationships between each chapter. The Recruitment activities are held during the evening and will not interfere with any of your classes. Recruitment is absolutely FREE so we encourage you to participate even if you aren't sure you want to join a Greek organization.
