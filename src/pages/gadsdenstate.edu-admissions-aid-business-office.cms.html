How much is tuition? How do i make a payment? Payments for tuition, insurance, diplomas, parking fines, library fines, testing and other fees may be made by cash, check, traveler\u2019s check, money order, MasterCard, Visa, American Express and Discover at the Business Office locations at the One Stop Center on the East Broad Campus and the Administration Building on the Ayers Campus. Gadsden State Cherokee only accepts checks or money orders. Payments may also be made online using your OneACCS account. Third-Party Payments If a third-party agency (employer, governmental agency, private scholarship donor, etc.) is paying tuition, fees and\/or other educational expenses, the student or agency should contact the Business Office. Student Account Information Gadsden State complies with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act, which prohibits the release of educational records without student permission. Only the student may request a copy of account transactions.
