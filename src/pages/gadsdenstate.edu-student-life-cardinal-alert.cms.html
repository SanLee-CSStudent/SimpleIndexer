Log In to Cardinal Alert As part of Gadsden State\u2019s efforts to ensure the safety of students, faculty, and staff, the College utilizes a campus emergency notification system known as Cardinal Alert. Through this system students, faculty, and staff will be able to receive time sensitive emergency messages via text and voice messages to cell phones and home phones. Cardinal Alert is a secure system and will not send advertising or spam to your personal phones. Messages will be sent through the system only for periodic testing and in emergency situations. How to unsubscribe from Cardinal Alerts: Unsubscribe from text messages by texting the word STOP in response to a message. (To opt back in, text the word START to the same short code number.) Unsubscribe from email alerts by clicking on the unsubscribe button at the bottom of the email. (To opt back in to email alerts, contact helpdesk@gadsdenstate.edu.)
