Major Programs UCA Undergraduate Majors The undergraduate majors table lists more than 150 programs of study in undergraduate degree programs. For information about non-degree pre-professional programs, see links in the Undergraduate Bulletin\u2019s Academic Programs directory. The Academic Maps (AMAPs) linked in the AMAP column of the table constitute a record of the official, approved programs of study for undergraduate degrees for students beginning at UCA in fall 2020. The AMAPs are in PDF format. An omnibus collection of this year\u2019s AMAPs is available from this link: 2020-2021 AMAP Omnibus Collection (updated 2020-11-23).
