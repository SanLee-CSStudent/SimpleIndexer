Go Bears! Greek Life at UCA Welcome to the University of Central Arkansas Greek Community! For 100 years, the Greek system at UCA has offered an opportunity for involvement that is truly unique. Scholarship, leadership, brotherhood and sisterhood, community service, and social activities are the key elements of the fraternities and sororities at UCA. We believe that the best way to get the most out of your college years is through the Greek experience. The opportunities for involvement and participation are numerous, and we encourage you to take a good look at what our Greek community has to offer. Since 1915, Greek Organizations at UCA have been an integral part of campus life. Four councils serve as the governing bodies of the chapters at UCA: Interfraternity Council (IFC) ,Panhellenic Council (Panhellenic), National Pan-Hellenic Council (NPHC), and United Greek Council (UGC) In this system, IFC, Panhellenic, NPHC, and UGC students work together as a unified force to promote Greek life, govern chapters, and sponsor Greek events. ? ? ?
