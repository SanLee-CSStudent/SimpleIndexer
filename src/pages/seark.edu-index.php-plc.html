The program will serve as a bridge between the SEARK community and greater community by developing partnerships with area leaders, businesses, and community service organizations. Experiential Learning President\u2019s Leadership Class (PLC) scholars will be given ample opportunities to learn about leadership through numerous hands-on experiences throughout their semester in the program. Provided opportunities include lectures from recognized leaders and leadership positions on campus and in the community. Service Learning The PLC program places an emphasis on community service and civic engagement in order to help scholars develop a social awareness, civic responsibility, and interconnectedness. Scholars will be required to preform a minimum of 10 hours of community service during the semester they are enrolled in the program. Criteria for Selection Selection is based on leadership potential, intellectual curiosity, and a desire to give of oneself, as evidenced by the following: A minimum 2.5 cumulative grade point average A completed written application Two letters of recommendation SEARK transcript Personal interview President\u2019s Leadership Class Scholarship (PLC) The President\u2019s Leadership Class Scholarship at SEARK recognizes outstanding students who demonstrate talent and distinguished achievement in the areas of leadership, citizenship, service and communication. The scholarship is comprised of a full-tuition waiver for up to 12 credit hours during the spring 2021 semester. Eligibility Criteria To be eligible to apply, applicants must meet the following requirements: Have 2.5 GPA or higher Current college students should be enrolled in a cumulative minimum of nine credit hours each semester Application Procedure Scholarship applicants who meet the eligibility requirements must submit the following material with this completed application: Current SEARK transcript Two letters of recommendation Submission Instructions All scholarship applications and supporting materials must be received by posted deadline. Complete your application and upload the supporting materials (SEARK Transcript and Letters of Recommendation). Selection Process All applicants who meet the specified requirements will be considered by the PLC Selection Committee. The Committee will review the applications and recommend the top applicants for an interview. Students who advance to the interview process and are recommended by the PLC Selection Committee will be considered for awards. Students who are selected will enroll in the 3-hour President\u2019s Leadership Class, offered Spring 2021. All applicants will be notified regarding the result of the application process. Contact Information For more information email Tracey Trafford, PLC Coordinator, at ttrafford@seark.edu.
