Learn more. Transportation Services coordinates parking enforcement, permitting and shuttle services for Samford University. Parking Permits Registration for new parking permits for both students and employees will open the first week of August 2021. Current permits will expire August 31, 2021. In the event that initial attempts to register for your parking permit are unsuccessful, please continue attempts until successfully registered. Contact Transportation Services at parking@samford.edu for assistance. Please watch for emails regarding dates, times and pick up locations for new permits. Current permits are no longer available. Temporary parking permits, for periods of less than seven days and visitor parking permits are available at Seibert Hall office 303. More Information Shuttle Routes For the spring semester, Sam Tram shuttle routes will run on the main campus from 7:30 a.m. - 5 p.m. Monday-Friday and consist of the Gray Line and Red Line. Shuttles will run continuous loops in clockwise and counterclockwise directions. The White Line will run the CHS Express from 7:30 a.m. to 9 a.m. Monday-Friday. This route is the quick way from Tri-Delta\/West Campus and West Village to the College of Health Sciences and back. Nine shuttle stops are conveniently located across campus. See shuttle route maps for details. Track shuttle locations online using the live shuttle tracker or download the Ride Systems app from the Apple App Store or Google Play. In addition to running two shuttles from 7:30 a.m.-5 p.m. Monday-Friday on fixed routes, the Red Line makes a trip to Target every Friday evening. The Red Line may be boarded at 4:30 p.m. at the stop located near the University Center. Target is located at Brookwood Village in Homewood and 1.2 miles from campus. Riders can expect to return to campus around 5:30 p.m. every Friday.
