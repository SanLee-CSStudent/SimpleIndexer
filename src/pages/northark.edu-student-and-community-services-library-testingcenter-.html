Development Testing Center Hours of Operation Monday \u2013 Friday, 8 a.m. - 5 p.m. Testing Center hours may change due to college closures Call us: 870-391-3533 The Northark Testing Center is located inside the Learning Resource Center\/Library and offers a wide range of testing, including Northark testing, proctored exams for distance learning students, GED exams, and professional exams. Appointments Please call the Testing Center to make an appointment. Appointments are required for all tests, except Accuplacer, as long as space is available. A photo ID is required for all exams. Before arriving to take the Accuplacer, please pay at Student Accounts. The cost is $10 for the entire test or $5 per section. There are two documents that are required to complete the registration: Distance Learning Agreement: To be signed by both student and instructor Distance Learning Exam Cover Sheet: To be completed by the instructor A test proctor will contact the student to set up an appointment once the Exam Cover Sheet is completed. Professional Testing The Northark Testing Center offers the following professional testing opportunities: Certiport Clep Pan Pan I-9 Pearson Vue Praxis PSI Please call the Testing Center for more information.
