The Center for Integrative Nanotechnology Sciences exists to advance the local, regional, and international study and application of nanoscience and nanotechnology through research and outreach and accelerate technological innovations into practical applications for society. We work with scientists from around the world to make breakthroughs in nanomedicine and multifunctional materials. However, the Center doesn\u2019t just offer collaboration opportunities for advanced researchers. Whether you\u2019re a curious community member, a driven high school student, or an industry leader in need of an instrument, we welcome you to join us.
