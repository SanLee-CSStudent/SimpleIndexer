The Office of Sponsored International Programs (OSIP) was established in the Office of the Vice President for Research to facilitate international service, education, and research programs by building on experiences and by adapting University policy for international activities. The responsibilities of the OSIP include the following: to serve as an institutional resource for information regarding UAB\u2019s international research, training and service programs; to work with UAB faculty and existing and potential international partners to promote, develop and facilitate sponsored international research, training, and services programs; to facilitate the establishment of memoranda of understanding (MOUs) and linkage agreements (LA) with international partners; and to facilitate communications and interactions between UAB\u2019s international programs and entities responsible for compliance and oversight. For assistance in exploring or developing an MOU or LA with an international partner, please contact the OSIP. Please visit the OSIP's Homepage to view a list of UAB's international partners.
