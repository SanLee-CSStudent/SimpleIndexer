STAR Institute is the Student Tutoring and Academic Resource Institute. We provide FREE one on one tutoring in all subject areas and success seminars throughout each semester. Student success is our number one priority at Calhoun Community College. Every effort is made to provide students with programs and services that can assist them in obtaining and reaching their academic goals. NOTE: Now Offering Tutor Appointments on Campus & Online! Here\u2019s How to Schedule your FREE Appointment using TracCloud Login to your MyCalhoun Portal. Under the Quicklaunch, click the TracCloud\/SAGE Icon. Click \u201CSchedule an Appointment.\u201D Select \u201CFind First Available Immediately\u201D or \u201CChoose a Date and Center.\u201D Schedule an Appointment for Help In: Select the Class that You Want Tutoring Press Search You will see ALL of the available appointments for that day in either Online Optional or Online Only Format. In the Online Optional Format, please pay attention to the Decatur or Huntsville Option. Press Load More to view other times or dates. To select your day and time, simply click on the time that you would like to attend. If you choose the Online Appointment, it is Online Only. If you select the Online Optional, you can choose to have an Online Appointment by checking the box, or leave it blank for an In-Person Session. Again, please make sure that you pay attention to the Decatur or Huntsville Option! On the Appointment Entry Page, fill out ALL of the required information, including making notes of what you\u2019d like to cover. Please include a phone number. PRESS Confirm. You and the tutor will receive a confirmation email. All tutor sessions are 50 minutes each. You may schedule up to 3 appointments per week. We have TWO locations to serve you: STAR Decatur: Room 230 of the Chasteen Student Services Center on the Decatur Campus.
