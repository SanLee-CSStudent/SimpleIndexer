By articulating certain college courses with courses taught in area high schools, North Arkansas College helps students make a smooth transition from high school to college without experiencing delays, duplication of courses or loss of credit. By mutual agreement between North Arkansas College and the participating high school, students completing these articulated courses with a grade of \u201CA\u201D or \u201CB\u201D will receive college credit for these courses upon enrollment at North Arkansas College. The college will not charge tuition or fees for course credit earned under approved Articulated Course Agreements.
