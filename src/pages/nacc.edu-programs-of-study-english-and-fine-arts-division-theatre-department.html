We produce 3-4 large mainstage productions each year including, a summer youth theatre workshop for students 18 years old and younger. NACC Theatre students have the opportunity to learn, grow, and develop their skills while earning an Associate in Arts Theatre degree. Theatre alumni continue their careers by entering the industry and continuing their education at universities around the country. If you are interested in studying theatre or learning how to be apart of our community theatre, please feel free to email or give us a call. Begin Your Future Here!
