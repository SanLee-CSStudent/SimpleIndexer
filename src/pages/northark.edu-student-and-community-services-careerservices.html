Area Job Opportunities Search for a Job or Post a Job on Ready for Life website Career Services is here to help you plan out your future. We will assist you in choosing a major, planning a career, preparing resumes, finding employment, re-entering the workforce, and working to meet your future goals. All students are encouraged to meet with a career coach: To explore personal interests, values, personality traits, and talents & match them to fulfilling career paths. To explore college majors and job possibilities. To clarify your long term career goals and create an action plan to achieve them. To receive skills coaching or determine room for improvement. To work on creating or updating resumes, cover letters & other application documents. To structure your job search, help with your application documents & coach you to present well in job interviews. Resume and Interview Assistance We are pleased to offer you a free training system that features a new and innovative way to help you prepare more effectively for a job interview. Big Interview is an online system that combines training and practice to help improve your interview technique and build your confidence. Big Interview offers a variety of tools including: Challenging, virtual mock interviews for all experience levels and hundreds of industries A database of thousands of interview questions with tips on how to answer them The ability to rate and share your interview answers for feedback A comprehensive video training curriculum covering all aspects of landing a job A step-by-step interview Answer Builder for crafting answers to behavioral questions A Resume builder and Resume Curriculum Here\u2019s how to set up your Big Interview account: STEP 1: Go to https:\/\/northark.biginterview.com and click \"Register.\" STEP 2: Enter your school email address, name, and password, organization code of NACCP13 and click \u201CCreate my Account\u201D STEP 3: Log in and start using Big Interview.
