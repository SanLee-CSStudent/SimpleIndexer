More Info Explore Programs All Programs Adult Education Career Technical Programs Dual Enrollment for Dual Credit Health Studies Special Programs University Transfer Programs Virtual Learning Blackboard Quality Enhancement Plan (QEP) Technology Resources for Students Workforce Training Blackboard NW-SCC now uses the Blackboard Learn Ultra Learning Management System (LMS) to access all online, hybrid, and web-assisted course materials. Students, faculty, and staff will log in to Blackboard using the same login credentials used for myNW-SCC (Ellucian Banner). If you are not able to log into your myNW-SCC and\/or Blackboard accounts, please complete the Login Assistance Form. Someone will reply within 24 hours. You may also contact the Distance Education Office at 256-331-5395 or 256-331-5277 for assistance or stop by the Virtual Learning Center in Building 110 on the Shoals Campus. Important Note: Please use the Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox browser when accessing course content in Blackboard. Students have reported difficulties opening some content when using the Apple Safari and Microsoft Edge browsers.
