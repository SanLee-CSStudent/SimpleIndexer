Welcome Back Vaquero\/Vaquera! A Returning Student has applied to CAC in the past and has been away for more than one year. Follow the steps below to continue on your path! STEP 1 Contact Admissions to Check Your Admissions Status: Email: admissions@centralaz.edu or call 520-494-5260 IMPORTANT: Include the following in your email: \u2022Brief description of your situation and what you need from admissions \u2022Your Phone number, current mailing address and personal email \u2022Copy of state-issued ID (if you do not have a state-issued ID, please let us know this in the email) \u2022Confirm CAC Pathway\/Program of Study. If unsure see options below. Review Areas of Interest: Areas of Interest are groups of related CAC Pathways\/Programs that lead to a degree or certificate. \u2022Discover your Areas of Interest and CAC Pathway\/Program options Payment Options: CAC In-State Tuition = $86 per credit hour IMPORTANT: Complete FAFSA TODAY-DON\u2019T Delay!!! \u2022Every year complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) form as soon as possible after October 1st. CAC\u2019s Federal ID Code is 007283. \u2022FAFSA will determine a student\u2019s financial need and aid eligibility for MOST financial aid. Payment Plan We strongly encourage all students to apply for some form of financial aid. Learn about the different types of financial aid and programs below: \u2022Federal Aid \u2022Scholarships \u2022Military & Veterans Educational Benefits To see a full list of CAC Tuition and Fees STEP 2 After You Confirm Student Status with Admissions: \u2022Access your Student Portal, CAC Student Email & Blackboard through MyCAC \u2022For technology assistance, email IT Support Services at it.help@centralaz.edu or call 520-494-5678 \u2022To obtain a CAC Student ID card, visit any CAC campus. Duplicate IDs are available for $10. On-Campus Housing\/Signal Peak Only ~ Application and Information Placement Tools and Academic Advising Appointment: Students returning to CAC should use Placement Tools and make an appointment to meet with an Academic Advisor AFTER confirming their student status with admissions. Complete the following to ensure a successful advising appointment: \u2022Use CAC\u2019s Placement Tools to determine recommended English\/Reading and Math course placement. \u2022Set an appointment with your Academic Advisor and be sure to bring your placement results to your appointment. \u2022If you have graduated or attended high school in the past three years, bring high school transcripts to your advising appointment. \u2022If you have attended another institution, bring to your advising appointment an unofficial transcript(s) from each institution you have attended. \u2022Official Transcript MUST be received and evaluated by CAC before you can register for 2nd semester classes. \u2022Have Official Transcripts sent to: Central Arizona College\/Attention Registration, 8479 N. Overfield Rd., Coolidge, AZ 85128-9030 STEP 3 Register for Classes and Pay Tuition and Fees Registration Options: \u2022Add\/Drop classes in Student Portal: \u2022Complete Add\/Drop form and email from your CAC Student Email to admissions@centralaz.edu or return to any CAC campus All tuition and fees must be paid at time of registration. CAC\u2019s Tuition and Fees Payment Options: Check Financial Aid Award Letter on Student Portal If your financial aid is pending you must pay in full or sign up for the payment plan. \u2022Pay Online: Login to your Student Portal to pay your tuition \u2022CAC Payment Plan \u2022Pay In Person \u2022Pay by Phone: 520-494-5240 Attend New Student Orientation at your discretion. This could be a good fresher on college polices and procedures. Registration and More information Orientations are an opportunity to connect with other students, faculty, and staff. Topics covered include: Working with Advising: Utilizing Financial Aid: Using Blackboard\/Email and more. Final Details \u2022Become familiar with Blackboard where you access your courses, clubs, organizations, technology tutorials, tutoring and more. \u2022Courses may not show up in Blackboard until the scheduled course start date. \u2022Review\/print your schedule anytime in your Student Portal \u2022Find Textbooks at CAC Campus Bookstores or online.
