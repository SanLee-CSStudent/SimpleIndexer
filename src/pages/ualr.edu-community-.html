Serving the Community Learning is not limited to the classroom. UA Little Rock is proud to partner with and support the community through various resources, services and programs. Community settings give students the opportunity to develop valuable skills that enhance their professional and personal lives. Academic experiences that integrate service into the curriculum also increase civic engagement by students while in college and after. The university facilitates outreach between campus and community partners, promotes experiential learning to increase student engagement, learning and retention, and enhances collaborative faculty and student research.
