Opportunities The Office of Human Resources at Southern Arkansas University lists all open positions available at the University. Full postings for these positions can be found at the Human Resources office and on our web page. Faculty Positions | Staff Positions | Part-Time Positions How to Apply Full-time Faculty and Staff Positions Online employment application Applicants apply through the Office of Human Resources. Refer to job advertisement or position announcement for specific application requirements. Part-time\/Temporary Positions Applicants apply through the Office of Human Resources. Refer to job advertisement or position announcement on Part-time\/Temporary open position web page for specific application requirements. Graduate Assistant Positions Applicants apply through the School of Graduate Studies. Student Employment All students interested in on-campus employment must first contact the Office of Financial Aid to determine eligibility. If eligible, students can contact the Student Employment Office for more information. Applications are accepted by the Office of Human Resources for all open advertised positions.
