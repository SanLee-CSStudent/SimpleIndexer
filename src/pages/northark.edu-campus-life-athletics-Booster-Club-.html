There are five classes of memberships available. I. Individual (cost $75) You receive: Membership card Pass to all Pioneer home games Pioneer T-shirt II. Family (cost $100) You receive: Membership card Pass to admit each family member* to all Pioneer home games Two Pioneer T-shirts or one T-shirt and one cap IV. Corporate Donor (cost $300) You receive: Membership card Pass for member and guests to all Pioneer home games Pioneer Pullover & Cap V. Pioneer Patron ($500) You receive: Membership card Pass for member and guests to all Pioneer home games Pioneer Club Jacket & Polo Shirt or cap Name displayed prominently on plaque in Pioneer Pavilion VI. Scholarship Donor (cost $1,000) You receive: Membership card Pass for member and guests to all Pioneer home games Pioneer Club Jacket, Polo Shirt, and Cap Name prominently displayed on individualized banner in Pioneer Pavilion * Husband and wife and unmarried children 18 years and under living at home. Call 870-391-3278 to become a member of the Pioneer Club. Make checks payable to North Arkansas College Pioneer Club. Thank you for supporting Pioneer athletics!
