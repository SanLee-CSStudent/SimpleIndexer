Applying for scholarships at UA Little Rock is an important part of your college journey, and we want to make sure the process is easy for you! Incoming freshmen and transfer students only need to apply for admission in order to be considered for most of our institutional scholarships (institutional scholarships are the ones that come from the university). Freshmen can receive half-off their tuition and fees for 2022-23. Learn more about this new scholarship opportunity! We\u2019ve also made it easy for current and newly admitted students to apply to thousands of internal and external scholarships. ScholarshipUniverse (SU) is our new scholarship-matching tool. SU will match you with the scholarships that you\u2019re qualified for based on your responses to the scholarship matching questions.
