To be eligible to participate in the Federal Work Study program, you must have a valid FAFSA and completed financial aid file in the financial aid office. You must be in good standing academically and have financial need. For specific questions about your eligibility, please contact the financial aid office at 870-391-3266. If you would like to apply for a work study position, please complete an application in the financial aid office on the South Campus.
