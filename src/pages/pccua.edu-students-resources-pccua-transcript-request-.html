Coronavirus Disease Get Ready for Life Training Center Faculty & Staff Community News Accessibility Statement PCCUA Transcript Request PCCUA has three options to receive copies of transcripts Option 1: Official\/Unofficial Transcripts through PCCUA Registrar\u2019s Office To receive a transcript at no charge through fax or USPS please fill out the online request form here: https:\/\/pccua.jotform.com\/51164021099954 Option 2: Electronic transcripts through National Student Clearinghouse PCCUA has contracted with the National Student Clearinghouse to process online transcript requests and electronic delivery of transcripts. Electronic transcripts can be sent to institutions and individuals (including yourself) via The National Student Clearinghouse (NSC), our third-party provider. There is a $3.00 fee for this service, payable to NSC by credit card. A physical address and a valid email address are required. NSC transcript request link: https:\/\/tsorder.studentclearinghouse.org\/school\/ficecode\/00110400 Option 3: Pick up copies at PCCUA-Registrar\u2019s Office on Helena Campus. If you have any questions please contact registration@pccua.edu or call (870) 338-6474 Ext: 1337. No transcript will be released until all financial obligations to PCCUA are satisfied.
