Major Political science majors focus on political systems and the ideas that bring them into being, transform them, and sustain them. Group power\u2014the 'how' and 'why' of collective decision making\u2014is considered, as well as the ethics of power. Justice, order, identity, equality, citizenship, and community are also studied. You will develop the habit of clear thought, rigorous analysis, and effective debate. Join us as we explore the practical and ethical dimensions of contemporary issues and compare political systems worldwide. % of Political Science majors are employed or in grad school within 6 months of graduation :1 student-to-teacher ratio Meet the Faculty Details Courses Careers Admissions Internships Scholarships Why study Political Science at Lyon College? The political science major at Lyon provides small class sizes in which faculty become familiar with student interests and career goals, a senior seminar \u201Ccapstone\u201D experience to complete the major, and the opportunity to present their scholarship at professional political science conferences. While these are not necessarily \u201Cunique\u201D individually, it is rare to find them all put together in this fashion at an institution. Political science students have attended Nichols trips to Germany, Austria, New Zealand, and South Korea. Ways to get involved Pi Sigma Alpha is the only honor society for college and university students of political science in the U.S. Its purpose is to encourage and recognize superior achievement in the study of government and politics at the undergraduate and graduate level. Members must have attained at least junior status, a \u201CB\u201D average in at least 10 credits of political science courses, and an overall GPA rank in the top third of their class. Majors also have the opportunity to work with political science faculty in Model U.N. and moot court.
