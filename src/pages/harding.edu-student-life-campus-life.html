Collaborate with Us Our Mission The Office of Campus Life is committed to developing positive and inclusive student opportunities and experiences for the Harding community. The Campus Activities Board (CAB) fulfills that commitment by working through co-curricular collaboration, the creation of campus activities, and the advancement of student belonging. These three objectives form the foundation for CAB: Collaboration \u2014 We develop co-curricular partnerships with student organizations, collegiate departments, and other areas of campus life. Activities \u2014 We create and promote inclusive shared experiences for all students, faculty\/staff, and the greater Harding community. Belonging \u2014 We help students find their place in Harding's Community of Mission. Events The Campus Activities Board (CAB) is frequently on the move to plan fun, engaging, and inclusive activities for the students on our residential campus. Broadly speaking, these events include movies, concerts, pep-rallies, celebrations, and more! Follow our social media accounts (@HardingCAB) and check out our calendar to stay current on upcoming events.
