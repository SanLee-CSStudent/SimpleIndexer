Apply Get Info MENU A Future Student A Current Student A Parent or Guardian A High School Counselor A Manager or Employer Looking for Workforce Training\/Certifications In Need of a GED or Adult Education Classes A Calhoun Alum or Former Student Home Adult Education Non-Traditional High School Diploma Option Program Non-Traditional High School Diploma Option Program The Alabama Non-Traditional High School Diploma Option (HSDO) is an alternative to the GED high school equivalency, designed for students who have completed all of the required credits for graduation but failed the Alabama High School Graduation Exam (AHSGE), or students who have dropped out of the traditional high school setting. Register for Classes Online Classes In response to our community needs Adult Education is conducting online classes. Someone will contact you within two business days. Option 1 For an adult who attended an Alabama high school and earned enough credits to graduate, but did not pass the high school graduation exam. Am I Eligible For Option 1? Participant must have all 24 credits required to graduate from a traditional high school, or the number of credits established by the local school system that the participant last attended Must be at least 19 years of age. Did not pass the Alabama High School Graduation Exam Option 2 For an adult who earned 10 or more credits from an Alabama high school and requires additional credits for high school completion. Am I Eligible For Option 2? Participants must have at least 10 credits of the 24 credits required to graduate from a traditional high school in Alabama Must be at least 19 years of age. Participants must assess on the TABE (Test for Adult Basic Education) Locations Both the Decatur and Huntsville campuses offer HSDO ONLY classes. Decatur Class Tuesday\/Thursday 1pm \u2013 4pm Adult Education Building, Room 118 (Building 9) Huntsville Class Tuesday & Thursday 8:30am \u2013 11:30am Sparkman Building Room 54 You must meet with a Coordinator before attending class. HSDO FAQ\u2019s My last high school was out of state, can I still be a part of the HSDO program? Your last school of record must be an Alabama high school. If I complete this program do I get a certificate or an actual diploma? You will receive a diploma from your high school. Note: The date of graduation will be this year. How long will it take me to complete the program? We don\u2019t like to estimate your time in the program simply because every student is an individual.
