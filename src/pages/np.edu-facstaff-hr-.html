Services The Office of Human Resources performs the following services: Publicizes employment openings and receives applications. Maintains personnel records and tracks all leave hours for full-time employees. Conducts new employee orientation for fringe benefits coverage to include enrollment, explaining benefits, and providing follow-up and transmitting that information to payroll. Acts as liaison between employees and all benefit companies for claims, enrollment, issues, and terminations. Oversees cafeteria plan administration including enrollment and fund disbursement.
