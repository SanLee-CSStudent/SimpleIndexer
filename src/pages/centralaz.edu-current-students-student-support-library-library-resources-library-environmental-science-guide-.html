Alternative Energy (2010) Explores the ongoing debate over alternative energy that gathered strength in the 20th century, showing how that debate mirrors larger attitudes toward energy and consumption. Encyclopedia of Environment and Society (2007) Provides information on issues, concepts, theories, examples, problems, and policies, with the goal of explicating an emerging way of thinking about people and nature. Written by experts from incredibly diverse fields. Environment: A Revolution in Attitudes (2008) Examines the steps that have been taken to protect Earth\u2019s natural environment and the controversies that surround them. Gale Encyclopedia of Science (2008) Covers all major areas of science. Journal Articles* Academic OneFile In addition to using search terms in a keyword search, Academic OneFile lets you search in a way that gives you a good overview of the number of articles on a certain subject. For example: click on Subject Guide Search and enter environment to auto-populate appropriate search terms. Click on the link for Subdivisions to find articles on specific topics within the overall subject or click on Related Subjects to find broader, narrower, and related terms. You can also find Environmental Science in the Browse by Discipline section. OmniFile Access journal articles by entering your keyword(s) into the Search box. OmniFile also lets you search by subject. Click the Advanced Search link and select SU Subject from the drop-down Select a Field (optional) menu to the right of the search box. *Articles, eBooks, and streaming videos accessed through our databases are available only to CAC students, faculty, and staff. Agriculture Collection Search by keyword(s) in this Gale database, which provides comprehensive periodical collection supporting research in agriculture and related fields. Search by keyword(s). Global Issues in Context Find journal, magazine, and newspaper articles covering a range of global issues with keyword searches. On the main page, you can also view Issues of Interest and Browse Issues, which include the category Environment and Climate Change. Issues & Controversies Search by keyword(s) or view All Issues by Subject such as Energy and Environment. Click on All Issues A to Z to browse all issues. Opposing Viewpoints in Context Search by keyword(s), browse current Issues of Interest, or Browse Issues. Salem Science Encyclopedia of Global Warming and Environmental Issues found under the category Science. Click Science to search both. Search by keyword(s).
