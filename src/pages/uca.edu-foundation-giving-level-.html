Privately Funded Scholarship List Emergency Scholarship Request Faculty Grants Forms Giving Levels The following giving levels recognize committed alumni and friends of the University of Central Arkansas that play a vital role as the foundation of growth for UCA. We welcome all potential donors to support UCA at any capacity, through any type of gift and to any area of the University. The 1907 Society Lifetime Giving The 1907 Society, named in honor of the founding year of the University of Central Arkansas, gives special recognition to those individuals and organizations that have a lifetime giving level of $50,000 or above. The 1907 Society consists of five levels, with those levels being named in recognition of many of the former names and symbols of the university. UCA Society \u2013 $50,000 to $99,999 SCA Society \u2013 $100,000 to $249,000 ASTC Society \u2013 $250,000 to $499,999 Normal Society \u2013 $500,000 to $999,999 Founders Society \u2013 $1,000,000 and above The President\u2019s Society Annual Giving Membership in The President\u2019s Society is extended to those who contribute $1,000 or more during a calendar year. There are five levels within The President\u2019s Society that are used to recognize the commitment of each member. The levels are named in honor of past presidents of the University. The levels are: Farris Club \u2013 $1,000 to $2,499 Snow Club \u2013 $2,500 to $4,999 Irby Club \u2013 $5,000 to $9,999 McAlister Club \u2013 $10,000 to $24,900 Torreyson Club \u2013 $25,000 The Coffee Club Annual Giving The Coffee Club recognizes donors that have given $150 or more during a calendar year and will receive a commemorative coffee cup. The Sustainers Society Consecutive Giving (3 or more years) The Sustainers Society recognizes donors who have made gifts for three or more consecutive years. These loyal donors are making a continued difference across the university and will receive special gifts upon reaching three years of consecutive giving and every five-year giving milestone thereafter. The Doyne Legacy Society Legacy Giving Members of the Doyne Legacy Society have indicated the UCA Foundation, Inc. as a benefactor of their estate through one of the many planned giving options. If you have done so and have not been recognized as a member of The Doyne Legacy Society, please contact Joan Shofner, Associate Vice President for Campaign & Legacy Planning. TorchBearers Faculty & Staff Giving Employees of the University of Central Arkansas are recognized as Torchbearers when they make a gift to the UCA Foundation during a calendar year. Gifts may even be given through payroll deduction. Corporate Partners Corporate partners are recognized for annual donations and sponsorships that provide scholarship support to students, factually, staff, programs and campus enhancements. Corporate partners that contribute $5,000 or more are invited to attend the annual event for The President\u2019s Society members.
