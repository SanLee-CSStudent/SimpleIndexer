Financial Aid Students Faculty & Staff Community News Accessibility Statement Arts and Sciences Associate of Science Program The Arts and Sciences Program (Associate of Science degree) provides students desiring to transfer to a four year college or university with the skills needed to succeed in advanced degrees. Program Student Learning Outcomes With an emphasis on math and science, the student learning outcomes include: Demonstrate knowledge of mathematical reasoning and skills Demonstrate knowledge of scientific processes Demonstrate skills in problem solving, critical thinking and scientific reasoning Communicate effectively in a written and oral manner Demonstrate knowledge of history, art, literature and other cultures Program Requirements for Arts & Sciences Associate of Science Majors Biology Chemistry\/Pre-Medicine General Science Mathematics Physics Pre-Engineering Career Options Medical Doctor Dentist Veterinarian Research Analyst Engineer (Civil, Mechanical, or Computer) Biologist Estimated Program Costs STATUS TUITION FEES BOOKS TOTAL In District $4,380.00 $415.00 $2,280.00 $7,075.00 Out of District $5,160.00 $415.00 $2,280.00 $7,855.00 Out of State $8,160.00 $415.00 $2280.00 $10,855.00 PCCUA Application - Apply Now!
