Northark offers several two-year degrees useful for transfer purposes including the Associate of Arts and Associate of Science. Northark has also developed specific transfer guides to popular majors at many transfer institutions. Students who follow these guides may complete a Northark two-year degree while taking coursework needed for transfer to a four-year college. Your advisor can work with you to customize a plan to guide your transfer to the college\/university and major you choose. Contact Northark\u2019s Advising Center at AdvisingVoicemail@northark.edu or call 870-391-3115 to request your customized transfer guide. Also, you can use the Arkansas Course Transfer System from the Arkansas Department of Higher Education to see which courses are guaranteed to transfer between institutions. Articulation agreements are formal agreements or partnerships between two or more colleges and universities to simplify the process of transferring to four-year institutions. Students can transfer to many different colleges and universities, but to make that process easier, Northark has entered into articulation agreements with several institutions.
