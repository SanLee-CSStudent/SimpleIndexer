Access to 24\/7 Mental Health and Psychiatry support for all Arizona Western College students. Who can use TimelyCare? Any enrolled Arizona Western College student can use TimelyCare. What services are available? 24\/7 TalkNow visits (24\/7 mental health support), Scheduled Counseling visits (12 per year), and Psychiatry visits (by referral) are available at no cost. How do I log in? Visit TimelyCare and follow the prompts. *Mobile users will be directed to download the TimelyCare app. What can I be treated for? Our counselors can treat\/discuss a wide range of common conditions, and after talking to you, will decide on the best course of treatment. Some common conditions are: stress and anxiety, relationships and depression. How much does a visit cost? There is no cost to enrolled Arizona Western College students. I already have insurance and a primary provider, how does TimelyCare benefit me? With TimelyCare, you will have free, 24\/7 access to counselors from anywhere in the United States, so you\u2019ll never have to spend time or money looking for resources when you\u2019re away from home or campus.
