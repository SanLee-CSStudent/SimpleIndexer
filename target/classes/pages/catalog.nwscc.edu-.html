Archived Catalog 2020-2021 Catalog Dr. Jeff Goodwin NW-SCC President It is my pleasure to welcome you to Northwest-Shoals Community College (NW-SCC). As Alabama\u2019s first public junior college, NW-SCC prides itself as an educational home for the people of northwest Alabama for more than 57 years. NW-SCC has two campuses, the Shoals campus in Muscle Shoals and the Phil Campbell campus located in Franklin County. In this catalog, you will find information on the College's admissions policies and procedures, financial aid, academic programs, student support services, tuition and fees, workforce training programs and much more. Please contact us at any time you may have questions or need additional information. Thank you for your interest in Northwest-Shoals Community College.
