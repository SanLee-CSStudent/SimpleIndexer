We offer FREE adult literacy, basic academic skills, GED preparation, Employability Skills through AIDT Ready to Work and MSSC CPT and CLA, Career Pathway Programs, ASVAB and ACT tutoring and English as a Second Language instruction for learners within our North Central Alabama service area (Madison, Morgan, Limestone, and Eastern Lawrence Counties). Each learner works at his or her own pace and all instructional materials are available at no cost to the learner. Any adult who is at least 18 years of age and not presently enrolled in school may participate in the Adult Education programs. Students ages 16 \u2013 17 may enter Adult Education Services with proper approval and paperwork. A picture ID and Social Security or Tax ID number is required for orientation and two forms of ID are required for GED testing services.
