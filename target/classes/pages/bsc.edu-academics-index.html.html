The Associated Colleges of the South, a consortium of 16 nationally recognized colleges and universities committed to providing the best liberal arts education possible at the most affordable price available. ACS institutions work together to improve student experiences, enhance curriculum, and provide learning programs for faculty and staff. The Birmingham Consortium for Higher Education (BACHE), a partnership among the five four-year colleges and universities in the greater Birmingham area. BACHE allows BSC students to take courses at partner institutions, brings visiting lecturers to the campus, and provides support services for faculty, staff, and the campus community. The Annapolis Group, an association of 130 of the nation\u2019s leading liberal arts colleges that work together to share the values of liberal arts education, share best practices, and seek higher levels of excellence. Phi Beta Kappa BSC is one of fewer than 290 colleges in the United States (and one of only three in Alabama) to shelter a chapter of Phi Beta Kappa. This honor society considers members of the top 10 percent of the graduating class for election based on good character and a record of academic performance in a broad range of courses. Each year, the chapter brings distinguished scholars to campus to speak.
