The designation recognizes the scope, quality and impact of Auburn\u2019s outreach mission through its institutional engagement in the community. Auburn has held its engagement classification continuously since 2010 and received renewal of the designation following a year-long application process led by the Division of University Outreach\u2019s Office of Public Service.
