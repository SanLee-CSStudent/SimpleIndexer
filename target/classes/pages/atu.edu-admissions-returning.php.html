If you\u2019re ready to get started, we\u2019ll support you every step of the way, making it easy for you to get started. Below are a few steps to help you begin. Step 1: Submit the Returning Student Application The first step in becoming an ATU student is applying to attend. Our application is available 24 hours a day for your convenience. Helpful info: Students who want to study at the ATU Ozark campus in Ozark, Arkansas, select the \"Ozark Campus\" in the application Students who want to study at the ATU Russellville campus in Russellville, Arkansas, select the \"Main Campus\" in the application Students who want to study in one of our 100% online programs select \"Online\" as their campus in the application Apply today Have questions about applying? The Admissions office is here to help. You can contact them at (479) 968-0343 or tech.enroll@atu.edu. Step 2: Apply for Financial Aid The federal government authorizes billions of dollars every year to help students earn their college degree. This first step in applying for federal aid is filling out the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA). Helpful info: ATU's school code for the FAFSA is 001089 Visit ATU's TechSense site for info about all the ways to fund a college degree Payment plans are available through our Student Accounts office for out-of-pocket expenses Apply for Federal Aid Have questions about the financial aid process? The Financial Aid office is here to help. You can contact them at (479) 968-0399 or fa.help@atu.edu. Step 3: Activate Your OneTech Portal Once you\u2019ve applied, you\u2019ll receive an email decision about five days after submitting your application. In that email, you\u2019ll get instructions to activate your OneTech Portal. Helpful info: OneTech is how you get information about aid, communicate with your professors, and view your classes. Your admission decision email will have all of the instruction you need to activate your portal login. Already got your admission decision email? Visit the site below to activate your OneTech account. Activate OneTech Have questions about activating your OneTech account? The Office of Information Systems is here to help. You can contact them at (479) 968-0646 or campussupport@atu.edu. Step 4: Register for Classes Once you\u2019ve applied and been admitted, you\u2019ll be able to find your advisor. Our advisors will work with you to schedule classes, answer questions you may have, and support you every step of the way. Helpful info: You'll be able to find your assigned advisor via OneTech. Feel free to reach out to your advisor and get to know them. They are ready to work with you to transform your world. Step 5: Stay the Course Once you\u2019ve started your course work, there are lots of resources to help you achieve your goals. Don't wait until you need help - get familiar with the offices at ATU that are ready to help you succeed. You can start using these free resources on your first day of classes.
