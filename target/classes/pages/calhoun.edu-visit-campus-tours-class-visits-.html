Staff Guided Tours Would you like to learn more about Calhoun\u2019s campuses, programs of study, or the process for becoming a Calhoun student? Then perhaps a Staff Guided tour is perfect for you. These small group tours are given by our Outreach Advising staff either virtually or onsite at the Decatur Campus, Huntsville Site, or the Alabama Center for the Arts in downtown Decatur. An appointment is required for all tours and the tour will last approximately an hour. The tour groups are limited to 6 people to allow for social distancing. We ask that all participants wear masks and observe social distancing while on campus. Click on the links below to schedule your campus tour TODAY! Decatur Campus Tours Huntsville Campus Tours Alabama Center for the Arts Tours Group Tours Would you like to bring a group of 5 or more to tour Calhoun? Please complete the group tour request form below and choose which campus you would like to tour. Requests must be made at least two weeks in advance. We offer group tours at our Huntsville Campus, Decatur Campus, or the Alabama Center for the Arts. Tours typically last 2 hours but will not go over 3 hours in length. Tours are not offered on Fridays, Saturdays, or Sundays. Due to COVID, tour groups are limited to 40 people to allow of social distancing. We ask that all participants wear masks and observe social distancing while on campus. Click on the link below to schedule your group tour TODAY!
