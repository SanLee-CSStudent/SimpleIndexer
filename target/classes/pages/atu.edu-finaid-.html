WITHDRAWAL INFO summer info This applies each summer unless otherwise noted. Summer 2022 uses the 2021-2022 FAFSA application. Be sure to fill it out now if you haven't! You must be enrolled in at least 6 hours to receive student loans in the summer. If you used all of your scheduled aid in Fall\/Spring, you may still be eligible for summer aid. Contact Financial Aid to inquire about your summer eligibility. **Summer aid will only pay for the classes you are enrolled in as of 5:00 pm on the last day to drop classes with 100% reduction of tuition and fees. You will not be eligible for more aid if you add classes after this date.** This date is: JUNE 1, 2022 for all sessions of summer 2022 Note: You should always check with the Financial Aid office before you drop a class as this could affect your aid eligibility.
