Graduation Process Commencement Ceremony Beyond AWC Graduation Process In the final semester of enrollment in your academic program, you should apply for graduation. Apply for Graduation Complete the Graduation Application through your Self-Service account NO LATER than 5 pm on the corresponding deadline. Fall Graduation Deadline: First Monday in November Spring Graduation Deadline: First Monday in March Summer Graduation Deadline: First Monday in July To be eligible for graduation, you must: Meet the basic requirements for associate degree or certificate as listed in the General Program Requirements section of the College Catalog. Note: a minimum of \u201CC\u201D grade and a minimum of 2.00 GPA is required for all courses in a certificate or degree program. Meet with your academic advisor to review your Academic Program Evaluation Provide official transfer credit documentation including Advanced Placement credit, military transcripts, CLEP scores, etc. (if applicable) Graduation Notification You will receive notice of your eligibility status for graduation via email to your ToroMail account. Be sure to follow any direction given to you in order to complete the graduation process. Diplomas Diplomas will be available for distribution four to six weeks after they have been posted to your academic record. You will be notified by email when your diploma is ready to mail out. To ensure your address is correct, check your Student Planning account and make necessary changes as soon as possible. A Student Information Update Form must be submitted for any formal name changes. *You must clear any indebtedness to the College with the AWC Cashier before the end of the semester you intend to graduate, or your diploma or official transcript will not be released. Academic Distinction Students who have achieved a cumulative GPA of 3.4 or higher will receive Academic Distinction upon completion of a degree. (Degrees only) Highest Academic Distinction 3.900-4.000 High Academic Distinction 3.600-3.899 Academic Distinction 3.400-3.599 Additional Degrees\/Majors Students may earn recognition of an additional major if all degree\/major requirements have been completed and the normal application process is followed. Graduates are expected to arrive by 6:00 p.m. and meet at Gila Ridge's Hawk Gym for graduate line-up.
