Why AWC? Arizona Western College is flexible scheduling transformative affordable tuition life-long learning powerful partnerships economic development your path to the university workforce development Transforming lives through education and partnerships to create thriving communities 100+ Degrees and Certifications 12 Locations 10,000 Square Miles 11,000+ Students Annually 300+ Students Living on Campus 7 Competitive Teams 60+ Student Clubs and Organizations Academic, Career Technical and Workforce Education We offer over 100 degrees and certificates in a wide range of academic and career technical programs, as well as non-credit courses in professional development, customized training, and personal enrichment areas. Locations We have 12 locations across our 10,000 square mile, 2-county service area, and we host all three state universities on our main campus for face-to-face classes. Transfer Degrees You can easily transfer to a university of your choice. We\u2019re #1 in AZ for the % of students who transfer to AZ public universities and earn a bachelor\u2019s degree within 4 years. Students More than 300 students reside on our main campus, and we field 7 competitive athletic teams. Our students participate in over 50 campus clubs, and we are home to a challenging Honors Program and curriculum, and a world-class International Program, representing 30 countries from around the globe. Our Mission Transforming lives through education and partnerships to create thriving communities Accreditation Arizona Western College is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and its next reaffirmation will be in the 2028-2029 school year.
