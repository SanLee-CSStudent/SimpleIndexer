Apply Get Info MENU A Future Student A Current Student A Parent or Guardian A High School Counselor A Manager or Employer Looking for Workforce Training\/Certifications In Need of a GED or Adult Education Classes A Calhoun Alum or Former Student Home Student Services Advising Placement Test Study Guide Placement Test Study Guide Tips & Study Guides Refreshing your skills and studying for the Placement Exam is important. Though the test is not a pass or fail exam, your score determines which courses you will take your first few semesters at Calhoun. Before you take your test: Study basic Math, Reading, and English concepts. For extra help, visit these sites: Official Accuplacer Study App WritePlacer Quantitative Reasoning, Algebra, and Statistics Guide Reading Comprehension Guide Get some rest the day before you test. Day of your test: Dress in layers. The room can be cold for some testers. You don\u2019t want that to distract you. Arrive 10 minutes early. Don\u2019t rush through. If you don\u2019t finish your exam or if you get tired, you are able to stop your test and come back within two weeks to finish. Bring a photo ID. Know your Calhoun C#. After your test: Schedule an appointment to meet with an advisor. You can go to www.calhoun.edu\/advising to schedule an appointment.
