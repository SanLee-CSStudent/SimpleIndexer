We provide a variety of support services including tutors and resources that can help you grow academically into a capable and confident learner. Tutoring is available in-person, virtually (video tutoring via Zoom), and by email. You can call or browse our tutor schedules to find out when a tutor is available for your particular subject. Appointments are required for individual in-person and virtual sessions. Please contact us for more information. Become a Tutor New Tutoring Schedule Math Center The Math Center offers assistance with all mathematics courses as well as business, technology, and science. Free tutoring is provided in-person or virtually (video tutoring via Zoom) during all hours of operation. The SSC also rents TI-84 graphing calculators for use in select math courses. Graphing Calculator Rental Writing Center The Writing Center provides free tutoring services for students at all levels and stages of writing in any academic subject. Our goal is to help you become a better, more confident writer. Writing tutors are available in-person, virtually, and via email. Tutors for ESL, ASL, and Spanish courses are also available in the Writing Center. Please request an appointment. Learn More At the Writing Center, we can help you: Clarify assignment instructions Develop and organize ideas Read for comprehension Apply formatting and citation Proofread your own essays Request an Appointment The goal of the Student Success Center is to serve students in a way that promotes their successful completion of courses at Arizona Western College and beyond.
