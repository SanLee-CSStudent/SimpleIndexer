Download: Discover AWC PowerPoint Discover AWC Student Handbook Sign Up for Discover AWC! Pre-Evaluation Post Evaluation What is Discover AWC? Discover AWC is our weekly event for all NEW students planning to register for Fall or Spring semesters and want comprehensive assistance in getting started on their first step into college. Sessions are available at the Yuma Campus, San Luis Campus, and Somerton Campus. Orientation to Advisement and Registration is designed for all incoming students to provide them with the necessary information, resources and advising assistance to help them have a successful start and experience at AWC. Participants will learn about AWC degrees and certificates, the importance of Guided Course Placement, tips on how to choose classes, ideas on how to build a semester schedule, how to register for classes using Student Planning, an introduction to other digital services like Canvas & TORO Webmail, and other important topics necessary for a successful transition into college. Who should attend Discover AWC? Discover AWC is designed for all new part-time or full-time students attending any AWC site. How much does it cost to attend? There is no cost to attend Discover AWC. What do I need to do to prepare for Discover AWC? Students should: (1) complete the Guided Course Placement; (2) set-up their AWC NetID* (for access to Self-Service\/Canvas\/TORO Webmail); (3) plan to make arrangements for tuition payment and\/or apply for financial aid. *For assistance to set up your AWC NetID, contact the Service Desk at (928) 317-5892 or email the Service Desk at servicedesk@azwestern.edu. How do I sign up for Discover AWC? Sign up for Discover AWC here! What if I have more questions? If you have additional questions, please contact us at (928) 344-7624 or email us at: academic.orientation@azwestern.edu How can I help improve these Discover AWC sessions? Please fill out the evaluation form after attending a Discover AWC session. This will help us to continuously improve our sessions. Discover AWC Experience AWC Discover AWC Experience AWC What is Experience AWC? Experience AWC is an orientation experience designed to provide new students with information, resources and advising that will facilitate a successful AWC experience. Participants will learn about AWC degrees and certificates, how to choose classes, how to build a schedule and register for classes, how to access and use student platforms like Blackboard TORO Webmail, and other important topics necessary for a successful transition into college. Students and guests will participate in a campus tour and attend faculty-presented program information sessions. Participants can attend a Lady Matador softball game and enjoy lunch provided by the Matador Athletic Department. Scholarships totaling $2,000 will be awarded during game innings Who should attend Experience AWC? Experience AWC is designed for all new part-time or full-time students attending any AWC site. How much does it cost to attend? There is no cost to attend Experience AWC. What do I need to do to prepare for Experience AWC? Students must (1) complete assessment\/placement testing at AWC Testing Services or have ACT\/SAT test results sent to AWC Testing Services PRIOR to the event (2) activate AWC accounts for access to Self-Service\/Blackboard\/TORO Webmail; (3) plan for payment of tuition during event. Also, please wear comfortable and weather-appropriate clothing and shoes. Campus tours will involve walking and some of the event will occur outdoors. How do I register for Experience AWC?
