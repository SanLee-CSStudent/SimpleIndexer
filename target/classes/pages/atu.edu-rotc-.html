SCHOLARSHIPS Army ROTC offers four, three, and two year scholarships, awarded strictly on merit to the most outstanding students who apply. The scholarship covers full tuition and fees. Additionally they receive a stipend of $300 a month as a freshman, $350 a month as a sophomore, $450 a month as a junior, and $500 a month as a senior; plus $1200 a year for books. FULL-TUITION SCHOLARSHIPS Army ROTC grants more than $270 Million in merit-based scholarships to 13,000+ students each year. Tuition or Room\/Board Scholarships may be used to pay for tuition or room\/board.
